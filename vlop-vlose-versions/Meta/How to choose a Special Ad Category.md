How to choose a Special Ad Category
===================================

![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/mci_ab/public/cms/?ab_b=e&ab_page=CMS&ab_entry=438223955063449&version=1666716831&transcode_extension=webp)

As part of our ongoing efforts to promote fairness on our advertising platform, we have introduced a new Special Ad Category "Financial products and services" for advertisers promoting financial products and services. Starting January 21, 2025, using this category is required for financial products and services campaigns for advertisers based in the United States or showing ads to audiences in the United States. Ads may be rejected if an appropriate category is not chosen. [Learn more about the update here](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/510724041294968).

Choosing the correct category for your campaign is an important part of following [Meta Advertising Standards](https://www.facebook.com/policies/ads). When you create a campaign, you are required to specify if you are running an ad to promote a Special Ad Category.

A Special Ad Category applies to ads that have specific requirements such as:

*   Authorization and “Paid for by” disclaimers for ads about social issues, elections or politics
    
*   Limited audience selection tools for ads about employment, housing opportunities or financial products and services to help protect people from unlawful discrimination across our platforms
    

**Note:** Using a Special Ad Category may limit your audience options during the ad creation process.

Create a Special Ad Category campaign
-------------------------------------

To create a Special Ad Category campaign:

1.  Go to [**Meta Ads Manager**](https://business.facebook.com/adsmanager) and create or open an existing campaign.
2.  Select **Special Ad Categories**.
3.  Below **Categories**, choose which category applies to your campaign.
4.  Below **Countries**, choose the country or countries where you want to run your campaign.

Audience selection tools for Special Ad Categories
--------------------------------------------------

**Ads about housing, employment and financial products and services opportunities**

Certain audience options are limited or unavailable for these ads for advertisers based in or reaching the US and advertisers reaching Canada and certain countries in Europe: age, gender, ZIP code or postal code, exclusion targeting, lookalike audiences and saved audiences. Some interests will also be unavailable when you create your audience. Audiences based on city or pin drop locations will include an expanded radius. [Meta Advantage+ catalog ads](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/397103717129942?id=1913105122334058) (previously known as dynamic ads) will be subject to the same limitations.

We encourage you to broaden—not restrict—your audience. Our audience options, like all audience selection tools on Facebook, must be used in ways that are inclusive and not discriminatory.

**Ads about social issues, elections or politics**

![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/mci_ab/public/cms/?ab_b=e&ab_page=CMS&ab_entry=438223955063449&version=1666716831&transcode_extension=webp)

In response to the European Union's Transparency & Targeting of Political Ads (TTPA) regulation, advertisers are not able to create or edit ads about social issues, elections, or politics in the European Union.

You can read more about our decision to prohibit ads about social issues, elections or politics in the EU in [Newsroom](https://about.fb.com/news/2025/07/ending-political-electoral-and-social-issue-advertising-in-the-eu/).

To enable healthy discourse and increase transparency and accountability, advertisers who want to run [ads about social issues, elections or politics](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/167836590566506) in [select countries](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/2150157295276323) may be required to get authorized in the country they want to run ads in. To do this, they must [confirm their identity](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/2992964394067299), provide more information about their organization to place [“Paid for by” disclaimers](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/488070228549681) on ads and have their ads entered into the public [**Ad Library**](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/2405092116183307) for seven years.

When creating campaigns that include ads about social issues, elections or politics, at the beginning of the ad buying process, make sure to select **I'm creating a campaign for ads in a Special Ad Category.** If you’re not authorized, you’ll be prompted to complete the process. If you do not select the Special Ad Category, and we determine your campaign includes ad(s) about social issues, elections or politics, the campaign will be disapproved.

When an advertiser tries to run an ad with a “Paid for by” disclaimer that targets people in a selected country, we use a variety of signals to determine whether the ads are coming from an authorized user in the same country they're trying to run issue, electoral or political ads in by checking for information about people, ad accounts and Pages. If there is a discrepancy in this information or our signals indicate that the person editing or creating the ad is located outside the targeted country, the ad will be disapproved.
