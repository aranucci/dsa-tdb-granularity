About Political Content Control on Facebook
===========================================

We’ve spent the last few years refining our approach to political content on Facebook, making it easier for people to adjust the amount of political content they see on Facebook.

Political content includes content that mentions:

*   Governments
*   Elections
*   Social topics

You can use the political content control to adjust whether Facebook recommends or does not recommend political content in your Feed.

You can further personalize your Facebook experience using the customization tools available in your Content Preferences through Settings, or in just a few clicks directly within your Feed.

Manage political content setting in Content Preferences through Settings
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Open the Facebook app.
2.  Tap ![top right menu](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=fb-toprighthamburger-andriod&density=1) in the top right of Facebook.
3.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=fb-settingsandprivacy-desktop&density=1) **Settings & privacy**, then tap **Settings**.
4.  Under **Preferences**, tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=fb-feed-desktop&density=1)**Content Preferences**.
5.  Tap the dropdown next to **Political content**.
6.  Select your preferred political content control setting.
    
    *   The **Default** setting suggests political content from profiles you don’t follow.
    *   If you toggle the control to “off”, we won’t suggest political content when we recognize it.
    

Manage political content setting in Feed directly from your posts
-----------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Open the Facebook app.
2.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=fb-threedotspostsettings-desktop&density=1) in the top right of the post in Feed.
3.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=fb-feed-desktop&density=1) **Manage your Feed**.
4.  Tap the dropdown next to **Political content**.
5.  Select your preferred political content control setting.
    
    *   The **Default** setting suggests political content from profiles you don’t follow.
    *   If you toggle the control to “off”, we won’t suggest political content when we recognize it.
    

You can also learn how to see [fewer ads about social issues, elections or politics](https://www.facebook.com/help/595432167810439?helpref=faq_content) while on Facebook. Learn more about [how Meta addresses political content](https://transparency.fb.com/features/approach-to-political-content).
