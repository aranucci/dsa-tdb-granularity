### **What are Gifts on TikTok?**

  
Gifts are virtual items you can use to engage with content on TikTok. You can send Gifts while browsing your feeds or during a LIVE on TikTok.

Note: You must be at least 18 years old (or 19 in South Korea) to access Coins and Gifts.

### **How Gifts work on TikTok**

  
To access Gifts, you'll need to [purchase Coins](https://support.tiktok.com/en/live-gifts-wallet/gifts/gifts#3). After you activate a Gift, the number of Coins needed will be deducted from your balance. The number of Coins needed to activate each Gift is displayed below the Gift.

Learn more about how to [send a Gift during a LIVE on TikTok](https://support.tiktok.com/en/live-gifts-wallet/gifts/send-a-gift-during-a-live-on-tiktok).

Note: The use of Gifts is subject to our [Virtual Items Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/virtual-items).

### **How to purchase Coins on TikTok**

  
You can purchase Coins in the TikTok app through third-party app stores such as the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. In supported countries, you can also purchase them directly from the [TikTok website](https://www.tiktok.com/coin). Depending on your location, Coins may also be available through gift cards, which can be redeemed on the TikTok website.

  
Note: You must be at least 18 years old (or 19 in South Korea) to purchase Coins. The purchase and use of Coins is subject to our [Virtual Items Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/virtual-items).

  
**Recharge Coins from Balance**  
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.  
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **Balance**.  
3\. Tap **Get Coins**, then choose the Coins package that you'd like to purchase.  
4\. Tap **Recharge**, then follow the steps to complete the purchase through the applicable app store.

  
**Recharge Coins from a LIVE**  
1\. In the TikTok app, tap the **Gift** button at the bottom of a LIVE. If you don't see Gifts, then gifting isn't available for this LIVE.  
2\. Tap **Recharge** at the bottom and choose the Coins package that you'd like to purchase.  
3\. Follow the steps to complete the purchase through the applicable app store.

  
**Recharge Coins from the TikTok website**  
1\. Go to [www.tiktok.com/coin](https://www.tiktok.com/coin).  
2\. Choose the Coins package that you'd like to purchase.  
3\. Click **Recharge**.  
4\. Follow the steps to complete the purchase through your preferred payment method.

  
**Recharge Coins from a gift card**  
1\. Go to [www.tiktok.com/coin/redeem](http://tiktok.com/coin/redeem).  
2\. Enter the PIN code from the gift card to redeem Coins.  
  
Keep in mind that recharging Coins from a gift card isn't available everywhere.

You can send a Gift during a TikTok LIVE to react and show your appreciation for the creator's content in real-time.  
  
To send a Gift, you'll need to purchase Coins. When you send a Gift, the value in Coins of this Gift will be deducted from your account balance.  
  
Note:  
•  You must be 18 years old or older (or 19 in South Korea) to purchase and use Coins which will allow you to send Gifts.  
•  Gifts are available only in select countries.  
•  Gifts can't be sent to LIVE videos from Government, Politician, and Political Party Accounts, and other public interest accounts. These accounts also can't send Gifts to LIVE videos.  
•  Sending Gifts is subject to our [virtual items policies](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/virtual-items).

  
1\. During a LIVE, tap the **Gift** button at the bottom.  
2\. Choose the Gift you'd like to send.  
3\. If you need to recharge your Coins, tap **Recharge** and follow the instructions provided to complete payment.  
4\. Below the Gift, tap **Send**.

You can send a Gift to a video on TikTok to react and show your appreciation for the creator's content.  
  
To send a Gift, you'll need to purchase Coins. When you send a Gift, the value in Coins of this Gift will be deducted from your account balance.  
  
Note:  
•  You must be 18 years old or older (or 19 in South Korea) to purchase and use Coins which will allow you to send Gifts.  
•  Gifts are available only in select countries.  
•  Sending Gifts is subject to our [virtual items policies](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/virtual-items).  
•  You can send Gifts to videos by Personal Accounts only. Business Accounts, Government, Politician, and Political Party Accounts, and any other public interest account are ineligible to send Gifts or receive Gifts on their content.

  
1\. In the TikTok app, tap the **Comments** button on a video.  
2\. Tap the **Gift** button next to **Add comment**. If you don't see Gifts, then the video is ineligible to receive Gifts at this time.  
3\. Choose the Gift you'd like to send.  
4\. If you need to purchase more Coins, tap **Recharge** and follow the instructions provided to complete payment.  
5\. Write a comment and tap **Send**.

### **How to set your Gift limit on TikTok**

  
To set your Gift limit:  
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **LIVE** at the top.  
2\. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom.  
3\. Tap **Settings**.  
4\. Tap **Gift limit notification**, then turn the **Gift limit notification** setting on and follow the steps to set a daily gift limit.  
5\. Tap **Set**. We'll notify you when you reach the limit.  
6\. When you receive the Gift limit notification, tap **Manage limit** to adjust your limit, or you can Also choose to switch off the notification for 7 days.
