Vetted researcher data accessUnder the [Digital Services Act](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/library/delegated-act-data-access-under-digital-services-act-dsa), researchers who have been granted vetted researcher status by a Digital Services Coordinator (DSC) may be eligible to access data to support research into the identified systemic risks in the EU and the measures taken to mitigate them.  
  
Access to this data is only available through a reasoned request submitted by [Coimisiún na Meán](https://www.cnam.ie/industry-and-professionals/online-safety-framework/certifications-schemes/vetted-researchers/), the DSC of establishment. TikTok does not accept direct applications from researchers.

##### How can I submit a vetted researcher data access application?

To request access, vetted researchers must apply through the European Commission Data Access Application Portal.

[Visit application portal](https://data-access.dsa.ec.europa.eu/home)

Already got an approved application?
------------------------------------

If your application has been approved by Coimisiún na Meán, you can now [create an account](https://developers.tiktok.com/signup) on TikTok for Developers and [continue to access our Research Tools](https://developers.tiktok.com/application/vetted-researcher).
