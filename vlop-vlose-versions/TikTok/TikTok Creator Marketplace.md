### **What is TikTok Creator Marketplace?**

  
TikTok Creator Marketplace (TTCM) is the official platform for brand and creator collaborations on TikTok.  
  
You can improve your content by tracking performance and finding creators to collaborate with on TikTok Creator Marketplace.

### **Who is eligible to join the Creator Marketplace?**

  
You can check your eligibility by visiting the Creator Marketplace in the TikTok app. Creators must meet the following criteria to join the Creator Marketplace on TikTok:  
•  Have a TikTok account in good standing, including no history of repeatedly or irresponsibly violating our [Community Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/) and [Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/terms-of-service-us?lang=en) policies and engaging in malicious or fraudulent activities.  
•  Be 18 years of age or older (or 19 in South Korea).  
•  Have at least 10,000 followers (or 50,000 followers in South Korea).  
•  Have at least 1,000 post views within 30 days.  
•  Have at least 1,000 post views within 30 days.  
•  Publish 3 posts within 30 days.

  

To access TikTok Creator Marketplace:  
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.  
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.  
3\. Within **Monetization**, select **Creator Marketplace**.  
4\. Tap **Check eligibility** to confirm if you can join the platform.  
5\. If you're eligible, tap **Join** and follow the steps to enter the Creator Marketplace.

  
Learn more about [TikTok Creator Marketplace](https://creatormarketplace.tiktok.com/help#/doc/9482/12154).
