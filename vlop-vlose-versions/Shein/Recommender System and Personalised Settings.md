Recommender System and Personalised Settings

"What is Recommender System and Personalised Settings?"

Recommender System

A recommender system is a technology used to suggest products or services to individuals based on product contents, preferences, behaviours, or previous interactions on a platform. It utilises algorithms to analyse user information, such as search and purchase history, to create bespoke experiences tailored to everyone. SHEIN uses recommender systems to help you discover new items that you might be interested in, saving you time searching for them and making your shopping experience more enjoyable and efficient.

Personalised Settings

At SHEIN, we believe that our users know best about their needs. We're thrilled to offer you a tailored shopping and interaction experience! You have the option to view and receive personalised or non-personalised content and information in each type of recommender system by simply adjusting the toggle below. You can change it at any time you prefer.

Here is how to do it:

Select "**ON**" to enable personalisation

Select "**OFF**" to disable personalisation

Useful Tips

You do not need to log in to your SHEIN account to choose this personalised setting. We do not link your choices made before and after you log in. Please review your settings for the best experience.

This personalised setting will not affect your marketing subscriptions or communication preferences. The idea is that you can decide whether you want to see personalised content or recommendations.

Personalised product recommendations

When you turn on this feature, you will receive personalised product recommendations (e.g., different types of products that may interest you, or suggested clothing sizes for the products you view based on your profile settings) in various sections of our website and app, including the homepage, main content, product descriptions, and shopping carts. You will not see personalised content when you turn off this feature.
