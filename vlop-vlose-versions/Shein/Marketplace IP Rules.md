SHEIN Global Marketplace Policy: Anti-Counterfeiting & Intellectual Property Infringement Policy

05/08/2024 | SHEIN Seller Adminis

  

   At SHEIN, we take customer trust extremely seriously and always strive to provide the best customer experience. **Therefore, products offered for sale on all of the SHEIN Marketplaces must be authentic. The sale of counterfeit products on any of the SHEIN Marketplaces is strictly prohibited. Failure to abide by this policy may result in actions against your account, such as suspension or termination of your SHEIN Marketplace account, cancellation of listings, suspension or forfeiture of payments, removal of selling privileges, and disposal of inventory if they are in our possession.**

  

   It is each seller’s responsibility to source, sell, and fulfill only authentic products. Prohibited products include bootlegs, fakes, or pirated copies of products; products that have been illegally replicated, reproduced, or manufactured; and products that infringe another party’s intellectual property rights, including copyrights, trademarks and design rights. In addition, we may pause remittance to you if we suspect that a SHEIN Marketplace account has been used to sell inauthentic goods, commit fraud, or engage in other illegal activity, until we can investigate and verify.

  

   We work with a variety of stakeholders and rightsholders to detect and prevent inauthentic or otherwise infringing products from reaching our customers. As a result, we reserve the right to remove suspect listings based on our own review of listings in our discretion. We also work with rightsholders and law enforcement worldwide to take and support legal action against sellers and suppliers that knowingly violate this policy and harm our customers. In addition to criminal fines and imprisonment, sellers and suppliers of inauthentic products may face civil penalties including the loss of any amounts received from the sale of inauthentic products, the damage or harm sustained by the rightsholders, statutory and other damages, and attorney’s fees.

  

   SHEIN strives to ensure a trustworthy shopping experience for our customers. By selling on any of the SHEIN Marketplaces, you acknowledge and agree that:

   · The sale of counterfeit products is strictly prohibited;

   · You may not sell any products that are not legal, such as products that have been illegally replicated, reproduced, or manufactured, or that bear the trademarks, service marks, names or logos of third parties to which you have not  been licensed;

   · You must provide records about the authenticity or originality of your products if SHEIN requests that documentation.
