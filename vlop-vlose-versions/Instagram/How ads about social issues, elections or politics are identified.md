How ads about social issues, elections or politics are identified on Instagram
==============================================================================

If you’re in the [European Region](https://www.facebook.com/help/681634310058367?helpref=faq_content) or the United Kingdom, your ad choices are different. Learn more about [your ad choices](https://www.facebook.com/help/1241090626576242?helpref=faq_content).
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To increase transparency around the ads we show you about social issues, elections or politics, we have measures to help ensure the authenticity of these types of ads on Instagram. A social issue, electoral or political ad in feed or stories can be identified on Instagram by the **Paid for by** disclaimer.

We consider ads to be about social issues, elections or politics if:

*   They’re made by (or on behalf of), or about a candidate running for public office, a political figure, a political party or advocates for the outcome of an election to public office.
*   They’re about any election, referendum, or ballot initiative, including "go out and vote" or election campaigns.
*   They’re about social issues in the location where the ad is placed. Learn more about [what we consider to be ads about social issues.](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/214754279118974?id=288762101909005&helpref=faq_content)
*   They’re regulated as political advertising.

Learn more about what's considered an ad about social issues, elections or politics in our [Business Help Center](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/167836590566506?id=288762101909005&helpref=faq_content).

Please note, if you're an advertiser looking to run ads about social issues, elections or politics, [you'll need to authorize your account and let us know the topic of the ads.](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/208949576550051?id=288762101909005https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fbusiness%2Fhelp%2F208949576550051%3Fid%3D288762101909005&helpref=faq_content)

Reporting an ad about social issues, elections or politics
----------------------------------------------------------

If you see an ad about social issues, elections or politics on Instagram that doesn't have a disclaimer showing information about the organization or individual responsible for the ad, you can report it.

Report an ad
------------

1.  Tap ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?set=content_operations_help_center&name=instagram-newreportfromphoto-android&density=1) **Options** in the top right of the ad.
2.  Tap **Report ad**, then tap **Unauthorized political ad**.

Ad Library
----------

Ads about social issues, elections or politics that have appeared on Instagram on or after May 22, 2018, will also appear in the Ad Library.

Learn more about the [Ad Library](https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/?active_status=all&ad_type=all&country=US&impression_search_field=has_impressions_lifetime).
