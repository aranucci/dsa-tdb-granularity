Information on data access for researchersunder the EU Digital Services Act



Updated on 29 October 2025



Vetted researchers

According to Article 40 (8) of the EU Digital Services Act (DSA), researchers may apply for dataaccess to either the Cyprus Radio Television Authority (CRTA) or the Digital Services Coordinator

of the Member State of the research organisation to which they are affiliated. The applicationmust meet all requirements of Article 40 (8) DSA. Subject to their confirmation as vettedresearchers for the specific research covered by the application, the Cyprus Radio Televisionwill issue a reasoned request to Aylo Freesites Ltd, who will, within a reasonable period as

specified in the request, provide access to data to vetted researchers who meet the

requirements. Please refer to Article 40 DSA for all detailed requirements of the application

process.



Please note that pursuant to Article 40 (4) DSA, data access to vetted researchers is onlyprovided for the sole purpose of conducting research that contributes to the detection,

identification, and understanding of systemic risks in the Union and to the assessment of theadequacy, efficiency, and impacts of the risk mitigation measures, in accordance with Articles34 and 35 DSA.



Data access portal

The European Commission created a DSA data access portal, which provides information on,supports, and streamlines the data access process for researchers and facilitates related

information exchange. The data access portal is available at:



https://data-access.dsa.ec.europa.eu/

For any inquiries, requests, or complaints to Aylo Freesites Ltd. related to data access for vettedresearchers in accordance with Article 40 DSA, please use dsa@pornhub.com.

Restrictions:



As general guidance, researchers should only request data pertaining to what is currentlypublished on the platform, or that which is or was published within the last 6 months. Only onetimeframe per request is permitted.



If data does not exist, for example in the case of data removed due to GDPR Manage My Datarequests, it cannot be provided.



Access modalities

The appropriate access modalities depend on the amount and type of data requested and will

usually be in the form a JSON feed or a .csv file. However, please note that we may need to

propose different access modalities to comply with privacy and security obligations and toprotect confidential business information.



Non-vetted researchers

According to Article 40 (12) DSA, researchers who have not been vetted through the processdescribed above, may apply directly to Aylo Freesites Ltd for access to data that are publicly

accessible on the online interface of Pornhub.com. The application must set out how the

researcher meets all of the conditions set out in Article 40 (8), points (b), (c), (d) and (e) DSA,and must warrant that the researcher will use the provided data solely for performing research

that contributes to the detection, identification and understanding of systemic risks in the

Union pursuant to Article 34 (1) DSA. Upon receipt of a fully reasoned and well-founded dataaccess request, Aylo Freesites Ltd will give access without undue delay to the requested data,including, where technically possible, to real-time data, provided that the data is publiclyaccessible in their online interface.
