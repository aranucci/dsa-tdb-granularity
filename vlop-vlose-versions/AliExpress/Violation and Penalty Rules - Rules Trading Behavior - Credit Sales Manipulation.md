AliExpress "Credit and Sales Manipulation" Rules Supplemental Explanation

Dear Sellers,

In order to help sellers better understand the "Credit and Sales Manipulation" rules and regulate their own business practices accordingly, the platform has provided supplemental explanations on the scope of application, definitions, manifestations of credit and sales manipulation, violation categories, and penalties. The specific rules are as follows:

1、Scope of Application
----------------------

To protect the legitimate rights and interests of consumers, operators, or AliExpress, if the platform discovers any violation by a merchant, the following violation handling measures will be taken during the violation processing period in accordance with the circumstances specified in these rules, until AliExpress confirms that the risks are basically under control and partial or complete lifting of control measures.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Credit and sales manipulation refers to the behavior of merchants obtaining false benefits such as fake product sales, store ratings, credit scores, product reviews, or transaction amounts through improper methods such as fabricating or concealing transaction facts, and evading or maliciously exploiting credit record rules.

Unfair forms of credit and sales manipulation include but are not limited to:

（1）Using fake shipping tracking numbers or reusing a single tracking number multiple times, or providing logistics information that cannot be queried or does not exist.

（2）Lowering product prices directly or indirectly (such as significantly modifying product prices or transaction prices, etc.) to attract buyers to place orders but not shipping or falsely shipping.

（3）Sellers registering their own accounts or manipulating other accounts (including but not limited to manipulation of team accounts, accounts of relatives and friends, accounts of colleagues in the company, etc.) to purchase their own listed products; or occurrences of repetitive bidding, account, and IP issues by buyers.

（4）Sellers engaging in false transactions using tools, services, or favorable conditions provided by third parties (including other sellers).

（5）Other abnormal trading methods.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

（1）For sellers who are first identified by the platform as engaging in credit and sales manipulation, the platform will delete the credit scores and sales records of their violating products, refund orders related to credit and sales manipulation, and impose penalties ranging from warnings to account freezes for 7 to 180 days, depending on the severity of the violation. In severe cases, direct account termination may be imposed.

（2）Goods suspected of false transactions will have their platform search ranking lowered for 30 days. In severe cases, the platform will also delist all products in the seller's store.

（3）For sellers who continue to engage in credit and sales manipulation after receiving warnings or account freezes, the platform reserves the right to directly terminate their accounts.

（4）Credit and sales manipulation penalty execution overview:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| Minor Offense | 6 points/times |
| Moderate Offense | 12 points/times |
| Severe Offense | 24 points/times |
| Particularly Severe Violation | 48 points/times |

Offense Penalty Standard Dimension: Based on the number of violating orders within 30 days, the proportion of violating orders to total orders, and the credit and sales manipulation situation on the platform.
