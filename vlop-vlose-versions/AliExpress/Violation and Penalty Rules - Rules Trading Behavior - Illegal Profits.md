AliExpress "Illegal Profits" Rules

Latest Revision Date: March 6, 2023 (Beijing Time)

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace, prevent illegal profit-making activities, and enhance the user experience of members, these rules have been formulated.

2、Definitions and Manifestations
--------------------------------

Illegal profits refer to the actions of sellers that fabricate or conceal transaction facts, violate or evade AliExpress rules, and are suspected of infringing upon the property rights or other legitimate rights and interests of others. They include but are not limited to the following situations:

（1）Sellers deceive buyers by publishing or providing false or mismatched goods, services, or logistics information to obtain transaction funds.

（2）Sellers induce their trading counterparts to deviate from the normal AliExpress transaction process and gain unfair advantages.

（3）Sending phishing links or Trojan virus messages to deceive others' property.

（4）Using unfair means, non-genuine transactions, or illegal methods to obtain subsidies from the AliExpress platform, including but not limited to platform After-Sales Compensation, platform coupons, promo codes, platform discounts, deposits, platform compensation funds, commissions, and bounty incentives.

（5）Deceiving others by impersonating AliExpress or its affiliated company employees or AliExpress store customer service.

（6）Using third-party accounts to commit fraud and deceive others of their property.

（7）Sellers illegally profit through means other than violating AliExpress rules.

（8）For the purpose of sales, sellers engage in or assist buyers in various types of tax evasion behaviors in any explicit or implied manner.

3、Violation and Penalties
-------------------------

Based on comprehensive risk investigations conducted by the platform, including data analysis, feedback from buyers or other sellers, and various potential risks, sellers' goods, transactions, etc., are evaluated for risks associated with illegal profit-making behaviors. Illegal profit-making behaviors are categorized as general violations, serious violations, and particularly serious violations based on severity.

General Violation: The platform determines that the seller has engaged in illegal profit-making behavior, but the number of orders involved or the amount of illegal profits is small, and the severity of the situation is minor.

Serious Violation: The platform determines that the seller has engaged in illegal profit-making behavior, and the number of orders involved or the amount of illegal profits is large, or the proportion of illegal profits to the store's total transaction amount is high, or it has caused consumers a negative shopping experience, or the platform has issued a warning but no corrective action has been taken.

Particularly Serious Violation: The platform determines that the seller has engaged in illegal profit-making behavior, using egregious methods, and the number of orders involved or the amount of illegal profits is significantly large, or the proportion of illegal profits to the store's total transaction amount is very high, or it has caused consumers a serious negative shopping experience or damages to the platform, or the platform has issued two or more warnings or penalties, and the store continues to violate the rules.

**Penalties:**

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| General Violation | 6 points/time |
| Serious Violation | 12 points/time |
| Particularly Serious Violation | 48 points/time |

Suppose the platform discovers that a seller is suspected of illegal profit-making. In that case, the platform has the right to immediately take restrictive measures against the seller, including but not limited to restricting product listing permissions, removing online products, freezing the seller's account, freezing the seller's Alipay account/AliExpress account, and extending the loan repayment time. The platform also has the right to close orders related to illegal profit-making behaviors (such as orders obtained by taking advantage of coupons) regardless of the order status (even if the seller has shipped the order or the order status is already marked as successful). After closing the order, the payment will be refunded to the buyer, and the platform subsidy will be returned to the platform. At the same time, if the seller has obtained platform subsidies through illegal profit-making behaviors, the platform has the right to demand that the seller bear breach of contract liability and directly deduct an amount equal to the funds involved in the illegal profit-making from the seller's Alipay international account or AliExpress account as liquidated damages. If there is an insufficient amount for a deduction, AliExpress has the right to continue to recover the remaining amount from the seller.

In addition to the penalties mentioned above, if the seller causes damage to the platform through illegal profit-making, the platform has the right to seek compensation from the seller and reserves other remedies permitted by law, including reporting to public security or procuratorial authorities.

Note 1: If you have objections to the related penalties or the orders involved in the violations, please file an online appeal against the punishment for illegal profit-making or serious disruption of platform order on the Merchant Backstage - Violations page. Please be sure to initiate the appeal within the time limit specified on the penalty page and submit supporting materials for each order, including but not limited to authentic and valid delivery orders or delivery certificates provided by the official postal service, logistics in transit proof, and logistics package weight settlement information. Please refer to the penalty notice for specific instructions. If your appeal is rejected or exceeds the appeal deadline during the appeal period, the platform will enforce the penalties and deduct the liquidated damages based on the content of the penalty notice. For clarity, the calculation of liquidated damages will be based on the final details of the violation order.

4、Interpretation
----------------

How should the funds involved in illegal profits be understood?

It refers to the subsidies provided by the AliExpress platform, such as platform coupons or other benefits, that are associated with violation orders committed during the seller's operation. These subsidies are funds provided by the platform to buyers in the form of promotional measures or other entitlements.

Taking platform coupons as an example, they are a type of promotional measure provided by the platform using its own funds. They are a form of preferential treatment that allows buyers to enjoy a certain amount of discount when making purchases. After the buyer uses the platform coupon during the transaction, the seller can directly or indirectly obtain cash from the platform by settling through the system or through manual means. If a seller illegally profits by taking advantage of coupons issued by the platform during their operation on the platform, the funds involved in illegal profits refer to the total sum of the face value of the coupons used by buyers in violation orders associated with the seller.

Note: When deducting the liquidated damages (an amount equal to the funds involved in illegal profits), the platform will convert the face value of the coupons into the currency of the seller's funding account based on the real-time exchange rate provided by the Bank of China. If a buyer requests a refund for a violation order or initiates a dispute process (such as a "dispute") or a chargeback in the system, the order will still be handled based on the platform's dispute rules and chargeback rules. The costs and liabilities associated with resolving disputes or chargebacks caused by disputes or chargebacks are the direct responsibility of the seller.
