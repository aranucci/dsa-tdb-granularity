全球速卖通关于禁止销售彩票的公告

发布日期：-

亲爱的速卖通商家：

根据国内外各国家及地区对于彩票类商品管控的相关法律法规及监管机构的相关规定，经综合考量，全球速卖通禁止商家销售彩票。

【管控范围】
------

彩票类商品，包括但不限于乐透、刮刮乐、大百万乐透、威力球乐透等各类实体或虚拟彩票。

【管控地区】
------

全球

【处置措施】
------

平台一旦发现商家有相关产品发布或销售行为，将对商品立即下架，对商家进行如下处罚：违规扣分2分/次。

平台提醒广大商家合规发品及合规交易，商家有责任遵守任何本国及销售目的国适用的产品合规法律，发布违规产品及信息的商家，平台将对相关产品及信息进行下架或删除，同时对存在违规情况的商家，依照相关平台规则进行处罚。对恶意绕过规则、在任何场景发布违规信息、重复违规、被监管部门通告、被重要媒体曝光以及存在其他严重违规情节的会员，依据平台相关规则，平台将采取包括但不限于删除产品信息、扣分、屏蔽店铺、限制使用网站产品功能、冻结账号直至关闭账号的处罚。我们将会持续关注各国的相关政策变化，并据此进行平台管控政策的相应调整。

Dear Sellers:

Pursuant to the relevant requirements of laws and regulations all around the world for the control of lottery tickets, Aliexpress.com will ban the sale of Lottery tickets.

\[Prohibited Product Scope\]
----------------------------

Lottery tickets, including but not limited to Lotto, Instant win tickets or scratch-cards, Powerball, Mega Millions, etc. whether in physical or virtual form.

\[Countries of Control\]
------------------------

Global

\[Violations and penalties\]
----------------------------

Once the platform discovers that the merchant has any relevant product release or sales behavior, related products or information will immediately be deleted and AliExpress will deduct 2 points for each violation.

AliExpress members shall list and sell products in accordance with applicable rules and abide by applicable laws and regulations of any country of origin and any country of destination. Where any member is found to be listing products or releasing information in violation of the rules, AliExpress will delist or delete the related products or information and punish the violator in accordance with relevant rules. For a member that knowingly circumvents the rules, provides information about prohibited products in any interaction scenarios, violates the rules repeatedly, is criticized in any form of media circulated by a regulator, receives negative publicity published by media, or commits any other serious violation, AliExpress will impose penalties, including but not limited to deleting product information, deducting points, blocking stores, restricting access to the website's product features, and freezing or even closing the account, in accordance with the platform's relevant rules.
