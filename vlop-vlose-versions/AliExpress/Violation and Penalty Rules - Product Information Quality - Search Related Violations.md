Illustration of Search Related Violations and Enforcement Actions

In order to ensure the search experience of buyers and provide a fair competition environment for sellers, AliExpress Platform will continuously take efforts against search related violations.

AliExpress Platform will monitor and take enforcement actions against search related violations according to this Illustration of Search Related Violations and Enforcement Actions (these “**Rules**”).

PART I DISPLAY OF PRODUCT INFORMATION: ABUSE IN POSTING
=======================================================

Chapter I Categories Put Wrong
------------------------------

### 1.1. Definition

“Catogries Put Wrong”refers to the product category selected or set to post a product is inconsistent with the actual category of the product, for instance, the product category selected or set for “fabric” is “wedding dress” and the special transaction items such as gifts / additional shipping fees / price difference / VIP / drop shipping are not categorized as other special categories.  
“Catogries Put Wrong”may result in display of the product on the website under a wrong category. The AliExpress Platform will regulate and deal with the problems of product categorization. Please note that you are required to check your product categorization and rectify the wrong category, and to select / fill in the correct category information for newly posted products.

### 1.2 Enforcement Actions against “Catogries Put Wrong”

Enforcement actions includes adjusting search rankings, deleting the products and taking the products off shelves will be taken against the products displayed under the wrong categories. Measures of adjusting search rankings will be taken against all products or part of the products (including products involved in “Catogries Put Wrong” and products not involved in “Catogries Put Wrong”) in a store whose products listed in violation of these Rules reach a certain amount. In the case of serious violation, all products in the store will be blocked. In the case of significant violation, the seller’s account will be frozen or closed.

Chapter 4 Repeated Posting of Products
--------------------------------------

### 4.1 Definition

Repeated Posting of Products refers to the products information posted is identical with or substantially similar in terms of main information (such as image, title, price, attribute, etc.) with other products posted in the same store or in other stores opened by the same seller or by the same entity as identified by the AliExpress Platform o based on multiple factors.

Please be reminded that Repeated Posting of Products is strictly prohibited. The seller shall avoid directly using the main images of the posted products or copying the titles and attributes of the posted products when posting new products. In addition to showing the differences in the main images, the seller shall also fill in key information on the products such as title, attribute and other detailed description.

### 4.2 Enforcement Actions against Repeated Posting of Products

Enforcement actions including adjusting search rankings, deleting the products and taking the products off shelves will be taken against the products involved in Repeated Posting of Products. Measures of adjusting search rankings will be taken against all or part of the products (including products which violate these Rules and products which do not violate these Rules) in a store whose posted products in violation of these Rules reach a certain amount. In the case of serious violation, all products in the store will be blocked. In the case of extremely serious violation, the seller’s account will be frozen or closed.

Chapter 5 Other Description Violations
--------------------------------------

### 5.1 Definition

Other Description Violations refer to non-standard display of product information which does not constitute Categories Put Wrong, Title Description Violation, Attributes Select Wrong or Repeated Posting of Products, which includes that: 1) the main images of products do not conform to the required standard, such as images comprising text only, images that may cause discomfort; 2) the measurement units of products do not follow the typical standard for sale, for instance, shoes are not sold in pairs; or 3) units of products posted are inconsistent with available units in stock and other descriptions which may easily lead to buyers’ complaints. For example, units of the product posted are 100pcs/lot while the units in stock are only 50pcs

### 5.2 Enforcement Actions against Other Description Violations

Enforcement actions including adjusting search rankings, deleting the products and taking the products off shelves will be taken against the products involved in Other Description Violations. Measures of adjusting search rankings will be taken against all products or part of the products (including products involved in violation and products not involved in violation) in a store whose posted products in violation of these Rules reach a certain amount. In the case of serious violation, all products in the store will be blocked. In the case of significant violation, the seller’s account will be frozen or closed.

If Other Description Violations also constitute other improper posting actions provided in Article 86 (6) of the AliExpress Platform Rules for the AliExpress Sellers, the AliExpress Platform also has the right to take actions against the seller pursuant to this Article 86 (6).

PART III TRANSACTION RELATED VIOLATIONS
=======================================

Chapter 1 Credit Sale Hype Up
-----------------------------

### 1\. Credit Sale Hype Up

#### 1.1 Definition

Credit Sale Hype Up refers to increasing sales of a product and credit rating by abnormal means of transaction.

#### 1.2 Enforcement Actions against Credit Sale Hype Up

1.2.1 If Credit Sale Hype Up is committed for the first time, the AliExpress Platform will delete the relevant credit and sales, adjust the search rankings, delete the product, take the product off shelves, close the relevant order and refund the money, and deduct 6 points/time, 12 points/time, 24 points/time or 48 points/time or close the seller’s account depending on the seriousness of violation.

1.2.2 If Credit Sale Hype Up is committed for the second time or more, the AliExpress Platform has the right to close the seller’s account directly regardless of the seriousness of violation. If the AliExpress Platform does not take actions to close the seller’s account, it has the right to take other enforcement actions, including deleting the relevant credit and sales, adjusting the search rankings, deleting the product, taking the product off shelves, closing the relevant order and refunding the money, and deducting the points.

The AliExpress Platform will announce on a regular basis of enforcement actions against sellers that have committed Credit Sale Hype Up, and will, at all costs, take all measures against sellers that sell fake and inferior products using their credit rating obtained through Credit Sale Hype Up and take legal actions against such sellers. Please note that the AliExpress Platform highly values integrity and every seller must uphold this value.

Chapter 2 Product Change
------------------------

### 2.1 Definition

Product Change refers to change of product through editing the key attributes of a product that has been posted for sale such as category, brand, model, configuration, material and function, etc, which results in the change of the posted product into another product. The seller shall post a new product if the product is upgraded. Editing of a product that has been posted for sale due to upgrade of the product will be regarded as Product Change.

### 2.2 Enforcement Actions against Product Change

Enforcement actions including adjusting search rankings, deleting the products and taking the products off shelves will be taken against the products involved in Product Change. Measures of adjusting search rankings will be taken against all products or part of the products (including products involved in violation and products not involved in violation) in a store whose posted products in violation of these Rules reach a certain amount. In the case of serious violation, all products in the store will be blocked. In the case of significant violation, the seller’s account will be frozen or closed.

### 2.3 Illustrations (including but not limited to the following types)

#### 2.3.1 Example 1: product A is changed to product B of a completely different category.

(a): The product originally posted for sale is socks while the product currently displayed for sale is T-shirt.

(b): The product originally posted for sale is night lamp while the product currently displayed for sale is crystal light.

(c): The products originally posted for sale are tissues and sofas (posted in one and the same product ID, which is an example of Wrong SKU) while the products currently displayed for sale are sofas.

#### 2.3.2 Example 2: product A is changed to product B of a completely different brand.

(a): The products originally posted for sale are Nike sneakers while the products currently displayed for sale are Adidas sneakers.

#### 2.3.3 Example 3: product A is changed to product B of a completely different model, or the refurbished product is changed to a brand-new product.

(a): The product originally posted for sale is iPhone 6 while the product currently displayed for sale is iPhone 6s.

(b): The product originally posted for sale is refurbished iPhone while the product currently displayed for sale is brand new iPhone.

#### 2.3.4 Example 4: product A is changed to product B with a different configuration

(a): The products originally posted for sale are toys without remote control function while the products currently displayed for sale are toys with remote control function.
