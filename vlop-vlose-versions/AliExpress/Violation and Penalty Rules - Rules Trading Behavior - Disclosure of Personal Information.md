AliExpress "Disclosure of Personal Information" Rule

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace, protect the privacy and security of platform users, and enhance the user experience, this rule is formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

**"Personal information" includes but is not limited to**:

Buyer's username, platform ID, real name, mobile phone number, shipping address (partial or complete), messages, comments, personal characteristics, preferences, and other order or personal-related information. Disclosure of personal data includes but is not limited to the following cases:

（1）Sellers disclose or transfer information to external parties without the written and explicit permission of the buyer and the platform (permission should be provided by the seller as evidence). The term "external parties" includes but is not limited to unnecessary internal employees, external third-party companies or individuals, and unspecified entities. The term "disclosure or transfer" includes but is not limited to any method, whether online or offline, public or targeted, explicit or implied, written or encrypted. For example, a seller publicly discloses personal information on the platform without permission, and a seller discloses personal information to third-party websites without permission, a seller discloses personal information to third-party companies that are not involved in completing transactions with buyers, or a seller discloses buyer's order information to non-buyers without permission.

（2）Sellers publish products for non-genuine transaction purposes and subsequently disclose buyer information obtained through it.

（3）Sellers' negligence in employee management, account management, system security, etc., leading to the disclosure of personal information. Negligence includes but is not limited to: lack of basic information security measures within the seller's store, poor employee management, obvious risks of information leakage in the system but no effective remedial measures taken by the seller.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

**"Disclosure of Personal Information" is divided into minor offenses and severe offenses based on severity. Severe violation behaviors include but are not limited to the following situations:**

（1）Disclosure of personal information to third-party websites, companies, or individuals.

（2）Bulk disclosure of personal information through technical means.

（3）Disclosure of personal information with subjective malicious intent, including but not limited to malicious methods or tactics.

（4）Other means of disclosure result in a significant quantity of leaked information.

（5）Cases where the disclosure of personal information may result in civil, administrative, or criminal liability.

（6）Cases where the disclosure of personal information causes serious consequences to users or the platform.

（7）Other severe instances of personal information disclosure.

Penalty Measures:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| Minor Offense | 2 points/time |
| Severe Offense | 12 points/time |
| Extremely Severe | 48 points/time |

In addition to the above rules, the platform reserves all legal rights to hold any user accountable for disclosing personal information, including but not limited to pursuing civil liability or reporting to competent authorities for administrative or criminal responsibilities.
