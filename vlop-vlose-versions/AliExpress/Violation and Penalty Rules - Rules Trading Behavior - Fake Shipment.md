AliExpress "fake shipment" Rules

Latest Revision Date: July 8, 2020 Beijing Time

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace, prevent fake shipment behaviors, and improve the user experience of members, these rules are established.

2、Definitions and Manifestations
--------------------------------

Within the specified shipment period, fake shipment behaviors include using invalid or irrelevant tracking numbers, a prolonged absence of logistics collection information, failure to ship according to the declared tracking number in the system, or failure to fulfill the shipment obligation as declared to the consumer.

**Manifestations of fake shipment:**

（1）Using invalid or irrelevant tracking numbers when declaring shipment (e.g., false tracking numbers, inconsistent recipient information and delivery addresses with the order);

（2）Seller declares shipment but there is a prolonged absence of logistics collection and delivery information, or the tracking number exceeds the time limit and is canceled;

（3）Seller fails to choose the shipping method according to the platform's logistics policy (e.g., inconsistency between the shipping tracking number and the declared shipment, unauthorized use of offline economic logistics for shipment, or using economic logistics to impersonate standard or express logistics);

（4）Seller declares shipment from a country different from the one chosen by the buyer for shipping or where the inventory is located, such as declaring overseas inventory but actually shipping from China.

**Friendly Reminder:**

（1）"Invalid tracking number" refers to a non-existent logistics tracking number (including situations where the logistics information cannot be traced due to using unregistered small packages).

（2）"Irrelevant tracking number" refers to a logistics tracking number that exists but is unrelated to the order, including but not limited to discrepancies in order placement time (e.g., the receipt time is significantly earlier than the order placement time) or the delivery address is different from the one provided by the buyer (e.g., the delivery address and recipient's address are in different countries).

（3）"Logistics collection and delivery information" refers to the first piece of information provided by the logistics provider, where online shipments are generally collected/received by the warehouse, and offline shipments generally have successful collection or delivery information provided by the logistics provider.

（4）If there is a problem with the logistics order declared for the first shipment, please make sure to modify the declared logistics tracking number within 10 working days. The platform does not accept logistics tracking numbers provided in order messages or buyer communications and only recognizes the logistics tracking number declared in the order.

3、Violation Categories and Penalties
------------------------------------

Fake shipment behaviors are categorized as general violations or serious violations based on severity.

Serious violations of fake shipment behaviors include but are not limited to the following situations:

（1）Large order amounts for fake shipments;

（2）Collusion between buyers and sellers to mislead the AliExpress platform in disbursing funds through fake shipment behaviors without actual transactions;

（3）Repeated incidents of general fake shipment behaviors.

**Penalties:**

| **Violation Category** | **Penalty** |
| General Violation | 2 points/time |
| Serious Violation | 12 points/time |
