AliExpress "Severe Malicious Underpricing" Rules

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace and prevent the practice of severe malicious underpricing, and to enhance the user experience of members, these rules are formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Severe malicious underpricing refers to the seller's act of significantly deviating from the normal selling price by offering a large number of products at a low price. Examples of particularly severe cases include but are not limited to the following:

（1）Severe malicious underpricing has a wide impact and may cause serious detrimental effects on the platform's security and reputation.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| General violation | 2 points/times |
| Severe violation | 12 points/times |
| Particularly severe violation | 48 points/times |
