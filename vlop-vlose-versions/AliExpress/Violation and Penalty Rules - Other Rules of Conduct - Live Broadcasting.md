AliExpress Live Broadcasting Platform Management Regulations

Release date: /

Revision date: September 5, 2024

  

Article 1 \[Scope of Application\]
----------------------------------

These regulations apply to all users (referred to collectively as "**Users**") who publish or broadcast content on the AliExpress live broadcasting platform (“**AliExpress Live**”). By publishing or broadcasting content on AliExpress Live on the AliExpress Live website ([https://live.aliexpress.com](https://live.aliexpress.com/)) or the AliExpress application, you agree or are deemed to have agreed that your access and use of AliExpress Live shall be subject to and regulated by these regulations.

Article 2 \[Effectiveness Level\]
---------------------------------

These regulations are supplemental to the [AliExpress Free Membership Agreement](https://terms.alicdn.com/legal-agreement/terms/suit_bu1_aliexpress/suit_bu1_aliexpress202204182115_37406.html),[AliExpress Terms of Use](https://terms.alicdn.com/legal-agreement/terms/suit_bu1_aliexpress/suit_bu1_aliexpress202204182115_66077.html?spm=a2g0o.new_account_index.0.0.244d25b9T2HONe), [AliExpress Prohibited and Restricted Sales Rules](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/?tocUuid=KqeTC8M1puePAZkT), [AliExpress Intellectual Property Rules](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/?tocUuid=E0vEu_6L2JILeQTi) and [AliExpress Community Guidelines](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/137261302). In case of any conflict or inconsistency between the provisions of these regulations and those of the foregoing agreements and rules, these regulations shall prevail.

Article 3 \[Admission\]
-----------------------

1、Live Streamer

1.1 AliExpress users who apply and are granted permission to access the live broadcasting function on AliExpress Live are referred to as Live Streamers.

1.2 Live Streamers must be at least 18 of age.

1.3 AliExpress reserves the right to formulate and implement special requirements for AliExpress users to be granted access to the live broadcasting function on AliExpress Live from time to time.

2、Merchant Streamer

2.1 AliExpress seller with a normal store status with a certain number of loyal fans, customer operation capabilities, and hosting qualities are referred to as Merchant Streamers and may be granted permission to access the live broadcasting function on AliExpress Live.

2.2 AliExpress reserves the right to formulate and implement special requirements for AliExpress sellers to be granted access to the live broadcasting function on AliExpress Live from time to time.

3、Interactive Viewers

3.1 AliExpress users who have completed the account registration can interact with the streamers (i.e. comment) during the live broadcasting on AliExpress Live and are referred to as Interactive Viewers.

3.2 Consent from guardians must be sought in advance for Interactive Viewers who are under the age of 18 if required by applicable laws and regulations.

Article 4 \[Information Publication\]
-------------------------------------

All Users who publish or broadcast content on AliExpress Live (including Live Streamers, Merchant Streamers and Interactive Viewers) are prohibited from:

1、publishing harmful information, such as sensitive information, obscene and pornographic information, etc.;

2、publishing false information, such as false advertising, fake prize draw, discrepancies between promoted product information and actual information, etc.;

3、faking activity information;

4、publishing spam advertisements;

5、publishing any other information that violates applicable laws and regulations or is not allowed to be published by the AliExpress Live under these regulations, including as set out in the Violation Handling Overview.

Article 5 \[Code of Conduct\]
-----------------------------

1、All Users who publish or broadcast content on AliExpress Live (including Live Streamers, Merchant Streamers and Interactive Viewers) are prohibited from:

1.1 engaging in promotion activity that violates the platform agreements or rules, such as promoting products that may involve counterfeit goods or violating platform promotion rules;

1.2 engaging in behaviors that may lead to transaction risks, such as guiding users to engage in offline transactions or posting products or information from external websites;

1.3 infringing upon the rights of others, such as leaking others' information, improper use of others' rights, or harassing others, as well as infringing intellectual property rights of others;

1.4 disrupting platform order, such as engaging in fraud or cheating, providing false information, etc.;

1.5 violating commitments and guarantees to users or the platform;

1.6 violating the streamer requirements published or updated by AliExpress from time to time;

1.7 engaging in any of the activities set out in the Violation Handling Overview.

2、All Users are responsible for the activities conducted on their account, including the publishing or broadcasting of any content on AliExpress Live, and must ensure that their activities are in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

3、All Users:

3.1 must keep their account information (including password and other login details) confidential and secure; and

3.2 must not provide any third party with access to their account. If any User knows or suspects any unauthorised access to or use of their account, the User must promptly notify AliExpress through Customer Service – Help Center.

4、Once the Live Streamer or the Merchant Streamer has created a live stream, AliExpress shall be entitled to review the title, cover picture, preview and proposed merchandise of the live stream for content moderation and safety evaluation purposes.

Article 6 \[Violation Handling\]
--------------------------------

1、Streamers:

In the event of a violation of these regulations by streamer, in addition to any of the penalties that may be imposed (as set out in the Violation Handling Overview), AliExpress reserves the right to issue warnings, take down the live broadcast, delete live content, freeze live broadcasting function, or terminate the account of any Live Streamer or Merchant Streamers immediately and without prior notice.

2、Interactive Viewers:

In the event of a violation of these regulations by Interactive Viewer, in addition to any of the penalties that may be imposed (as set out in the Violation Handling Overview), AliExpress reserves the right to delete the violating content, suspend live broadcast commenting function, or disable live broadcast commenting function of any Interactive Viewer immediately and without prior notice.

For specific violations and corresponding handling measures and penalties, please refer to the Violation Handling Overview.

3、In addition to the penalties set out in the Violation Handling Overview and the right of termination under Article 7, AliExpress reserves the right to bring legal proceedings against any User as AliExpress feels is necessary or as required or permitted under law. AliExpress further reserves the right to report Users and disclose any information relating to Users’ accounts, streaming and commenting activity to law enforcement authorities if it deems necessary or if it is required or permitted under applicable law.

Article 7 \[Termination\]
-------------------------

In addition to any of the penalties that may be imposed (as set out in the Violation Handling Overview), the account of any Live Streamer or Merchant Streamers may be terminated immediately in the following circumstances:

1、no longer satisfying the admission criteria specified in Article 3 of these regulations.

2、being subject to termination as listed in the Violation Handling Overview.

3、violating any applicable laws and regulations or guidelines.

4、no live broadcasting activity within three months.

5、streamer has been convicted of a serious crime or offense anywhere in the world or has engaged in violent or illegal acts whether offline or online on any online platform in the opinion of AliExpress.

Article 8 \[Reinstatement after Termination\]
---------------------------------------------

Live Streamer or Merchant Streamer whose account is:

1、terminated under Article 7.1 or 7.4 may apply for reinstatement.

2、terminated under Article 7.2 will be subject to the provisions listed in the Violation Handling Overview.

3、terminated under Article 7.3 or 7.5 will not be allowed to apply for reinstatement.

Any reinstatement shall be at the sole and absolute discretion of AliExpress and AliExpress is not obligated to approve any reinstatement application.

Article 9 \[Exclusion of Liability\]
------------------------------------

ALIEXPRESS AND ITS AFFILIATED COMPANIES EXCLUDE THEIR LIABILITY FOR ALL ACTIONS THAT THEY MAY TAKE IN RESPONSE TO VIOLATIONS OF THESE REGULATIONS. THE ACTIONS THAT ALIEXPRESS AND ITS AFFILIATED COMPANIES MAY TAKE ARE NOT LIMITED TO THOSE DESCRIBED ABOVE, AND ALIEXPRESS AND ITS AFFILIATED COMPANIES MAY TAKE ANY OTHER ACTION THAT THEY REASONABLY DEEM APPROPRIATE. THE USER SHALL INDEMNIFY ALIEXPRESS AND ITS AFFILIATED COMPANIES ON DEMAND AGAINST ALL CLAIMS, DEMANDS, LIABILITIES, LOSSES, DAMAGES, COSTS AND EXPENSES ARISING DIRECTLY OR INDIRECTLY FROM A BREACH OF THESE REGULATIONS OR AS A RESULT OF ANY ACT OR OMISSION OF THE USER IN RELATION TO THE USE OF ALIEXPRESS LIVE.

Article 10 \[Revision\]
-----------------------

AliExpress reserves the right to update these regulations time to time and the revised regulations will be published on [https://rule.aliexpress.com](https://rule.aliexpress.com/). If Users do not agree to these updated regulations, they should immediately stop using any of the functions available on AliExpress Live. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the revised regulations.
