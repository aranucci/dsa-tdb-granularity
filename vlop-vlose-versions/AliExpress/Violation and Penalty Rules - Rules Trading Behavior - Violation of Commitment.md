AliExpress "Violation of Commitment Penalty" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

  

1、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Violation of commitment refers to the seller's failure to provide services to the buyer in accordance with the following commitments, thereby harming the legitimate rights of the buyer:

**（1）Commitments related to transactions and after-sales services, including but not limited to：**

1) The seller refuses to trade at the price agreed upon by the buyer (except for offline agreements between the two parties), or the seller promises discounts on the product price but fails to fulfill them.

2) The seller promises to provide the buyer with gifts or invoices, or other items beyond the transaction goods but fails to deliver or provide them.

3) The seller promises to provide after-sales services such as returns, exchanges, or repairs to the buyer but fails to fulfill them.

**（2）Commitments related to logistics, including but not limited to:**

1) The seller promises free shipping in the product title or content, but the buyer discovers that there is a shipping fee when placing the actual order.

2) The seller makes self-commitments or agrees with the buyer on specific shipping methods, logistics companies, etc., during the transaction process but fails to fulfill the relevant commitments or agreements.

3) The seller promises to bear the return shipping fee but fails to fulfill it.

**（3）Violation of established platform rules or requirements, including but not limited to:**

1) The platform requires both buyers and sellers to conduct transactions online, but the seller guides the buyer to not make payments and transactions through the AliExpress platform in various ways.

2) The seller participates in official AliExpress activities but fails to provide services according to the activity requirements (except for delivery time).

**（4）Sellers violating other commitments made by themselves.**

2、Violation Behaviors and Penalty Measures
------------------------------------------

For the aforementioned violation of commitment behaviors, the platform has the right to independently judge and impose the following penalties: Within one performance year (starting from the date of the first identified violation of commitment; the second performance year starts 365 days after the end of the first performance year, and so on):

（1）If identified as violating commitment behavior for the 1st to 5th times, deduct 1 point per occurrence from the store.

（2）If identified as violating commitment behavior for the 6th to 10th times, deduct 2 points per occurrence from the store.

（3）If identified as violating commitment behavior for the 11th time or more, deduct 3 points per occurrence from the store.
