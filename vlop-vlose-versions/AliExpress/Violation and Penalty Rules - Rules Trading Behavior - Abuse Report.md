AliExpress "Abuse Report" Rules

Release date: August 25, 2023

Revision date: October 17, 2024

  

1、Scope of Application
----------------------

AliExpress encourages global users to use the reporting function to report illegal behavior. In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace and to uphold the quality of reports, rules have been established to prevent the abuse of the reporting function.

When users use the reporting function, the reported behavior includes:

(1) Reports initiated by buyers or unregistered users:

1) Intellectual property complaints (limited to rights holders)

2) Intellectual property infringement reports (excluding rights holders)

3) Prohibited or restricted sales

(2) Reports initiated by sellers:

1) Intellectual property complaints (limited to rights holders)

2) Intellectual property infringement reports (excluding rights holders)

3) Prohibited or restricted sales

4) Violation of product listing rules

5) Search manipulation

6) Reporting malicious buyers

The platform has the right to clarify illegal behavior and add new reportable scenarios, and notify through the platform rules center, in-site messages, etc. before execution. Please pay attention.

2、Definition and Presentation
-----------------------------

### (1) The submitted content is illegal

Illegal content refers to information that endangers national and social security according to the laws and regulations of various countries and other illegal speech and illegal content stipulated in the laws and regulations of various countries, including but not limited to:

1) Oppose the basic principles established by the laws of various countries;

2) Endanger the unity, sovereignty and territorial integrity of all countries;

3) Leaking state secrets, endangering national security, or damaging national honor and interests;

4) Incite ethnic hatred and discrimination, undermine ethnic unity, or infringe on ethnic customs and habits;

5) Undermining national religious policies and promoting cults and superstitions;

6) Spread rumors, disrupt social order, and undermine social stability;

7) Promote gambling, violence, murder, terror, drug abuse, pyramid schemes, self-abuse, or instigate crime;

8) Inciting illegal assembly, association, procession, demonstration, or gathering of people to disrupt social order;

9) Obscene and pornographic information, including but not limited to:

**·** Specific depictions of sexual acts or explicit promotion of pornographic and obscene information;

**·** Exposed pictures and videos, audios and texts with pornographic connotations, hints, provocations and arousing similar feelings in others;

**·** Vulgar information, such as: using only a small covering to cover the hidden parts of the human body, wearing revealing clothing, topless, naked children, breastfeeding performances, etc., except for descriptions, instructions and comments related to product functions.

### (2) The submitted content has no obvious basis, including but not limited to the following scenarios:

1) Incorrect or unrelated links to other products;

2) Failure to clearly and accurately express the reasons for the report or the reported reasons clearly do not match the actual situation;

3) Failure to provide valid evidence to support the reported facts;

4) Repeatedly submitting duplicate reports for the same reported content.

### (3) The submitted content does not comply with platform rules.

When your report content is rejected, please prioritize reviewing the rule requirements under the corresponding report category. You can also check the rule details on the report submission page:

1) Intellectual property complaints (limited to rights holders) and intellectual property infringement reports (excluding rights holders): [Update of Enforcement Actions for Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/127561565);

2) Prohibited or restricted sales: [AliExpress Index of Prohibited and Controlled Items](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/127561551);

3) Reporting malicious buyers: [AliExpress "Unfair Competition" Rules](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/137340205).

3、Violation and Penalties
-------------------------

The platform will take measures to warn and restrict the use of the reporting function for users who engage in abusive reporting behavior. The platform has the right to independently determine the severity of the violation based on the frequency of users' reports and impose the following penalties.

For general violations where the number of abuse reports exceeds that of most platform users within a certain period, the platform will issue a warning to the user. For serious violations where users use scripts and other tools to submit reporting information in batches or submit reports far more than the average user within a certain period, the platform will take measures such as freezing the user's reporting function for 3 days or freezing the user's account. After the penalty period expires, the user's reporting function will be restored.
