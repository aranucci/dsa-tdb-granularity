Rules on Product Safety and Compliance on AliExpress

Release date: /

Revision date: January 15, 2026

  

AliExpress is committed to providing consumers with safe and quality goods, creating a proper business environment for AliExpress users, continuously improving consumers’ shopping experience and safeguarding the rights and interests of honest sellers. As a seller, you are responsible to ensure that the goods you offer comply with the relevant laws and regulations in the countries you are selling to. Before listing the relevant product, please make sure that you understand your responsibilities as seller.

The following are the rules related to product safety and compliance on AliExpress:
