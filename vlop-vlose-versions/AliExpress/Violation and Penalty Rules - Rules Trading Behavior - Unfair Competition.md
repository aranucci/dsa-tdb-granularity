AliExpress "Unfair Competition" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

Alibaba's Global AliExpress platform has always been committed to maintaining an honest, fair, and just trading environment and continues to supervise and crack down on behaviors that affect the legitimate rights and interests of other users or the platform.

1、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

（1）The product information, store decoration, or other information published by a seller creates confusion or misleads consumers and other sellers into believing that there is a specific connection with other sellers' normal operations.

（2）A seller engages in malicious behaviors such as intentionally placing orders, leaving malicious reviews, or making malicious complaints against other sellers, which affect the reputation and normal operations of other sellers.

Malicious Orders: Refers to a seller using an overseas member account to place orders against other sellers, which adversely affect their normal operations, such as placing orders for inventory without payment.

Malicious Reviews: Refers to a seller using an overseas member account to place orders against other sellers, intentionally leaving negative reviews or reviews that do not reflect the facts.

Malicious Complaints: Refers to a seller making complaints against other sellers through themselves or using other member accounts, with complaints lacking relevant evidence or improper complaint purposes, intending to damage the reputation of fellow sellers, extort legitimate sellers, or deliberately undermine the legitimate operations of fellow sellers.

Behaviors with particularly severe circumstances of unfair competition include but are not limited to the following situations:

（1）Causing severe negative impacts on the normal operations of other sellers.

（2）Causing serious misunderstandings, confusion, and significantly affecting the shopping experience of buyers.

（3）Making false statements or providing false supporting documents during the platform investigation process.

（4）Sellers who refuse platform reminders or rectification requests while knowingly and intentionally committing violations.

2、Violation and Penalties
-------------------------

Regarding the aforementioned unfair competition behaviors, the platform has the right to independently judge the severity of the violations and impose the following penalties:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |     |
| General Violation | Being identified as engaging in "unfair competition" behavior for the first time, deduct 3 points from the store, and delete the products and store decoration information causing unfair competition. |     |
| Severe Violation | Being identified as engaging in "unfair competition" behavior for the second time, deduct 12 points from the store, freeze the account for 7 days, and delete the products and store decoration information causing unfair competition. |     |
| Particularly Severe Violation | Engaging in behaviors with particularly severe circumstances or being identified as engaging in "unfair competition" behavior three times or more, deduct 48 points from the store and close the store. |     |
