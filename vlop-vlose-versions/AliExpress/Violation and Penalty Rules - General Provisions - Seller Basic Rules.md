Chapter III Violations and Penalty Rules
========================================

Section I Violation Handling Measures
-------------------------------------

**Article 69** To safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of consumers, operators, and AliExpress, the platform may impose the following measures on members during the violation handling period in accordance with these rules. These measures may remain in effect until the risk is deemed largely controllable by AliExpress, at which point they may be partially or fully lifted:

(i) Warning – AliExpress may issue a verbal or written warning to sellers regarding improper conduct.

(ii) Search Ranking Adjustment – This refers to the adjustment of the ranking of all or part of a store's goods in search results.

(iii) Product Visibility Block – This refers to all goods (including compliant and non-compliant items) that will be hidden from front-end search results and only visible in the seller's own store.

(iv) In-platform Messaging Restriction – This refers to the restriction on sending in-platform messages within the AliExpress platform.

(v) Review Removal – This refers to the removal of store ratings and review content; removed reviews will not be counted in performance metrics.

(vi) Goods Listing Restriction – This refers to the restriction on posting new goods by the member on AliExpress.

(vii) Brand Delisting – This refers to the restriction or prohibition of displaying goods under specific brands on the platform.

(viii) Goods Delisting – This refers to the removal of a member’s product(s) from sale by AliExpress as a penalty measure.

(ix) Goods Deletion – This refers to the deletion of goods from the member's account by AliExpress as a penalty measure.

(x) Marketing Participation Restriction – This refers to the restriction to sellers on participating in marketing campaigns organized or supported by the platform.

(xi) Operating Privilege Closure – This refers to the closure of a seller's right to operate within specific or all business categories.

(xii) Advance Payment Feature Closure – This refers to closing the seller's access to early fund withdrawal services.

(xiii) Increase in Security Deposit for Stores – This refers to the requirement for violating stores to pay an increased security deposit in order to continue operating. Sellers must, within 14 days of receiving the payment notice, top up the store's security deposit to no less than CNY 30,000 before normal business operations can resume. Otherwise, all goods under the store may be subject to visibility restrictions in certain countries or regions.

(xiv) Account Freeze – This refers to the takedown of all active goods and restriction on new goods listings.

(xv) Seller Funds Freeze – This refers to the freeze of the seller's account funds (including but not limited to Alipay International and AliExpress accounts) until the platform determines that risks are under control.

(xvi) Account Closure – This refers to the freezing of the account, restriction on sending in-platform messages, suspension of store access, and a 180-day freeze on seller funds.

(xvii) AliExpress reserves the right to review and process the seller's remaining orders based on the severity of the violation at the time of account closure.

(xviii) Country-Specific Sales Restriction – This refers to the restriction on selling goods to specific countries.

(xix) Credit and Sales Record Deletion – This refers to the deletion of all or part of the seller's credit points and/or sales records for all or selected goods.

In addition to the aforementioned violation handling measures, if a seller violates applicable national laws, regulations, or platform rules, and such violation directly or indirectly causes AliExpress to suffer significant losses, AliExpress reserves the right to hold the seller fully liable for all resulting damages. These losses include, but are not limited to: any direct or indirect penalties imposed by regulatory authorities due to the seller's illegal actions; costs incurred by AliExpress in taking remedial actions to protect the legitimate rights and interests of the platform, other sellers, or buyers; any liquidated damages or compensation payable by AliExpress to third parties as a result of legal rulings; refunds, penalties, or compensation paid by AliExpress on behalf of the seller to buyers; legal fees incurred by AliExpress; and any other necessary expenses for mitigating adverse impact.

AliExpress will handle ongoing orders as follows:

1\. Orders placed but not paid by buyers: Orders will be closed.

2\. Orders paid but pending risk control review, and not yet shipped by seller: Orders will be closed, and full refunds will be issued to buyers. (Note: The platform defines "shipment" based on whether the seller has filled in and submitted a valid tracking number.)

3\. Orders paid and passed risk control review, but not yet shipped by seller: Orders will be frozen. The seller must provide shipping proof and procurement proof (and relevant qualification certificates for certain products). If the seller fails to provide valid documentation, the order will be closed and fully refunded to the buyer; if the documentation is verified, the order will be unfrozen and proceed through the normal transaction flow. (Note: The platform defines "shipment" based on whether the seller has filled in and submitted a valid tracking number.)

4\. Orders already shipped but with no disputes raised: Orders will be frozen. The seller must provide shipping proof and procurement proof (and relevant qualification certificates for certain products). If the seller fails to provide valid documentation, the order will be closed and fully refunded to the buyer; if the documentation is verified, the order will be unfrozen and proceed through the normal transaction flow.

5\. Orders with existing disputes: The seller must provide supporting evidence within the appeal period specified by the AliExpress platform. AliExpress will make a determination based on the evidence submitted by both the buyer and the seller.

6\. Orders successfully completed but not yet disbursed: The order payment will be frozen. The seller must provide shipping proof and procurement proof (and relevant qualification certificates for certain products). If the seller fails to provide valid documentation, the full order amount will be refunded to the buyer.

7\. Other order situations: For any other circumstances not explicitly covered above, the AliExpress platform may handle such cases at its discretion, and may require the seller to submit relevant supporting documentation for assessment.

8\. If a member is suspected of registering or controlling additional accounts on the AliExpress platform, the above order handling measures will also apply to orders associated with such related accounts.

Section II Violation Types and Handling Measures
------------------------------------------------

**Article 70** The platform classifies violations into four distinct point-based systems according to the nature of the misconduct: Intellectual Property Prohibited and Restricted Sales Violations, Transaction and Other Violations, Goods Information Quality Violations, and Severe Intellectual Property Violations. Each of the four point systems operates independently in terms of point deduction, accumulation, and enforcement of penalties.

**Article 71** Intellectual Property Prohibited and Restricted Sales Violations include general intellectual property infringement and illegal publication of prohibited or restricted items. When a seller accumulates 48 points under this category, the account will be closed.

**Article** **72** Severe Intellectual Property Violations include acts of serious intellectual property infringement. Accounts will be closed upon the third confirmed violation. If the infringement is deemed particularly severe, the account will be immediately closed without needing to reach three violations.

**Article 73** Transaction and Other Violations include transaction violations and other behaviors strictly prohibited by the platform. Once 48 points are accumulated under this category, the account will be closed.

**Article 74** Goods Information Quality Violations include misleading listings such as search manipulation and other publication-related violations. If a seller accumulates 12 points or every multiple of 12, the account will be frozen for 7 days.

**Article 75** Point reset logic: Each violation under the four point systems is calculated on a rolling 12-month (365-day) basis. For example, if 6 points are deducted at 12:00 PM on February 1, 2013, those 6 points will be reset at 12:00 PM on February 1, 2014.

**Article 77** If the business entity operating a seller account is suspected of registering or controlling other accounts on the platform simultaneously, AliExpress may take actions such as freezing the related accounts, increasing the related stores' security deposit, restricting operations in certain countries or regions, or removing the related stores from the platform. In cases involving malicious or intentional serious violations, or where infringement is particularly egregious or extreme, the platform will close all stores under that entity and reserves the right to confiscate all security deposits paid by those stores.

**Article 78** If a seller is subject to account closure or removal from the platform due to violations of platform rules, AliExpress shall, in order to protect the legitimate rights and interests of buyers and rights holders, freeze the seller's Alipay International account and AliExpress account for six months. In cases where the violation is particularly serious or egregious, the platform reserves the right to extend the freeze period to one year or two years. For violations that significantly disrupt platform order and cause actual harm to the platform or other sellers, AliExpress reserves the right to freeze the seller's Alipay International account and AliExpress account until reasonable compensation has been made to the affected party.

**Article 79** During the freeze period, the seller is prohibited from making withdrawals or conducting any other fund operations via their Alipay International or AliExpress account. All funds in these accounts will remain frozen.

**Article 80** During the freeze period, if the seller does not incur any refund, compensation, or dispute obligations, then upon the expiration of the freeze period, the platform will return any remaining funds in the Alipay International account (if any) to the seller and close the Alipay International account.

**Article 81** During the freeze period, if the seller becomes liable for refunds or compensation due to disputes, chargebacks by banks, or other reasons, the platform reserves the right to process the corresponding refunds or compensation directly from the seller's Alipay International and/or AliExpress account. Upon expiration of the freeze period, the remaining account balance (if any) will be returned to the seller, and both the Alipay International and AliExpress accounts will be closed.

**Article 85** Others

**I. Improper Gains**

Unfair profiteering refers to the behavior of members using improper means to seek benefits, including:

(i) Members offer money, gifts, entertainment, hospitality, or commercial opportunities to AliExpress staff and/or their affiliates;

(ii) Members seek improper benefits from AliExpress staff through other means.

If a member is found to have engaged in improper gain, regardless of whether any benefit was actually obtained: The member's store(s) and any affiliated store(s) will be permanently closed; AliExpress will permanently cease to provide or accept any products or services from the member; if the member has violated improper gain policies or committed other dishonest behavior on other Alibaba Group platforms (including but not limited to Alibaba China Site, Alibaba International Site, Taobao, and Tmall), leading to account closure, AliExpress reserves the right to take equivalent action on the member's AliExpress accounts, whether owned or controlled by them.

For service providers found to have engaged in improper gain, AliExpress will permanently cease to provide or accept any products or services from them; stores operated by the service provider must switch to self-operation or another provider within three months of receiving AliExpress notification. If they fail to do so within the specified time, AliExpress will impose supervision measures on the store until the required changes are completed.

If the member truthfully and voluntarily discloses or actively reports their involvement in improper gain, AliExpress may, at its discretion, apply lenient or mitigated penalties.

From the date an investigation into improper gain is initiated, AliExpress will restrict the member's store(s) and related store(s) from participating in any marketing activities until the investigation concludes.

If a member explicitly expresses intent to engage in improper gain toward AliExpress staff and/or their affiliates, or has already initiated such behavior, the member's store(s) will be permanently closed, even if the attempt was unsuccessful due to reasons beyond the member's control.

The following circumstances shall be deemed as acts of improper gain:

(i) The seller is an AliExpress employee – the member's store will be immediately and permanently closed.

(ii) The seller is an affiliate of an AliExpress employee who failed to declare the relationship in accordance with the Alibaba Group Code of Business Conduct, and the employee leveraged their position – the member's store will be immediately and permanently closed.

**II. Improper Marketing Practices**

Sellers must engage in marketing in a lawful, compliant, and reasonable manner. Without prior consent from the buyer, sellers must not contact buyers in a disruptive way for marketing purposes. This includes, but is not limited to, sending emails, making phone calls, sending SMS messages, or using AliExpress system tools to reach out to buyers for promotional activities.

Section IV Liquidated Damages
-----------------------------

**Article 87** If a seller accumulates a total of 48 penalty points due to severe intellectual property violations, prohibited/restricted product listings, transaction violations and other violations, or if the account is directly closed in accordance with platform rules, the platform reserves the right to fully confiscate the security deposit paid on that account as compensation for the seller's breach of contract. If the seller is found to have committed intentional and serious violations, engaged in particularly egregious infringement, or clearly acted with malicious intent to evade platform control rules, the platform also reserves the right to confiscate the security deposit paid by the other associated accounts under the same entity.

**Article 88** If the remaining balance of the security deposit is insufficient for AliExpress to deduct the corresponding amount of the liquidated damages, the seller shall be responsible for paying the shortfall to AliExpress. If MYBank only fulfills part of the penalty to the platform, the seller shall be responsible for paying the shortfall to AliExpress.

**Article 89** If the seller's actions cause greater actual losses to the platform, AliExpress reserves the right to pursue further compensation from the seller until the full amount of the loss is recovered.
