AliExpress "Mismatched merchandise" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

1、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

To promote a fair trading environment, the behavior of selling Mismatched merchandise on the Alibaba Global AliExpress platform is considered a violation.

"Mismatched merchandise" refers to situations where the received product does not match the description or promises made by the seller at the time of the transaction in terms of category, parameters, material, specifications, etc.

**Manifestations of "Mismatched merchandise" include but are not limited to the following:**

（1）Sending empty packages to buyers;

（2）Products in the order are electronic storage devices with capacities that do not match the product description or promises;

（3）Products in the order are computer hardware with configurations that do not match the product description or promises;

（4）Products in the order are precious metals or gemstone jewelry with material contents that do not match the description;

（5）Received goods do not match the order information;

（6）Other situations where the ordered products do not match the shipped products.

2、Violation and Penalties
-------------------------

（1）General violations: There are slight quality or service issues based on buyer feedback, data analysis, or product inspections, but they do not have a substantial impact on the buyer's normal use.

（2）Serious violations: There are apparent quality or service issues based on buyer feedback, data analysis, or product inspections that have affected the buyer's normal use, or have caused harm to the buyer/other individuals' personal or property, or there have been three or more instances of general violations.

（3）Particularly serious violations: There are severe quality or service issues based on buyer feedback, data analysis, or product inspections, including but not limited to buyer disputes/evaluation data significantly lower than the level of similar products in the category, non-compliance with platform testing conducted directly or through third parties, and numerous incidents causing harm to the buyer/other individuals' personal or property; or there have been three or more instances of serious violations.

Violation Category Penalty General Violation 2 points/time.

Violating products cannot be relisted or must be deleted, and the store's search ranking is downgraded. Serious Violation 12 points/time.

Violating products cannot be relisted or must be deleted, and the store's search ranking is downgraded. Particularly Serious Violation 48 points/time. Store closure.

These rules are effective supplements to the "AliExpress Rules". All AliExpress sellers must comply with both the "AliExpress Rules" and the provisions of this standard when listing products. If the "AliExpress Rules" already have provisions, they shall be followed. If there are no provisions or if this standard has special provisions, this standard shall be followed.
