AliExpress "Disruption of Platform Order" Rule

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022 (Beijing Time)

  

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace, prevent behaviors that disrupt platform order, and enhance the user experience of members, this rule is formulated. Disruption of platform order refers to behaviors that interfere with platform management, disrupt platform order, and harm the legitimate rights and interests of other users or the platform.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

（1）Behaviors that maliciously evade platform rules or regulatory measures.

（2）Behaviors that interfere with the normal transactions of other users through malicious violations or other means.

（3）Behaviors that cause serious negative experiences for buyers during the shopping process, the trend towards damaging buyer's rights, and have a detrimental impact on the business environment of AliExpress.

（4）Merchants who do not cooperate or fail to timely submit/update compliance qualification documents in accordance with platform rules.

（5）Existence of issues related to product quality or store compliance, which may lead to judicial investigations, administrative supervision, administrative litigation, media coverage, public questioning, or involve goods, information, or behaviors that may be related to current affairs, public opinion events, and can easily trigger inappropriate public opinion.

（6）Other behaviors that disrupt platform order.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

The platform has the right to independently judge the severity of the circumstances and impose the following penalties on merchants who engage in the above-mentioned disruptive behaviors that violate platform order:

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| Minor Offense | 6 points/time |
| Severe Offense | 12 points/time |
| Extremely Severe 48 points per occurrence Offense | 48 points/time |

Meanwhile, the search rankings of shops involved in violations will be downgraded.

Note: The platform has the right to independently determine the severity of circumstances based on factors such as the level of malice in the means adopted, the amount of profits gained, the number of orders involved, the severity of consequences caused, and the impact on consumer experience.

4、Interpretation
----------------

（1）What does "maliciously evading platform rules or regulatory measures" refer to? This includes but is not limited to:

1）Evading the regulation of major categories for platform operations and posting goods that are not allowed to be sold in non-admissible major categories.

2）Evading category regulations of the platform and posting a large number of goods that are not suitable for cross-border sales and should not be sold (such as perfumes; edible health products and food such as tea, powdered animal/plant extracts, etc.).

3）Guiding buyers to modify cancellation or dispute reasons to evade platform assessment.

（2）What does "behaviors that cause serious negative experiences for buyers during the shopping process and have a detrimental impact on the business environment of AliExpress" refer to? This includes long-term selling of poor-quality products, choosing logistics methods with poor timeliness, etc., where the overall operating indicators of the store are significantly lower than the industry average (e.g., high dispute rate, DSR significantly lower than the average level, low positive feedback rate), significantly affecting the consumer experience and causing serious negative experiences for buyers.

（3）How is "extremely severe circumstances" defined? If there are circumstances where means adopted to disrupt platform orders are extremely malicious, involve a large amount of profits, affect a significant number of orders, cause adverse consequences, or severely impact the consumer experience, they are considered as extremely severe circumstances.
