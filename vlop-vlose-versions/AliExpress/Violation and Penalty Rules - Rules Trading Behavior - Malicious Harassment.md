AliExpress "Malicious Harassment" Rule

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace, prevent malicious harassment, and enhance the user experience of members, this rule is formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

It refers to the behavior of sellers frequently or using malicious means to harass members, which affects others' normal lives or impairs their legitimate rights and interests. Examples include demanding buyers to leave positive reviews or using abusive language towards buyers due to disputes. This behavior may include but is not limited to frequently contacting others through methods such as phone calls, text messages, AliWangWang, messages, emails, etc., which disrupts others' normal lives.

Severe malicious harassment behavior includes but is not limited to the following:

（1）The use of abusive language (such as "fuck," "suck," etc.), threats, curses, racial discrimination, religious or personal attacks, or repeated harassment that affects others' physical and mental well-being and normal lives; Extremely severe malicious harassment behavior includes but is not limited to the following:

（2）Harassment on a large scale, with a widespread impact, which may cause or has caused serious consequences to the platform's security and reputation;

（3）Using extreme means or tools to harass users, severely affects others' normal lives and causes significant harm to their physical and mental well-being. This includes but is not limited to mailing offensive or threatening items to members and publicly disclosing a buyer's personal phone number, address, name, purchase records, etc., on the internet.
