AliExpress Review Policy

Release date: July 31, 2024

Revision date: September 11, 2025

  

1\. Scope of application
========================

In order to ensure that every user of the AliExpress.com platform (referred to in this policy as "AliExpress" or the "Platform") can enjoy a fair and honest shopping experience, and to maintain a fair and competitive business environment, AliExpress has established this review policy (the “**Review Policy**”).

This Review Policy applies to all users of the Platform who submit for publication on the Platform any form of comment, evaluation, opinion, recommendation or rating about an experience with a product, a seller or the Platform (such user being a “**Reviewer**” and their comment, evaluation, opinion, recommendation or rating being a “**Review**”). This Review Policy sets out certain rules which all Reviewers, and the Reviews they submit, must comply with.

This Review Policy does not apply to feedback and/or complaints from users which are not intended for publication. To open a dispute or submit a report about a seller, please click [here](https://www.aliexpress.com/p/seller-report/nav.html). For any other support, please contact our help centre [here](https://helpcenter.aliexpress.com/s/BuyerHelp?spm=a2g0o.home.sitefooter.1.33f16278V1Kb6k&language=en_US&tsuCode=ByrCmn&terminal=pc&region=UK&suid=1030142508201815392141239C3682).

2\. How to submit a Review
==========================

A user may submit a Review in relation to a completed order by clicking on "Write a review" within the "My orders" page when logged into their AliExpress account. Logged in users may also submit answers through the app about products they have purchased, which may constitute a Review (if the definition in section 1 above is met), in response to other users' questions.

Reviewers should submit Reviews with the following purposes in mind:

1) to provide sellers with honest feedback regarding their experience with the relevant product; and

2) to help other buyer users obtain genuine information about products.

3\. Review Rules
================

All Reviews must reflect the Reviewer’s genuine experience with a product purchased on the Platform, a seller on the Platform or another aspect of the Platform and meet the requirements of applicable laws and regulations, as well as the [AliExpress Community Guidelines](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/137261302).

The following practices are **not permitted** on the Platform:

**(1) Submission of fake Reviews**, being Reviews which are not based on the Reviewer’s genuine experience of a product purchased on the Platform, a seller on the Platform or another aspect of the Platform.

**(2) Submission of incentivised Reviews**, being Reviews provided in exchange for rewards by sellers or third parties (such as money, coupons, gift cards, free or discounted products, etc.) and Reviews from or on behalf of individuals with a financial interest in the product or seller.

**(3) Submission of malicious Reviews**, being Reviews intended to damage a seller’s reputation or business which are not isolated, genuine Reviews.

**(4) Aggregation of hijacked Reviews**, which means the aggregation of Reviews which represent that they relate to a particular product, when in reality they relate to a different product.

Users who suspect that a Review contravenes this Review Policy may report that review to us for further investigation by clicking on the ellipsis provided next that Review.

4\. Violations and penalties
============================

AliExpress will remove any fake, incentivised, malicious and/or hijacked Reviews identified on the Platform, as well as any other Reviews which AliExpress deems to violate this Review Policy. Where applicable, AliExpress will update a seller’s or product’s average rating and review count to exclude any removed Reviews.

AliExpress may also take further action against violations of this Review Policy, including but not limited to by taking one or more of the following steps in relation to a product or a seller:

1) demoting search rankings for products where necessary as a result of any updated average rating;

2) banning user accounts;

3) banning users from leaving Reviews;

4) disapplying Review functionalities in relation to certain products;

5) removing all Reviews for a seller or a product;

6) delisting product listings;

7) refunding orders which were placed in reliance on a Review which breaches this Review Policy; and/or

8) where a seller is engaging in the submission of Reviews which breach this Review Policy, imposing a penalty on the seller which is proportionate, in AliExpress’ reasonable opinion, to the severity of the violation, determined as follows:

For more information on the measures that we may take based on the points accumulated, please refer to the [AliExpress "Credit and Sales Manipulation" Rules Supplemental Explanation](https://rule.aliexpress.com/rule-channels/37978936/137340756).
