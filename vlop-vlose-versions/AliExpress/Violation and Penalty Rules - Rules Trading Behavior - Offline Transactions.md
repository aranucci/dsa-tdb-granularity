AliExpress "Guiding Offline Transactions" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace and prevent offline transactions, and to enhance the user experience of members, these rules are formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Refers to the seller's act of guiding the buyer to engage in offline transactions, thereby harming the legitimate interests of the buyer and the platform. Examples of guiding offline transactions include but are not limited to:

（1）Publishing or promoting third-party products or information that may lead to transaction risks, such as publishing the names, logos, QR codes, hyperlinks, contact accounts, etc., of third-party websites or platforms such as social media, shopping guides, group buying, promotions, shopping platforms, etc., except for specific market agreements with certain groups.

（2）Inducing consumers to redirect to third-party websites or platforms through other means, such as using SMS notifications.

（3）Third-party information includes but is not limited to non-AliExpress shopping links, QR codes, third-party website information, non-IM contact information, bank account and other payment methods, non-AliExpress platform identifiers, etc.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

For the above-mentioned behavior of guiding offline transactions, the platform has the right to independently judge and impose the following penalties: Within one performance year (starting from the date of the first identified act of guiding offline transactions; the second performance year starts 365 days after the end of the first performance year, and so on):

（1）If identified as guiding offline transactions for the 1st to 5th times, deduct 1 point per occurrence from the store.

（2）If identified as guiding offline transactions for the 6th to 10th times, deduct 2 points per occurrence from the store.

（3）If identified as guiding offline transactions for the 11th time or more, deduct 3 points per occurrence from the store.
