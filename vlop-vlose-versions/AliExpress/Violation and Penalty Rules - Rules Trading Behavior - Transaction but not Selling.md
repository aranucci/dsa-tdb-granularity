AliExpress "Transaction but Not Selling" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

1、Scope
-------

We are committed to making AliExpress the preferred platform for overseas buyers in cross-border shopping. It is the primary obligation of sellers to strictly adhere to the promised price, delivery time, logistics services, and product quality when fulfilling orders placed by buyers. However, the platform has found that some sellers have caused a large number of products to be undelivered due to their own reasons, resulting in the closure of orders due to delivery timeouts. This has caused extremely negative experiences for buyers, leading to disputes and complaints. In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace and enhance the shopping experience for buyers on the platform, these rules are formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Definition of "Transaction but Not Selling": Refers to the behavior of sellers failing to ship the order after the buyer has made payment. The aforementioned delay refers to the failure to upload the tracking number before the seller's shipping timeout.

Based on the severity, the behavior of "Transaction but Not Selling" is categorized as general violation, severe violation, and particularly severe violation.

Examples of particularly severe violations of "Transaction but Not Selling" include but are not limited to the following situations, with particularly severe circumstances constituting particularly severe violations:

（1）A large number of "Transaction but Not Selling" orders or a significant proportion of "Transaction but Not Selling" orders in the seller's store within the past 7 days or 30 days.

（2）Intentional "Transaction but Not Selling" for improper purposes such as diverting traffic.

（3）Repeated occurrences of general violations of "Transaction but Not Selling".

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **Violation Category** | **Penalty Measures** |
| General violation of "Transaction but Not Selling" | 2 points/times |
| Severe violation of "Transaction but Not Selling" | 12 points/times |
| Particularly severe violation of "Transaction but Not Selling" | 48 points/times |
