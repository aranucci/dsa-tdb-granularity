1、Overview:
-----------

No information that contains or is suggestive of any prohibited or controlled items may be published on the platform. AliExpress has the right to punish any act in violation of these Rules in accordance with the AliExpress Index of Prohibited and Controlled Items.

Users shall not circumvent these Rules, other administrative provisions on prohibited goods published by the platform and the contents specified in the platform announcements by any means; otherwise, they may be subject to heavier penalty.

For principles of penalty and specific standards of point deduction, please refer to Penalties for Violations.

2、Index of Prohibited and Controlled Items
------------------------------------------

Platform users shall not post any products that are in violation of the legal or regulatory requirements of any countries, regions or jurisdictions on AliExpress.

AliExpress Index of Prohibited and Controlled Itemsis an index of certain prohibited or controlled items on the platform for users’ reference only. However, the completeness, timeliness and accuracy of such index is not guaranteed. The platform shall have the right to make any addition, deletion and modification to the following information in accordance with legal provisions, regulatory requirements and its own rules, while platform users shall have the obligation to ensure that the products posted by them do not violate the requirements of any jurisdiction.

Unless otherwise specified, the AliExpress Index of Prohibited and Controlled Items also apply to the publishing of information and the online transaction (“Online Transaction”) provided for in the Transaction Services Agreement.

AliExpress Index of Prohibited and Controlled Items also apply to the AliExpress sites in English and other languages.

For goods prohibited or controlled by the laws of certain countries and goods that are not suitable for cross-border sales due to their nature, please refer to the laws and regulations of such countries and the latest announcements of the platform.

If there is any discrepancy between the Chinese and English versions of any announcement or between the content of any announcement and other rules of AliExpress platforms, or if there is any other matter that is not detailed, AliExpress reserves the right of final explanation.

3、Penalties for Violations
--------------------------

The platform has the right to raise or mitigate the penalties depending on the severity of the violation, i.e., posting prohibited or controlled items, and the member’s acts.

Examples of malicious acts include but are not limited to evading system monitoring utilizing secretive means such as hiding, blocking or blurring product information, utilizing implicit description, or willfully through vague descriptions or deliberate mis-categorization; listing a large number of prohibited items at the same time; repeatedly publishing non-compliant information; maliciously testing the regulations. For malicious violations, the penalty will be raised depending on the severity. For example, the penalty will be doubled for a general violation, or in the case of serious violation, the relevant account will be closed.

Raised penalties for general violations: for a general violation that is identified as a malicious violation, the penalty will be raised (if the same violation occurs again, the penalty points will be doubled for the second time)

4\. The items listed above which are not suitable for trading on AliExpress are not exhaustive. AliExpress will adjust the scope of such items from time to time.
