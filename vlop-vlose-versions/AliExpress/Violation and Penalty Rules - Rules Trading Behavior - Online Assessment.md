AliExpress "Online Assessment" Rules

Latest revision date: May 6, 2022 (Beijing Time)

On April 19, 2022, AliExpress held its annual merchant summit and presented the three most important things for AliExpress in 2022. One of them is to "accelerate the improvement of global logistics fulfillment capabilities, incentivize merchants who fulfill orders quickly, and deliver goods within 72 hours with an online rate >90%." Fulfillment certainty is an important reason for consumers to continue repurchasing. In order to improve the consumer receiving experience and safeguard the rights and interests of more merchants with quality supply chains, one of the key areas for service improvement in 2022 is how to improve the 72-hour online rate and obtain more platform benefits.

This set of rules will introduce the definition of the online rate rule, assessment scope, incentive policies, control policies, and suggestions for improving shipping capabilities to help merchants improve their shipping abilities. These rules were officially released on April 28, 2022 (Beijing Time), and the public notice period for the rules is from April 28, 2022 to May 5, 2022. The rules will officially take effect from May 6, 2022.

1、Updates to the Online Rate Rules
----------------------------------

### （1）Description of the 24-hour/48-hour/72-hour/5-day online rate indicators

1) Numerator: The number of orders in the past 30 days that have been shipped within 24 hours/48 hours/72 hours/5 days after the logistics online time (minus the time for successful payment and risk control review).

2) Denominator: The number of orders that have been successfully paid (after risk control review) in the past 30 days, minus the number of canceled orders that were successfully canceled or expired.

3) The calculation is done based on Pacific Time, with a 7-day buffer for logistics online feedback delay. For example, the data on January 8 is the data from 30 days prior, starting from January 1.

4) All calculations are based on natural days, with the following exceptions:

**·** For orders shipped from mainland China, the effective time for calculation is Monday to Saturday. Sundays, public holidays, National Day holidays, and Spring Festival holidays are not included in the 24-hour/48-hour/72-hour/5-day period.

**·** For orders shipped from Hong Kong warehouses, the effective time for calculation is Monday to Friday. Saturdays, Sundays, public holidays, National Day holidays, and Spring Festival holidays are not included in the 24-hour/48-hour/72-hour/5-day period.

**·** For orders shipped from overseas warehouses, the effective time for calculation is Monday to Friday. Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays in respective countries are not included in the 24-hour/48-hour/72-hour/5-day period.

### （2）Assessment scope of online rate indicators

Taking into account merchants' operations and logistics collection and delivery situations, the following types of orders are temporarily not assessed for the online rate indicators, while all other orders will be included in the calculation of the 24-hour/48-hour/72-hour/5-day online rate indicators:

1) Customized attribute orders: Orders with a delivery period of more than 7 days are not assessed. A notice on the adjustment of customized attribute categories on AliExpress is expected to be released in May 2022. Please pay attention to platform notifications.

2) Links for price adjustment of special categories are not assessed.

3) Orders for special items (such as large items, liquid powders, etc.) that are shipped through custom shipping methods are not assessed.

4) For orders in overseas warehouse routes, orders generated using non-Cainiao official warehouse routes or non-platform direct trajectory data are not assessed.

5) Other situations are not assessed for a long time or temporarily based on platform discretion. For example, during recent global epidemic outbreaks, the platform will actively exempt merchants from responsibility based on the impact of the epidemic, and support for exemption indicators should be based on the latest announcements. For details, click here: 2022 Epidemic-related Shop Assessment Exemption Information Announcement Section, AliExpress and merchants working together during difficult times (constantly updated).

### （3）Querying and downloading details of the online rate indicators

Note: We recommend that you use the relevant pages to check the latest indicator rules after the rules come into effect on May 6. The details displayed on the product page should prevail.

1) You can log in to the seller's backend, and go to \[Store\] - \[My Store\] - \[Seller Service Score\], to check the online rate indicators for your store.

2) You can log in to the seller's backend, go to \[Business Insights\] - \[Logistics Overview\], to check the online rate indicators for your store.

3) You can log in to the seller's backend, go to \[Business Insights\] - \[Product Analysis\], to check the online rate indicators for your products.

### （4）FAQ

Q: What specific time does the logistics online time refer to in the online rate?

A: The logistics online time refers to the first information provided by the logistics provider. For online shipping, it is generally the time when the order is shipped from the warehouse, picked up by the delivery service provider, or delivered to the destination country. The actual shipping method should be used as the reference.

Q: When there are multiple logistics tracking numbers for the same transaction order, which logistics tracking number's time should be considered for the online time?

A: For a transaction order with one or more items, each item should have its corresponding shipping notification tracking number. All tracking numbers should have online logistics information within 24 hours/48 hours/72 hours/5 days in order for the order to be counted as an online order within that time frame. It is recommended to accurately fill in the tracking numbers corresponding to each item.

Q: For products belonging to a category with a set lead time of more than 7 days, is the assessment based on the actual lead time set for the product or the lead time set for the category the product belongs to?

A: The assessment is based on the actual lead time set for the product. If the lead time set for your actual product is ≤7 days, your orders will participate in the assessment as usual, and if they meet the criteria, you will enjoy the benefits.

2、Platform has a series of incentive policies and will implement them gradually for products and merchants with excellent delivery efficiency
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The 72-hour online rate indicator will be applied in search ranking, Gold and Silver Seller Access, marketing enrollment, and grouped order enrollment. Sellers with excellent online rates will have more opportunities to receive search recommendations, Gold and Silver Seller Access, and marketing enrollment opportunities.

（1）Positive impact on search ranking: Preferred warehouses & overseas warehouses > 24-hour online rate > 48-hour online rate > 72-hour online rate. Faster delivery will give your products a higher-ranking advantage on top of the natural search ranking.

（2）Grouped orders/cross-border expedited delivery, etc.: For products that meet the requirements of the 72-hour online rate, the platform will provide additional labeling, exposure, and exclusive marketing benefits. For details, click here to view the Introduction to Upgraded Grouped Orders and the Introduction to 72-hour expedited delivery.

（3）Gold and Silver Seller Access and Exclusion: Starting from July, the 72-hour online rate will be included in the assessment criteria for Gold and Silver Seller Access and Exclusion. The specific implementation details and timing will be announced at a later date. Please pay attention to platform notifications.

3、Platform benefits will be affected for products and merchants with slow delivery efficiency
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

（1）Platform marketing campaign access: The online rate will be included in the threshold for participating in platform marketing campaigns, and products that do not meet the criteria will not be able to participate in platform campaigns.

**·** Currently, the platform uses a 5-day online rate >80% for products in the store as the standard for participating in platform campaigns. For details, click here: \[AliExpress Rules\] - \[Marketing Rules\]. Products that do not meet the criteria will not be able to participate in platform campaigns.

**·** The standard for participating in marketing campaigns will gradually be upgraded to the 72-hour online rate. It is expected that important marketing fields on the platform will prioritize this starting in May. Please refer to the activity enrollment page for specific activities and criteria.

（2）Store service assessment: The indicator will be included in the merchant service score, and the platform will define the online rate assessment criteria in the merchant service score. If it is significantly lower than the industry standard, it may result in a lower search ranking.
