AliExpress "Inducing Early Confirmation of Receipt" Rules

Latest Revision Date: September 16, 2022, Beijing Time

1、Scope
-------

In order to maintain a healthy and orderly marketplace and prevent the practice of inducing early confirmation of receipt, and to enhance the user experience of members, these rules are formulated.

2、Definition and Manifestations
-------------------------------

Refers to the seller's act of inducing the buyer to confirm receipt before receiving the goods.

3、Violation Categories and Penalty Measures
-------------------------------------------

For the above-mentioned behavior of inducing early confirmation of receipt, the platform has the right to independently judge and impose the following penalties: Within one performance year (starting from the date of the first identified act of inducing early confirmation of receipt; the second performance year starts 365 days after the end of the first performance year, and so on):

（1）If identified as inducing early confirmation of receipt for the 1st to 5th times, deduct 1 point per occurrence from the store.

（2）If identified as inducing early confirmation of receipt for the 6th to 10th times, deduct 2 points per occurrence from the store.

（3）If identified as inducing early confirmation of receipt for the 11th time or more, deduct 3 points per occurrence from the store.
