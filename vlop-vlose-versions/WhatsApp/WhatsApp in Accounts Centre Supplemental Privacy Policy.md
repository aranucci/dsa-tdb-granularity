WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Supplemental Privacy Policy
=======================================================

Effective February 25, 2026 ([archived versions](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/accounts-centre-supplemental-privacy-policy-eea/revisions))

### About your information if you add your WhatsApp account to **[Accounts Centre](https://faq.whatsapp.com/849367647393371)**

You can choose to add your WhatsApp account to an **[Accounts Centre](https://faq.whatsapp.com/849367647393371)** with other [Meta Company Product](https://faq.whatsapp.com/532729362019677/) accounts. This enables data sharing for features that work across these products such as more personalised experiences, including content suggestions and ads (“**WhatsApp in Accounts Centre**”).

WhatsApp in Accounts Centre is entirely optional. You can remove your WhatsApp account from an Accounts Centre at any time in your WhatsApp or Accounts Centre Settings. [Learn more](https://faq.whatsapp.com/519792767655150) on how to remove your WhatsApp account from an Accounts Centre.

  
  

### What does this WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Supplemental Privacy Policy cover?

If you live in the [European Region](https://faq.whatsapp.com/1013614932324960/), this supplemental privacy policy (**“WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Privacy Policy”**) explains how we collect and use your information when you choose to add WhatsApp to an Accounts Centre with other accounts. When we say “WhatsApp”, “our”, “we” or “us”, we are referring to “WhatsApp Ireland Limited”.

This WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Privacy Policy supplements the [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea/?lang=en) and [any supplemental policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/) which applies to the use of all our Services including WhatsApp in Accounts Centre. Any capitalised terms used but not defined in this WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Privacy Policy are defined in the WhatsApp Privacy Policy and [any supplemental policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/). If there is any conflict between this WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Privacy Policy, the WhatsApp Privacy Policy and [any supplemental policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/), this WhatsApp in Accounts Centre Privacy Policy will apply to WhatsApp’s use of your information when you add WhatsApp to Accounts Centre.

WhatsApp in Accounts Centre allows you to add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre to enable features and experiences across your accounts for [Meta Company Products](https://faq.whatsapp.com/532729362019677) which you have added to the same Accounts Centre. For example: you may use your login information from one account to log into your other accounts, share content across products, and see personalised experiences such as personalised content and suggestions, including ads. For example, if you see ads depending on your settings in Status and Channels, information across the account(s) in your Accounts Centre is used to personalise these ads.

  
  

### Does this affect my WhatsApp messages?

When you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, this does not impact the privacy of your WhatsApp personal messages or calls, which continue to be end-to-end encrypted as described in the [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea/?lang=en) and won’t be shared with Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (“**Meta Ireland**”) or any other Meta company. The same goes for any information that we process to convey, or that provides information about, your messages or calls (such as who you communicate with, the time and date you make a call or send a message, and the duration of your calls) as well as your contact list.

  
  

### Information We Collect for WhatsApp in Accounts Centre

The [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea/?lang=en) describes information we collect on our Services. When you add WhatsApp to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, we also collect the information described below which is supplemental to the categories of [Information We Collect](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea#privacy-policy-information-we-collect) described in the [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea):

**Automatically Collected Information**

*   **Device and Connection Information**  
    If you choose to add your WhatsApp account to Accounts Centre, we will collect device and connection-specific information such as a unique ID created every time you use WhatsApp in Accounts Centre.
    
*   **Log and Troubleshooting Information**  
    We collect information about how WhatsApp in Accounts Centre is performing, like service-related, diagnostic and performance information. This information includes log files, timestamps, crosspost status activity and metrics, error events and other performance logs.
    

  

**Information Meta Companies Provide About You**

When you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre with other [Meta Company Product](https://faq.whatsapp.com/532729362019677/) accounts, we receive information from Meta Ireland to provide more personalised experiences such as personalised content and suggestions, including ads in Status and Channels (if we show you ads) and to enable you to use cross-app experiences, such as single sign on for your WhatsApp account, sharing a Facebook story to your WhatsApp Status or sharing your WhatsApp Status as a story on Instagram

This includes:

*   account information such as identifiers related to the Facebook or Instagram accounts you are linking to or cross-posting information to;
*   age information for example, to determine whether you are eligible to use WhatsApp in Accounts Centre and Meta Company Products generally (including WhatsApp);
*   user content such as a Facebook or Instagram story if you decide to share your story as a status on WhatsApp;
*   your activity and information you provide on Meta Products, such as content you create, view or interact with, how you interact with it and the time, frequency and duration of your activities on Meta Products; and
*   device and connection information.

  
  

### Information Shared by You and by WhatsApp with Other Meta Companies to enable You to use WhatsApp in Accounts Centre

When you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, we will share information with Meta Ireland:

*   [Information We Collect](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea/#privacy-policy-information-we-collect) (supplemented with information we collect from [any supplemental policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/) which is relevant to your use of WhatsApp). This includes:

*   [Account information](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea#privacy-policy-info-you-provide-your-account-information) such as your phone number and your WhatsApp profile picture (to e.g. display it on your Accounts Centre Settings) and age information (to e.g. determine whether you are eligible to use WhatsApp in Accounts Centre and Meta Company Products generally (including WhatsApp)).
*   [User Content](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea#privacy-policy-information-you-provide) such as your WhatsApp status, if you decide to share your status updates on Facebook or Instagram.
*   [Device and Connection Information](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea#privacy-policy-auto-collect-info-device-connection) such as your app version or your set language in WhatsApp and other information.
*   [Usage Information](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy-eea#privacy-policy-auto-collect-info-usage-information) on how you use our Services such as how many times you share your WhatsApp status on Facebook or Instagram.

When you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, Meta Ireland will use your information as described in the [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/) to do things like provide, personalise and improve its products and services, including to show you ads (if you see ads on [Meta Company Products](https://www.facebook.com/help/1561485474074139?helpref=faq_content)) and to promote safety, security and integrity. You can find out about how to exercise your rights when Meta Ireland is using your information for these purposes in the [What is our legal basis for processing your information, and what are your rights?](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/?section_id=7-WhatIsOurLegal) section of the Meta Privacy Policy. Meta Ireland’s own terms and privacy policy will govern your use of Meta services and products. To add WhatsApp to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, you must be above the digital age of consent in your country. The information Meta Ireland receives when you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre may include data that was collected when you were below the digital age of consent in your country.

  
  

### Joint processing by WhatsApp and Meta Ireland

When you add your WhatsApp account to an Accounts Centre with other Meta Company Product accounts, WhatsApp Ireland and Meta Ireland are joint controllers for the purpose of associating the accounts you have added to an Accounts Centre with each other (“Joint Processing”).

The Joint Processing is based on your consent (see below at [How and Why We Process Information and Our Legal Basis For Processing](#how-and-why-we-process-information-and-our-legal-basis-for-processing)) and you can withdraw your consent to processing by both controllers at any time by removing your WhatsApp account from the Accounts Centre.

WhatsApp and Meta Ireland have agreed that WhatsApp is responsible for enabling you to exercise your rights under the GDPR with regard to the Joint Processing (please see below at [How to Exercise your Data Subject Rights](#how-to-exercise-your-data-subject-rights)).
