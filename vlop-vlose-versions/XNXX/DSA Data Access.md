DSA Data Access
===============

In accordance with Article 40 of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065, the Digital Services Act (“DSA”) and Delegated Regulation (EU) 2025/2050 (“Delegated Regulation”), this page provides information for vetted researchers and regulatory authorities on how to request access to data held by XNXX for the sole purpose of conducting research that contributes to the detection, identification and understanding of systemic risks in the EU, as set out pursuant to Article 34(1) DSA, and to the assessment of the adequacy, efficiency and impacts of the risk mitigation measures pursuant to Article 35 DSA. The information below is published pursuant to Article 6 of the Delegated Regulation.  

Any personal data processed in connection with the DSA data access process are processed only to the extent proportionate and necessary for that purpose and in line with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (“GDPR”). Processing within the DSA Data Access Portal uses IT infrastructure located in the EEA.

When providing access to data under a reasoned request from the relevant Digital Services Coordinator (“DSC”), XNXX applies measures proportionate to sensitivity of the data, including secure processing environments and access controls.

Please note that the information on this page is indicative and does not guarantee availability of or access to the data. Any access to data requires a valid reasoned request issued by the relevant DSC.

**DSA Data Access Portal**

All data access applications and reasoned requests are managed via the official [DSA Data Access Portal](https://data-access.dsa.ec.europa.eu/home) (“Portal”) maintained by the European Commission. The Portal serves as the central digital point for information on the data access process and facilitates the information exchanges among applicant researchers, vetted researchers, data providers and DSCs. The Portal is not a data-access modality.

**Suggested Access Modalities**

In line with Article 6(4)(d) of the Delegated Regulation, XNXX suggests the following data access modalities for the data included in the Data Catalogue, which are adequate to the level of sensitivity of the different data assets. Please note that access modalities listed below are indicative only. The final access modalities are determined by the DSC in each reasoned request.

*   Aggregated export (S1): for low-sensitivity data (aggregated/anonymised).
*   Guarded API access (S2): for medium-sensitivity data (technical/organisational controls and rate limits).
*   Secure processing environment (S3): for highly sensitive data (account-based access, audit logs, no raw export)
