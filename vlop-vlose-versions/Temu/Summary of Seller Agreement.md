SUMMARY OF TEMU'S SELLER AGREEMENTS
===================================

Release date: Jan 22, 2026

This is a summary of Temu’s [Seller EU Services Agreement](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=10) ("**SSA**") including, to the extent applicable, any supplementary agreements and policies (the "**EU Seller Rules**"). Our EU Seller Rules, as published in the Temu EU [Seller Center](https://seller-eu.temu.com/) also form part of our agreement. These cover a variety of areas, including in relation to [sellers' conduct](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=38), [sellers' fulfilment obligations](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=31), [anti-fraudulent transactions procedures](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=35), [seller’s fraudulent/deceptive registration](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=45), [product safety and compliance requirements](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=36),[extended producer responsibility](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=50), [data protection](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=30), [VAT Calculation Methodology and our advertising services (if applicable)](https://seller-eu.temu.com/policy-page.html?type=22).

Together, all of these documents form the “**Agreement**” for the purposes of this summary.

This summary is provided for the convenience of European Economic Area (EEA) sellers. It is not a legal document. This summary does not contain all elements of the Agreement. Should there be any discrepancy or conflict between this summary and the terms of the Agreement, the Agreement will apply. We kindly encourage you to read and familiarise yourself with our full Agreement, including any applicable supplementary policies.

### 

1\. Overview

The Agreement is a binding contract between you and us, Whaleco Technology Limited, an Irish company. The Agreement is in respect of your access to and use of services provided by us (or one of our affiliates) through the Temu APP and the Temu.com website in the EU (together, the “**Temu Platform**”).

We may change the Agreement from time to time. When we do, we will inform you of these changes. Some changes will start applying immediately (e.g. where these changes are required by law) but most material changes will only start applying after 15 days. If you continue to access and use the Temu Platform after these changes, you will be deemed to have accepted them. If you object to any changes, you should stop any activity on the Temu Platform immediately and let us know of your decision to do so (see SSA Section 1.3).

General. ### 

3\. When and how can the Agreement end

The Agreement does not have a set duration. It explains how and when either you or us can terminate it and also describes in which circumstances we can limit or suspend your use of the Temu Platform including your use of the Advertising Services (see SSA Sections 13.6, 15.2 and 22 and Section 6 of the Advertising Terms).

### 

4\. How complaints or disputes are handled

Any complaint you may have about our services or the way we’ve treated you should firstly be submitted via our internal complaints handling process available at the Temu EU [Seller Center](https://seller-eu.temu.com/). You can find additional information about the process in SSA Section 23.1 and in the Seller Center.

If we are unable to resolve your complaint in this way, the Agreement explains what your options are, including:

*   Mediation: Any dispute between us can be referred to one of our preferred independent mediators and we will bear a reasonable proportion of the total costs. SSA Section 23.2 of the Agreement sets out further detail on how this process works.
    

*   Out of court dispute settlement: You have a right to choose a certified out-of-court dispute settlement body to resolve any dispute that relates to a decision by us to restrict your content or access to Temu on the basis of illegality or incompatibility with our terms (see SSA Section 23.3). The list of certified out-of-court dispute settlement bodies published by the European Commission can be found here: [https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/dsa-out-court-dispute-settlement.](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/dsa-out-court-dispute-settlement.)
    

You also always have the right to start legal proceedings in the Irish courts. The Agreement is governed by the laws of Ireland.
