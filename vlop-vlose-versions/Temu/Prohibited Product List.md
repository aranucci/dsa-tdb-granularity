Prohibited Product List
=======================

Last Updated: September 4, 2025

*   Live animals, animal products, and products that encourage, promote or facilitate animal cruelty
    

*   Plants, seeds and products from plants or seeds that are incompatible with applicable laws
    

*   Human body parts, remains, and mortuary products
    

*   Tobacco and products containing tobacco
    

*   Alcoholic beverages
    

*   Gambling and lottery products
    

*   Sexual services
    

*   Listings or products containing pornography content
    

*   Listings or products promoting non-consensual or paid sex acts or other illegal sexual themes
    

*   Listings or products containing any underage sexual content, such as child sexual abuse or exploitation, lolita or teen-themed pornography, underage dating, and pedophilia
    

*   Listings or products containing violent, terroristic, hateful, illegal, offensive or otherwise controversial materials or content
    

*   Listings or products promoting discrimination based on race, gender, religion, ethnicity, sexual orientation, or any other protected class
    

*   Listings or products promoting self-harm
    

*   Firearms, ammunition, explosives, weapons and controlled devices
    

*   Hazardous materials, such as fireworks, flammable chemicals, radioactive materials, radiation devices, toxic or hazardous chemicals, ozone-depleting substance, persistent organic pollutants, and other materials that may pose health, physical or environmental hazards and are prohibited
    

*   Drugs, drug paraphernalia, or products or services specifically offered or intended to be used to create, produce or grow drugs or drug ingredients
    

*   Products for military, police and other government agencies
    

*   Government papers and documents
    

*   Logos, seals, insignias, credentials, ID cards, badges, trade dress, or branding of accredited agencies or organizations
    

*   Counterfeit products
    

*   Products infringing on other’s valid and enforceable intellectual property rights
    

*   Unlicensed media
    

*   Privacy and technology violations
    

*   Misleading listings
    

*   False advertising
    

*   Virtual/digital goods, intangible items, or services
    

*   Cash, cash equivalents, gift cards and coupons
    

*   Counterfeit currency
    

*   Products that support illegal activities, such as locksmithing tools, illegal surveillance equipment, hacking equipment
    

*   Products that require a prescription or supervision or direction from a healthcare professional
    

*   Products with ultrasound technology intended for wrinkle removal and/or body contouring
    

*   Noninvasive blood glucose meters or monitoring products (in the U.S.)
    

*   Dangerous and unsafe items
    

*   Recalled items
    

*   Products prohibited by applicable laws, nationwide or regional
    

*   Products that do not comply with applicable laws and regulations regarding product safety, product documentation or certification, labeling, marketing and other requirements
