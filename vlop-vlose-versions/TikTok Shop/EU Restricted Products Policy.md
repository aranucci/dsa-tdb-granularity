3.4 Surprise-Based Products тАЛ

*   Sellers must include the following information clearly in their listing description:тАЛ

*   Visual image(s) and written descriptions showing the product(s) the customer may receive in the surprise-based product including the quantity, category, and description of products the customer may receive when purchasing the surprise-based product;тАЛ

*   Sellers must ensure that the listing description for the boxes only shows products that can actually be obtained by customers. Sellers must not show people obtaining products that in practice are rare to obtain;тАЛ

*   Details of all the variations (e.g. colours, tastes, flavours, scents, models) of the potential products the customer may receive;тАЛ

*   The quantity of products the customer will receive and the overall quantity of different potential items that can be included in the surprise-based product. The exact quantity should be fixed (in units, pieces, or fixed weight) and not presented as a range; andтАЛ

*   In case of mystery boxes or bundles, Sellers must highlight that the product selection is entirely random and that customers might receive duplicates either within the same order or across multiple orders.тАЛ

*   The product listing must also meet the following pricing requirements:тАЛ
