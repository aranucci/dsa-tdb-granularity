TikTok Shop EU Platform Abuse Policy

25/02/2025

For Seller

*   This TikTok Shop EU Platform Abuse Policy (hereafter referred to as "this Policy") lists Seller practices that constitute abuse of TikTok Shop's platform and services and are therefore prohibited on TikTok Shop.​

*   This Policy applies to Sellers only. ​

*   All references to TikTok Shop in this Policy are a reference to TikTok Shop EU.​

*   This Policy is not exhaustive, and TikTok Shop Sellers are solely responsible for ensuring that they do not engage in any practice that is prohibited under this Policy, and that they comply with applicable laws and regulations, and all [TikTok Shop EU Policies](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/home?identity=1&role=seller&content_id=7344097824802562&menu=policy), the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6488689480009504&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Sale](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2334721925220128&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Shop EU Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2225681119987488&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/terms-of-service/en), and the [TikTok Community Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/?cgversion=2023). ​

*   Sellers may wish to seek independent legal advice if they have questions about the laws and regulations concerning their practices.​

*   Our [TikTok Shop EU Policies](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/home?identity=1&role=seller&content_id=7344097824802562&menu=policy) are updated periodically. Please see the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6488689480009504&article_type=agreement&identity=1) for details of how we may make changes to our terms and policies and how we notify you.​

*   TikTok Shop reserves the right to take enforcement action as set out in the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Performance Evaluation Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6671917692716833&identity=1) and [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6488689480009504&article_type=agreement&identity=1) against Sellers who breach this Policy or in the case of suspicious activity in relation to a Seller's account. Sellers have the right to appeal in accordance with the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Performance Evaluation Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6671917692716833&identity=1) and [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6488689480009504&article_type=agreement&identity=1). TikTok Shop will then investigate the case and take corrective action where applicable. ​

2\. Definitions​

*   Fake Order refers to any order that was not made by a legitimate TikTok Shop customer or for a legitimate purpose (and is therefore contrary to the TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service and the TikTok Shop EU Policies and/or applicable laws and regulations).​

*   Platform Incentive Abuse refers to when a Seller misuses an incentive granted by TikTok Shop or abuses any of the services offered by TikTok Shop.​

*   Seller refers to a third-party trader on TikTok Shop.​

*   Unauthorised Access refers to when a person gains entry to a Seller account, computer network, system, application software, data or any other resource, without the permission of the owner of that resource, or another person who is authorised to access or grant access to that resource. ​

3\. Unauthorised Access​

*   Unauthorised Access is prohibited on TikTok Shop and includes any actions that maybe taken after gaining Unauthorised Access.​

*   In addition to enforcement action against Sellers who breach this Policy, TikTok Shop may take any action it deems necessary as a precaution when Unauthorised Access is suspected or to prevent further Unauthorised Access.​

4\. Fake Orders ​

*   Fake Orders are prohibited on TikTok Shop. ​

*   A Fake Order can be placed by the TikTok Shop Seller themselves or any individual connected to, acting on behalf of, or directly or indirectly instructed by the Seller. This may be for the purpose of increasing sales numbers or boosting shop performance (for example, to increase Shop Performance Scores, order numbers, or positive reviews). ​

*   TikTok Shop Sellers must not fulfill any orders that they know to be Fake Orders. ​

5\. Platform Incentive Abuse​

*   Platform Incentive Abuse is prohibited on TikTok Shop.​

*   Platform Incentive Abuse includes but is not limited to manipulating the Shop Performance Score, TikTok Shop orders, price discounts and/or customer reviews.​

6\. Privacy​

*   For more information about how TikTok Shop processes and shares your information, including for the purposes of enforcing this Policy, please see the [TikTok Partner Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/partner-privacy-policy/en) which does not form part of the contract with TikTok Shop.​
