TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy

**_Last updated_**_: January 15, 2026_

  

**Summary**
-----------

This TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy (“**TikTok Shop Creator** **Privacy Policy**”) sets out the way in which we collect, use, share, and otherwise process any personal information we collect from you, or that you provide to us, if you participate in the [TikTok Shop Affiliate Program](https://business.tiktokshop.com/uk/affiliate) as a creator (**“TikTok Shop Creator”**) who promotes products offered by TikTok Shop sellers (“**Sellers**”) . This TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy applies if you live outside of the US. For individuals in the United States, please refer to the [US TTS Creator Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/tiktok-shop-creator-privacy-policy-us).

This TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy incorporates and supplements the [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy). This means that, for example, personal information we collect via your participation in the TikTok Shop Affiliate Program as a creator may also be used for the purposes described in the [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy) and, to the extent applicable, information collected via the TikTok platform (“**Platform**”) can be processed as described in this TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy. For more information, please read the [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy) which also includes important information on your rights and how and where we store and protect your personal information. Any capitalised terms not defined in this TikTok Shop Creator Privacy Policy have the meaning given to them in the [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy).

**What information do we collect about you via TikTok Shop?**
-------------------------------------------------------------

When you participate in TikTok Shop Affiliate Program, we may collect the following information from you:

### **Information You Provide**

*   Your full name and date of birth;
*   Your phone number and email address;
*   Your billing and shipping addresses;
*   A copy or photo of your passport or national/government issued ID card or driving licence;
*   Your bank account information; and
*   Your tax information, such as your VAT and TIN (or place of birth in the absence of a TIN).

### **Automatically Collected Information**

We also collect the following information automatically when you participate in TikTok Shop Affiliate Program:

*   Products you have received as a sample and/or chosen to promote, and related information regarding sales and commission in relation to your promotion of such products, and performance metrics of the relevant videos and live-streams you create;
*   Your rating, rankings and aggregated information about your followers, such as aggregated demographic information;
*   Insights about your content, such as why it is performing well on the Platform; and
*   Sellers and agencies you collaborate with.

### **Information From Other Sources**

We may receive the information described in this Privacy Policy from other sources, such as:

*   payment providers and identity verification providers; and
*   Sellers and agencies you collaborate with.

**How do we use information collected about you via TikTok Shop?**
------------------------------------------------------------------

We process your information for the following purposes:

*   To check your eligibility to participate in the TikTok Shop Affiliate Program and facilitate the payment of commission to you.
*   To enable Sellers, their service providers (e.g. [TikTok Shop Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f19852dc7f3e028d380a2a)), [TikTok Shop Affiliate Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f198679495ef028184fa10) and [Creator Agency Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f1985acb677b0286e75491) to discover your profile, contact you to discuss potential projects or collaborations, collaborate with you, evaluate your performance, and so that you may receive commission.
*   To personalise and customise certain features and content, such as showing you products that we think may be of interest to you in TikTok Shop Creator Centre and analysing your content to enhance content accessibility.
*   To enable you to request and receive samples from Sellers to test their products, in advance of any potential collaboration or promotion of their products on TikTok, as well as to enable Sellers and their service providers (e.g. [TikTok Shop Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f19852dc7f3e028d380a2a#Back%20To%20Top)) to analyse and evaluate sample promotion.
*   To enable TikTok Shop Creators and Sellers to view other TikTok Shop Creators’ inspirational and successful content for the purposes of inspiring more creative content on the Platform.
*   To facilitate sales, promotion, and purchases of goods and services.
*   To comply with our legal obligations.

**Who do we share information collected about you (including information collected under the TikTok Privacy Policy) with?**
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### **Sellers, Agencies, Transaction Fulfilment Providers and Other Creators**

*   We share Information You Provide and Automatically Collected Information with Sellers, their service providers (e.g. [TikTok Shop Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f19852dc7f3e028d380a2a)), [TikTok Shop Affiliate Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f198679495ef028184fa10), and [Creator Agency Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f1985acb677b0286e75491) for the purposes of allowing them to discover TikTok Shop Creators in order to promote their products, contact, collaborate with and pay commission to them.
*   We share Information You Provide with Sellers and shipping providers when Sellers approve your sample requests, so that the product sample can be dispatched to you. When requesting a sample, please review the product and Seller location details provided on the Platform for information about where your information will be sent.
*   We share successful TikTok Shop Creators' content, as well as insights and certain performance data about that content, with all TikTok Shop Creators and Sellers for the purposes of inspiring better content promoting TikTok Shop products.

### **Service providers**

*   We share information you provide with third-party service providers such as payment providers and identity verification providers. This is so we can check your eligibility to participate in the TikTok Shop Affiliate Program and facilitate the payment of commission to you.

### **Legal Obligations and Rights**

We may access, preserve, and share the information described in “What Information We Collect” with law enforcement agencies, public authorities (for example, tax authorities), entities within our corporate group, researchers, copyright holders, or other third parties if we have good faith belief that it is necessary to:

*   Comply with applicable law (including tax information collection and tax reporting requirements), legal process or government requests, as consistent with internationally recognised standards,
*   Protect the rights, property, and safety of our users, copyright holders, and others, including to protect life or prevent imminent bodily harm. For example, we may provide information (such as your phone number) to law enforcement in the event of an emergency where someone’s life or safety is at risk,
*   Investigate potential violations of and enforce our Terms, Guidelines, or any other applicable terms, policies, or standards, or
*   Detect, investigate, prevent, or address misleading activity, copyright infringement, or other illegal activity.

To learn more about how we handle requests from law enforcement agencies and public authorities, see our [Law Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/law-enforcement).

#### **Data Transfers**

We share Information You Provide and Automatically Collected Information with Sellers and other third parties, as described in “Who do we share information collected about you (including information collected under [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)) with?”. When we transfer your information outside of the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, we ensure it benefits from an adequate level of data protection by relying on:

*   Adequacy decisions. These are decisions from the European Commission under Article 45 GDPR (or equivalent decisions under other laws) where they recognise that a country offers an adequate level of data protection. We transfer your information as described in “What information do we collect about you via TikTok Shop?” to some countries with adequacy decisions, such as the countries listed [here](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/transferee-countries/en);
*   Article 49. In the absence of an adequacy decision, we rely on Article 49(1)(b) and (c) GDPR to transfer Information You Provide and Automatically Collected Information to Sellers, their service providers (e.g. [TikTok Shop Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f19852dc7f3e028d380a2a#Back%20To%20Top)), [TikTok Shop Affiliate Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f198679495ef028184fa10), and [Creator Agency Partners](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/64f1985acb677b0286e75491) in countries without an adequacy decision when necessary to enable You and these third parties to connect and work together as part of [TikTok Shop Affiliate Program](https://business.tiktokshop.com/uk/affiliate).
*   Standard contractual clauses. The European Commission has approved contractual clauses under Article 46 of the GDPR that allows companies in the EEA to transfer data outside the EEA. These (and their approved equivalent for the UK and Switzerland) are called standard contractual clauses. We rely on standard contractual clauses to transfer Information You Provide and Automatically Collected Information to third parties in countries without an adequacy decision.

For more information on Our Global Operations and Data Transfers please see [TikTok Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy).
