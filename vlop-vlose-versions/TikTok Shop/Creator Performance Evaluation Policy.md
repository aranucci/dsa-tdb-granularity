TikTok Shop Creator Performance Evaluation Policy

12/12/2025

For Creator

1\. Overview ​

*   This TikTok Shop EU Creator Performance Evaluation Policy (hereafter referred to as "this Policy") details the enforcement action TikTok Shop will take against Creators for violating its TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies and how Creators can appeal against such enforcement action.​

*   TikTok Shop may take enforcement action as set out in this Policy, any other TikTok Shop EU Policy, and the [TikTok Shop Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1).​

*   This policy applies to Creators only. ​

*   All references to TikTok Shop in this Policy are a reference to TikTok Shop EU.​

*   This Policy is not exhaustive, and Creators are solely responsible for ensuring that they do not engage in any practice that is prohibited under this Policy, and that they comply with applicable laws and regulations, and all TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies.​

*   Creators may wish to seek independent legal advice if they have questions about the laws and regulations concerning their practices.​

*   Our TikTok Shop EU Policies are updated periodically. We will generally notify you in advance of any changes to this Policy. Please see the [TikTok Shop Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1) for details of how we may make changes to our terms and policies and how we notify you.​

2\. Definitions ​

Defined terms in this Policy have the following meaning:​

*   Associated Accounts refer to Seller accounts, other Creator accounts, or any other type of TikTok platform account which TikTok Shop determines based on objective criteria to have sufficient connections ​

*   Balance refers to any monies due, or that may become due, to the Creator in connection with their TikTok account (including but not limited to TikTok Shop commission fees). ​

*   Creator refers to a TikTok user who creates and promotes E-commerce Content.​

*   Creator E-commerce Permissions means permissions provided to certain Creators that enable their creation of E-commerce Content as defined in the [TikTok Shop EU Creator E-commerce Permissions Eligibility Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7680324121134880&identity=1), including but not limited to:​

*   Displaying products on their TikTok profile page;​

*   Advertising and promoting products in videos uploaded on TikTok; and/or​

*   Promoting products during TikTok livestreams.​

*   E-commerce Content refers to the creation of promotional images, videos, or other content, (including via short video, live streaming, or recorded content) to promote Sellers’ TikTok Shop product listings on the TikTok platform in the EU.​

*   Prohibited Content refers to E-Commerce Content that violates any of our TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies, including but not limited to the [TikTok Shop EU Content Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8154736403449633&identity=1) and the [TikTok Shop EU Giveaway and Purchase-based Incentives Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8425027070920481&identity=1).​

*   Seller refers to a third-party trader on TikTok Shop.​

*   TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies refers to all [TikTok Shop EU Policies](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/home?identity=1&role=seller&content_id=7344097824802562&menu=policy), the [TikTok Shop Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Shop EU Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2225681119987488&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/terms-of-service/en), and the [TikTok Community Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/?cgversion=2023).​

*   Payouts refer to funds payable to a Creator from TikTok for their activities in respect of TikTok Shop, including commissions earned from sales.​

*   Creator Health Rating (CHR) refers to a numerical score ranging from 0 to 1,000 that reflects the overall health of a Creator's account.​

3\. Enforcement Policy​

*   Creators are responsible for ensuring that their E-commerce Content complies with applicable laws and regulations, and that they comply with the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies.​

*   TikTok Shop may check the Creator's E-commerce Content for Prohibited Content to evaluate the Creator's performance and where necessary, to take enforcement action against violations of the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies by Creators.​

*   TikTok Shop monitors and records any violations of the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies by Creators.​

*   Based on the severity and the impact of the violation of the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies, TikTok Shop may take appropriate enforcement action against the Creator. TikTok Shop will determine the enforcement action in a diligent, non-discriminatory, and non-arbitrary manner.​

*   Depending on the violation(s) of the Creator, TikTok Shop may take multiple enforcement actions against the Creator.​

*   In the event that a Creator does not comply with an enforcement action that TikTok Shop has imposed, TikTok Shop may take additional enforcement action against the Creator.​

*   If TikTok Shop restricts, suspends or terminates the Creator's account, TikTok Shop will provide a prior warning and/or a statement of reasons where required by and in accordance with applicable law. ​

*   Details of the violation will be provided via the Creator Health Rating page of the Creator's TikTok App. Creators should regularly track their account health via the Creator Health Rating of their TikTok app and review any violation tickets that may have been issued to them.​

*   Depending on the severity of a Creator's violation(s) of the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies, TikTok Shop:​

*   May periodically clear violations from a Creator's account; and​

*   May or may not take previous violations (including those cleared from a Creator's account) into consideration in determining enforcement action against a Creator who has violated the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies repeatedly.​

4\. Associated Accounts Enforcement Actions ​

*   If TikTok Shop finds that a Creator account has Associated Account(s), and such accounts have violated the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies, we may take enforcement action against the Creator and the Associated Account(s). ​

*   The enforcement action(s) taken against the Associated Account(s) will depend on the severity of the violation. TikTok Shop will determine the severity of any enforcement action in a diligent, non-discriminatory, and non-arbitrary manner.​

5\. Enforcement Actions​

*   TikTok Shop may take the following non-exhaustive enforcement actions against Creators who violate our TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies:​

*   Removing product links from E-commerce Content; ​

*   Removing E-Commerce Content; ​

*   The restriction or suspension of Creator E-Commerce Permissions. For more details, please refer to the [TikTok Shop EU Creator E-Commerce Permissions Eligibility Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7680324121134880&identity=1).​

*   Issuing a formal warning to the Creator; ​

*   Restricting the Creator's access to the Affiliate program, campaigns, promotions, and other services offered by TikTok Shop to the Creator;​

*   Temporarily or permanently suspending the Creator's access to the Affiliate program, campaigns, promotions and other services offered by TikTok Shop to the Creator;​

*   Temporarily or permanently suspending the Creator's access to TikTok Shop;​

*   Terminating the Creator's account and the [TikTok Shop Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1);​

*   Commencing legal action independently or in conjunction with affected third parties against the Creator; ​

*   Reporting the Creator to the relevant regulatory authorities, payment processors, or other relevant third parties and providing any necessary information on the Creator's account to the extent permitted by applicable laws and regulations; and​

*   Restricting the number of short videos a Creator may publish within a defined time period during an enforcement validity period (for example, limiting publication to a maximum of five (5) short videos within each rolling three-day period).​

*   TikTok Shop may take any additional necessary corrective actions and controls, in accordance with our TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies.​

5.1 Creator Health Rating (CHR)​

*   The Creator Health Rating (CHR) system is established to evaluate the overall health of a creator's TikTok Shop account. The CHR is a numerical score, ranging from 0 to 1,000, that reflects a creator's compliance with TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies and their performance on the platform.​

*   All new Creators begin with a CHR of 200 points. Creators who maintain a higher CHR benefit from continued access to TikTok Shop features and opportunities. Where a CHR falls to specified thresholds, enforcement actions may be taken against the creator's account.​

*   Individual violations of TikTok Shop policies or applicable laws will continue to be enforced directly (for example, through content removal). The CHR system adds an additional layer of account-level enforcement, ensuring that repeated or serious non-compliance results in progressively stricter consequences.​

*   CHR deductions may occur for violations of TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies, including but not limited to the TikTok Shop EU Content Policy. Deductions depend on the severity and frequency of the violation and will reset after ninety (90) days. TikTok Shop may also impose immediate enforcement actions for serious or high-risk conduct such as violations related to compliance and safety risks, which may include permanent removal of e-commerce permissions, regardless of a creator's current CHR.​

*   Creators may improve their CHR through compliant activities, such as creating eligible content or completing policy quizzes. Points earned through these activities are valid for ninety (90) days.​

*   Where a CHR drops to 150, 100, 50, or 0 points, milestone enforcement actions will apply. Enforcement actions may include, but are not limited to, temporary suspensions of creator features, ineligibility for campaigns, and permanent removal of e-commerce permissions. Warnings may also be issued as a creator approaches the next milestone threshold.​

6\. Withholding a Creator's Payouts​

*   In addition to the enforcement actions set out above, if TikTok Shop reasonably suspects that a Creator has violated the TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies in any of the following ways, any available Payouts may be temporarily or permanently withheld (in full or in part) from the Creator, including but not limited to: ​

*   promoting unoriginal content;​

*   simulating live events that are pre-recorded or AI-generated;​

*   generating artificial orders; and​

*   generating misleading traffic by using Buyer accounts to automatically join livestreams.​

*   See TikTok Shop EU Content Policy for details on these violations.​

*   TikTok Shop will notify the Creator when a restriction is placed on the Creator's ability to request a Payout due to such suspected violation(s) of the Creator Terms.​

*   The amount of any Creator Payouts that will be withheld will correspond to the amounts earned through such suspected violation(s) of our TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies.​

*   Notwithstanding Clause 7 of this policy, TikTok Shop can withhold the corresponding amount from the Creator's available Payouts:​

*   For 90 calendar days from the date of TikTok Shop's notification to the Creator that the corresponding Payout amount will be withheld; or ​

*   Until completion of TikTok Shop's investigation into the Creator's suspected violation(s), if this occurs before the 90-day period has expired; or​

*   Pending an appeal filed by the Creator if the handling of the appeal exceeds the 90-day period.​

*   A Creator may file an appeal against this enforcement action within 180 days from the date of TikTok Shop's notification to the Creator that the corresponding Payouts amount will be withheld. ​

*   If the appeal is successful or if the investigation revealed no violation(s), TikTok Shop will release the Payouts amount withheld to the Creator.​

*   If the Creator does not file an appeal and the investigation confirms the suspected violation(s) or if the appeal is not successful, the withheld Payouts will not be released to the Creator.​

7\. Appeal​

*   If a Creator believes that enforcement action was taken against them in error, they may appeal through the appeal functionalities provided by TikTok Shop in the enforcement action notification. Please refer to the TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use for information on other available legal rights. Please refer to the TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use for information on other available legal rights.​

*   TikTok Shop will then investigate the case and take corrective action where applicable. ​

*   Please note that submitting an appeal does not guarantee a successful outcome for the Creator.​

7.1 Appeal Procedure​

*   Creators can raise a maximum of one appeal against one enforcement action.​

*   The appeal must be raised within 180 days from the day on which the Creator is notified about the enforcement action. Appeals are not available after this time.​

*   Any appeals raised after the timelines stipulated above will not constitute a valid appeal and will not be reviewed by TikTok Shop. ​

*   Further information on how to submit an appeal and other redress possibilities is set out [here](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8468565700658976&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1).​

7.2 Appeal results​

*   TikTok Shop will evaluate a Creator's appeal and will aim to inform the Creator of the outcome or make a request for further information without undue delay. Creators will also be informed of the possibility of out-of-court dispute settlement and other available possibilities for redress.​

*   The decision made by TikTok Shop regarding the Creator's appeal shall be notified to the Creator via their Creator Health Rating page without undue delay.​

*   If the Creator's appeal is successful, TikTok Shop will remove any penalties issued and/or restore any withdrawn privileges to/from the Creator. ​

*   All decisions made by TikTok Shop are at TikTok Shop's sole discretion and are non-discriminatory, diligent and non-arbitrary. Regardless of the out-of-court dispute settlement process and mediation procedures offered by TikTok Shop, a decision is final after the appeal is rejected.​
