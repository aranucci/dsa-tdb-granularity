Definitions ‚Äã

Defined terms in this Policy have the following meaning:‚Äã

*   Carrier refers to a logistics service provider. ‚Äã

*   Default Shipping Type refers to the Shipping Type that will automatically be offered to a TikTok Shop customer if the Seller does not make an alternative selection. ‚Äã

*   Hazardous Goods refer to goods that can be dangerous when transported and are therefore subject to regulatory requirements. Dangerous goods include, but are not limited to, products which are explosive, flammable or toxic. Dangerous goods can often still be shipped despite being considered dangerous, but only in limited amounts or in specific packaging that reduces the risk of damage being caused.‚Äã

*   [Late Dispatch Rate (LDR)](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2634717434693409&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1) is the percentage of all packages over a period of 7 calendar days that take more than 2 working days to be updated to "Shipped" status in the Seller Center after the order placement. This is only applicable for shipping type "Ship by TikTok".‚Äã

*   Local to Local refers to a Package being shipped from and delivered to the customer within the same EU country. ‚Äã

*   Package refers to the parcel containing a customer's TikTok Shop order. ‚Äã

*   Seller refers to a third-party trader on TikTok Shop.‚Äã

*   [Seller Fault Cancellation Rate (SFCR)](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7822609539843872&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1) is the percentage of all orders cancelled by the seller prior to the orders being Shipped (e.g. Out of Stock etc). The SFCR is calculated on a weekly basis.‚Äã

*   Shipping Type refers to the method by which a TikTok Shop order is delivered to a TikTok Shop customer. ‚Äã

*   [Valid Tracking Rate (VTR)](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8360088285497110&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1) is the percentage of all Packages that are "Shipped by Seller" using a tracked shipping method by approved couriers. The assessment period used to calculate VTR can be found in [Order Volume Limits (OVLs).](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8410400744179478&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1)‚Äã

*   The [On Time Delivery Rate (OTDR)](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=5822032341403415&role=1&course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1) is the percentage of all packages (over a period of 7 calendar days) that are delivered within the delivery timeframes set out in section 5 of this Policy. OTDR is calculated at the Seller account level from the preceding 7 days and is updated every Monday on a weekly basis. For example, if a seller has five shops across the EU, the OTDR reflects the delivery percentage of all the packages delivered in the preceding 7 days across the 5 shops in the EU.‚Äã

*   Working Day refers to any day from Monday to Friday, excluding national public holidays.‚Äã

*   Balance refers to any monies due, or that may become due, to the Seller in connection with their TikTok Shop Account (including but not limited to TikTok Shop sales proceeds).‚Äã

*   Late Delivery Fee refers to the fixed fee that TikTok Shop charges a seller whose delivery performance consistently falls below the delivery timeframes set out in section 5 of this Policy. A late delivery triggering Late Delivery Fee(s) occurs when an order is delivered after the applicable delivery timeframes set out in [here](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=1&knowledge_id=8138868415792918&from=policy). TikTok Shop will determine Late Delivery Fee eligibility based on the Seller's OTDR or LDR, calculated as described in this Policy. Certain orders will be exempt from Late Delivery Fee(s), as set out in [here](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=1&knowledge_id=8138868415792918&from=policy).‚Äã

5.2 Confirm the Order ‚Äã

*   Within two Working Day from the receipt of the order, the Seller must provide confirmation of the order to the customer. ‚Äã

*   If the Seller is unable to fulfil an order, the Seller must follow the procedure described in our [TikTok Shop EU Customer Order Cancellation, Return and Refund Policy](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2163458502330144&identity=1) to cancel the customer's order. ‚Äã

*   If the order is not confirmed, packed and/or a shipping label has not been created within the Agreed Timeframe (as set out in the table above), Customers will have an extended period of time to cancel the order until the Seller Center Order Status has been updated to Awaiting Collection or Awaiting Shipment. Please see the [TikTok Shop EU Customer Order Cancellation, Return and Refund Policy](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2163458502330144&identity=1) for more information.‚Äã

5.3 Pack the Order‚Äã

*   The Seller must pack the order within two Working Day from receipt of the order.‚Äã

*   The Seller must pack the order in a manner that minimises the risk of damage during shipping and ensures compliance with the [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6416044148655905&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Shop EU Policies](https://seller-es.tiktok.com/university/home?identity=1&content_id=7733890979890945&role=seller), and applicable laws and regulations. This includes, but is not limited to:‚Äã

*   Complying with the Carriers' latest terms, conditions, and policies and any special requirements imposed by the Carrier, which can be found in the Seller Academy;‚Äã

*   Not enclosing any messages soliciting positive customer reviews or indicating that customers may initiate returns in exchange for other benefits;‚Äã

*   Ensuring that no unsolicited marketing or promotional materials are included with the order;‚Äã

*   Ensuring that the Package does not contain products that the customer did not order; and‚Äã

*   Before shipping any products that require special handling, Sellers must review the Carrier's applicable terms and conditions, to confirm whether the Carrier can ship the goods. A link to the Carriers terms and conditions can be found in the Seller Academy.‚Äã

5.4 Create and Affix a Shipping Label‚Äã

*   The Seller must create and affix a shipping label to the Package within two Working Day from receipt of the order. ‚Äã

*   The Seller must securely affix a shipping label to the Package. The shipping label must include the following information, as well as any other information required by applicable law: ‚Äã

*   The customer‚Äôs name; ‚Äã

*   The customer's full postal address (including zip/postcode); and‚Äã

*   The Seller‚Äôs full name and address.‚Äã

*   Shipping labels must meet all of the following requirements:‚Äã

*   A shipping label must not include Seller contact information, other than the Seller's contact details as included on their TikTok Shop; and‚Äã

*   A shipping label must not cover or remove other barcodes to render them unscannable.‚Äã

*   Each Package must have the correct label attached to it. Carriers may dispose of mislabelled Packages or re-label the Package at the Seller's expense. ‚Äã

*   Sellers are not responsible for the accuracy of delivery information that has been provided by the customer. If any issue arises in relation to delivery of the product due to incorrect or incomplete information provided by the customer, the customer may bear any additional delivery cost, where agreed with the customer. ‚Äã

*   The Seller may liaise with the customer to obtain the correct information and aid in delivery to the correct address and, if required, raise an appeal to TikTok Shop's Customer Service in the Seller Center for assistance in resolving these issues.‚Äã

5.5 Adding and Verifying packing information ‚Äã

*   For Shipping Type Ship by Seller, Sellers bear the responsibility of using only approved Carriers and providing accurate tracking information. In instances where the Carrier, or partner Carrier, fails to deliver timely and/or to provide accurate information regarding the Package's actual status and tracking information, these gaps will be regarded as a Seller failure to adhere to the dispatch SLAs and will negatively impact Valid Tracking Rate set out in this Policy.‚Äã

*   Repeated failures to provide accurate tracking information (intentional or otherwise) may result in temporary restrictions for Sellers in addition to order cancellations by TikTok Shop and in accordance with our TikTok Shop EU Seller Performance Evaluation Policy. We may place platform restrictions, such as Order Volume Limits (OVLs), on shops with low Valid Tracking Rate.‚Äã

*   For the Shipping Type Ship by TikTok, Sellers do not have to add the tracking information for each Package and will not have any Valid Tracking Rate penalties as TikTok Shop automatically generates tracking information.‚Äã

5.6 Hand Package to the Carrier‚Äã

*   When handing over a Package to a Carrier, the Seller is responsible for: ‚Äã

*   Ensuring the Package is either:‚Äã

*   available for pick up at the time and location agreed with the Carrier; or‚Äã

*   delivered to the Carrier, drop-off parcel shop or sorting hub belonging to the Carrier. ‚Äã

*   Ensuring the product and its packaging are intact before the Package is dispatched. TikTok Shop recommends that Sellers take photos of Packages before dispatch for record-keeping purposes or in case of any future dispute. ‚Äã

*   For shipping type Ship by TikTok, Sellers must ensure all packages are handed over to Carrier within 2 Working Days from the customer placing the order. If a Seller does not adhere to this timeframe, this affects their Late Dispatch Rate. In accordance with our TikTok Shop EU Seller Performance Evaluation Policy, we may place platform restrictions, such as order volume limits, on shops with high Late Dispatch Rates.‚Äã

*   Ensuring that the order status is up to date.‚Äã

5.7 Monitor Package progress until delivery‚Äã

5.7.1 Final Order Status‚Äã

*   If a Seller uses the Ship by Seller Shipping Type, TikTok Shop may use logistics data provided by a third-party Carrier to check and verify the order status.‚Äã

*   Sellers using "Ship by Seller" shipping type must ensure that all orders are "Delivered" within the defined Delivery SLA from the date of the Seller's receipt of an order. *   If a Seller uses the Ship by TikTok Shipping Type, the Carrier will update the Seller Center order status to "Delivered" after obtaining the recipient's signature or other acknowledgement of receipt. ‚Äã

5.7.2 Lost or Delayed Delivery‚Äã

*   A Package will be deemed "lost" if it has not been received by a customer and the Carrier has indicated that the Package is considered to be lost. ‚Äã

*   If the Carrier fails to deliver a Package within the estimated delivery time provided to the customer, the Package will be deemed "delayed". ‚Äã

*   If a Package becomes lost or if it becomes damaged during the packaging or shipping process, or if its shipping is delayed, TikTok Shop will notify the customer and grant them the option to cancel the order and get a full refund or, if possible, to receive a new product at no additional cost. ‚Äã
