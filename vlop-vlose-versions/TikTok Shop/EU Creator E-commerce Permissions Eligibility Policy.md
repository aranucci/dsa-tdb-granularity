TikTok Shop EU Creator E-commerce Permissions Eligibility Policy

09/10/2025

For Creator

1\. Overview​

*   This TikTok Shop EU Creator E-commerce Permissions Eligibility Policy (referred to as "this Policy") outlines the requirements TikTok creators must meet to qualify for TikTok Shop Creator E-commerce Permissions, and when those Creator E-commerce Permissions may be restricted or suspended. ​

*   This Policy applies to Creators only.​

*   All references to TikTok Shop in this Policy are a reference to TikTok Shop EU.​

*   This Policy is not exhaustive, and Creators are solely responsible for ensuring that they do not create and/or promote any E-commerce Content that is prohibited under this Policy, and that they comply with applicable laws and regulations, and the [TikTok Shop EU Content Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8154736403449633), the [TikTok Shop EU Creator Performance Evaluation Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=2&knowledge_id=8145389292357409&from=policy), the [TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Shop EU Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=2225681119987488&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/terms-of-service/en) and the [TikTok Community Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/?cgversion=2023). ​

*   Our TikTok Shop policies are updated periodically. Please see the [TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1) for details of how we may make changes to our terms and policies, and how we notify you. ​

*   TikTok Shop reserves the right to take enforcement action as set out in the [TikTok Shop EU Creator Performance Evaluation Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=2&knowledge_id=8145389292357409&from=policy) and [TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1) against Creators who breach this Policy. Please refer to the TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use for information on how we resolve any disputes with Creators. Creators have the right to appeal in accordance with the [TikTok Shop EU Creator Performance Evaluation Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?identity=1&role=2&knowledge_id=8145389292357409&from=policy) and [TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1). TikTok Shop will then investigate the case and take corrective action where applicable. ​

2\. Definitions​

Defined terms in this Policy have the following meaning:​

*   A "Creator" is a TikTok user who creates and promotes E-commerce Content. ​

*   "Creator E-commerce Permissions" means permissions provided to certain Creators, in accordance with this Policy, to enable their creation of E-commerce Content, including but not limited to:​

*   Displaying products on their TikTok profile page;​

*   Advertising and promoting products in videos uploaded on TikTok; and/or​

*   Promoting products during TikTok livestreams.​

*   "E-commerce Content" means the promotional images, videos, short videos, live streaming, recorded content, or other content on TikTok that promotes sellers’ TikTok Shop product listings to TikTok users. ​

*   "Member State" refers to a country that is part of the European Union.​

*   "Prohibited Content" means all content that contains or promotes any behaviour or product that violates our [TikTok Shop EU Policies](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/home?identity=1&role=seller&content_id=7344097824802562&menu=policy), the [TikTok Shop Creator Terms of Use](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7058140927952672&article_type=agreement&identity=1), the [TikTok Terms of Service](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/terms-of-service/en), and the [TikTok Community Guidelines](https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/?cgversion=2023), as well as any applicable laws and regulations. This is further defined in the TikTok Shop EU Content Policy. ​

3\. Requirements to Obtain and Retain Creator E-Commerce Permissions​

*   To obtain and retain Creator E-commerce Permissions, Creators must continually comply with this Policy and meet the following requirements:​

*   Access their TikTok account from the same Member State where it was registered;​

*   For example if you registered your TikTok account in Germany, you could obtain Creator E-commerce Permissions to promote E-commerce Content for TikTok Shop Germany.​

*   Maintain the required number of followers in the Member State for which they are applying;​

*   Ireland: 1,500 followers.​

*   Be at least 18 years old;​

*   Use a TikTok account that is not identified as a Government, Politician and Political Party Accounts ("GPPPA") as defined in this [article](https://support.tiktok.com/en/using-tiktok/growing-your-audience/government-politician-and-political-party-accounts); and​

*   Not have previously had Creator E-commerce Permissions that were revoked by TikTok Shop. This includes previous accounts of the Creator. ​

*   Creators should refer to the article [How to Check Your E-Commerce Permissions Status](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8490968224352032&identity=1) for details on the application process for Creator E-commerce Permissions. ​

4\. Restriction or Suspension of Creator E-commerce Permissions​

*   TikTok Shop may restrict or suspend the Creator's E-commerce Permissions, if the Creator violates the TikTok Shop EU Policies, the TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use, the TikTok Shop EU Terms of Use, the TikTok Terms of Service and the TikTok Community Guidelines.​

*   Creators will be notified by TikTok Shop in the event their Creator E-commerce Permissions are restricted or suspended via a violation ticket issued in the "Account Health" section in the "Creator Health Rating" page of their TikTok app and in accordance with the TikTok Shop EU Creator Terms of Use.​
