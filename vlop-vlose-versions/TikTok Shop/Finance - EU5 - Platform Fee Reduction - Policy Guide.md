Definitions​

*   Defined terms in this Policy have the following meanings:​

*   Commission Fee refers to the platform fee paid by Sellers to TikTok Shop on all GMV generated by sales.​

*   Commission Fee rate refers to the specific percentage of GMV charged as Commission Fee.​

*   Mission refers to action-based incentives available to eligible Sellers in the Seller Centre. Successfully meeting the terms of the Mission will unlock Seller incentives​

*   Seller refers to a third-party trader on TikTok Shop UK based in the EU.​

*   GMV refers to Gross Merchandise Value or a Seller's gross sales revenue from sales made on TikTok Shop. ​

​

Commission Rates​

TikTok Shop charges a fee from Sellers on all transactions on the platform. The Commission Fee is a specific percentage of an order's total amount charged. ​

*   In the EU, TikTok Shop charges 5% commission on settled orders (before incentive reductions are applied, as outlined in section 4).​

*   This fee is inclusive of applicable taxes, including but not limited to Value Added Tax (VAT).​

The Commission Fee owed will be calculated as: ​

Commission Fee = ((Net Sales + Customer-paid Shipping Fee + Platform Discount) - (Net Sales Refund + Platform Discount Refund)) \* Commission Fee rate​

​

Customer Returns​

If the entire order is returned, the full Commission Fee will be refunded to the Seller. If part of the order is refunded, only the Commission Fee charged on the refunded item(s) will be refunded to the Seller.​

Commission Reduction Incentives​

TikTok Shop offers EU sellers an opportunity to reduce Commission Fee rates on the platform: ​

*   New Sellers onboarding onto TikTok Shop for the first time may benefit from the following Commission Fee rate:​

*   Reduction Rate: From 5% to 2% commission if they onboard after 00:00 on 31st March 2025​

*   This is valid for up to 90 days if Sellers complete a Seller Mission (located in the Seller Centre) to upload a minimum of 5 active products to any one of their EU active shops within 60 days of onboarding​

*   The reduced commission will apply to all EU shops, regardless of where the products are listed.​

​

Mission Overview​

Seller Missions to unlock Commission Fee rate reductions will soon be visible in the Growth Centre section of the Seller Centre for Sellers to accept. The mission will be online for 60 days after the seller onboards.​

Sellers must upload a minimum of 5 active products to any one of their EU active shops to activate the commission reduction. ​

*   The reduced commission will apply to all EU shops, regardless of where the products are listed.​

*   Example: A French seller listing 5 products in their French shop will automatically qualify for the reduced commission in their German shop, even if the German shop is set up later.​

⚠️ Important note: Sellers who fail to complete the mission within the 60-day period will lose eligibility for commission reduction. ​

​

Start and End Dates:​

*   The 90-day commission reduction period begins on the day the seller completes the mission in their first EU shop.​

*   If a seller sets up additional EU shops later (.i.e in France, Germany, Italy...), the commission reduction will still end on the same 90-day cutoff date.​

*   Example: If a seller activates the commission reduction on April 1, it will end on July 1 for all Europe shops, even if the German shop is set up on May 1.​

​

How to Qualify:​

To qualify for the 2% commission incentive, sellers must complete a simple mission:​

1.  Accept the Mission: Log in to the Seller Centre and navigate to the Missions section: [Seller Centre > Growth > My Missions](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/challenges/missions?mission_tab=common&shop_region=IE)​

2.  Upload Products: List a minimum of 5 active products that pass TikTok Shop’s quality checks in one of the EU shops (e.g. France)​

3.  Claim the Reward: Upon mission completion, the Seller must return to the Seller Centre to view the 'Congratulations' message in order to claim their commission reduction. This message will automatically be triggered upon the successful completion of the Mission as described above.​

*   90 days of commission reduction incentives will begin the same day the reward has been claimed and will take effect from the time the 'Congratulations' message has first been seen.​

Important Notes:​

*   The commission reduction will remain valid for 90 days after the reward is claimed.​

*   The policy is universal and applies to all EU active markets, replacing previous market-specific missions (e.g., French mission, German mission).​

FAQ​

1.  How do I qualify if I am already onboarded?​

*   Existing merchants who meet the mission requirements (e.g., having 5 approved products) can claim the reward immediately after accepting the mission.​

2.  What happens if I don’t complete the mission on time?​

*   Merchants who fail to complete the mission within the 60-day period will lose eligibility for commission reduction.​

3.  Where can I check my reward status?​

*   Reward statuses and validity periods are displayed under the My Rewards section in the Growth Centre.​

4.  What if my products don’t pass moderation?​

*   Products that do not pass TikTok Shop’s quality checks can be reuploaded or adjusted to meet platform requirements.​
