2\. Definitions ‚Äã

*   "Seller" refers to any individual or corporation that sells items on TikTok Shop. This includes all cross-border sellers. ‚Äã

*   "Content" refers to all forms of media that can be uploaded by creators/sellers to TikTok Shop. This includes all available formats, such as e-commerce shop windows, videos, and live streams. ‚Äã

*   "Unsafe products" refer to any products banned by local law or regulation, recalled by national authorities and manufacturers, and/or flagged as unsafe are not allowed for sale on TikTok Shop. Unsafe Products are considered illegal and, therefore, are prohibited.‚Äã

*   "Recall" shall mean any measure aimed at achieving the return of an unsafe product that has already been supplied or made available to consumers by the producer or distributor;‚Äã

*   "Withdrawal" shall mean any measure aimed at preventing the distribution, display and offer of an unsafe product to the consumer.‚Äã

3.  Product Safety and Conformity‚Äã

*   Products must conform with all applicable safety standards of the European market. Confirmation of conformity should be supplied and/or displayed in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.‚Äã

*   When viewing and purchasing product listings, customers expect the information contained within the listing to be fair, accurate, verifiable and honest. TikTok Shop customers should be confident that the product they purchase is safe and compliant with the safety standards required by national authorities. Sellers must comply with official standards and product conformity guidelines, as well as all applicable laws and regulations when creating product listings. ‚Äã

*   For products sold in the EU, Sellers must ensure the following information is provided in every product listing:‚Äã

*   Manufacturer Information: Products are labelled with the correct manufacturer's contact details and, if relevant, the importer's information. This information must be visible on the physical product itself, as well as in each product listing.‚Äã

*   Responsible Person: For most non-food products, EU Responsible Person requirements will apply. Sellers are required to display the relevant Responsible Person details on each product listing.‚Äã

*   Safety Warnings: All applicable warnings and safety-related information related to the product must be clearly displayed in each product listing. ‚Äã

*   More information on the requirements of the EU General Product Safety Regulation (GPSR) can be found in the [General Product Safety Regulation](https://commission.europa.eu/business-economy-euro/product-safety-and-requirements/product-safety/general-product-safety-regulation_en).‚Äã
