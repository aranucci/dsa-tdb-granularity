What are imported reviews?​

Imported reviews on TikTok Shop are provided by third party customer review platforms. These third party platforms collect reviews from other websites offering products also sold on TikTok Shop.​

TikTok Shop strives to ensure imported reviews are genuine, lawful, and not misleading. Reviews from third parties are only imported when processes are in place to ensure this.​

As imported reviews are collected and provided by a third party, TikTok Shop cannot always guarantee that:​

*   the review was written by a genuine customer of the relevant product or that the review is objective, honest, and not offensive;​

*   the review was written by someone not paid or given an incentive to write a positive review; and​

*   there are no variations between the product you’re buying and the product that was reviewed.​

If we become aware that any imported reviews shown on TikTok Shop do not meet these standards, we will remove the relevant imported reviews.​

Find out more about customer reviews on TikTok Shop generally (including information on how to report a customer review) in [TikTok Shop UK/EU Customer Review Policy](https://sf19-draftcdn-sg.ibytedtos.com/obj/ies-hotsoon-draft-sg/magellan_ecommerce/tts_eu_pdp_review_guidelines_en-IE.html).​

Cad iad athbhreithnithe iompórtáilte?​

Cuireann custaiméirí tríú páirtí athbhreithnithe allmhairithe ardáin athbhreithnithe ar fáil ar TikTok Shop. Bailíonn na hardáin tríú páirtí seo athbhreithnithe ó suíomhanna gréasáin eile a thairgeann táirgí a dhíoltar ar TikTok Shop freisin.​

Déanann TikTok Shop a dhícheall a chinntiú go bhfuil athbhreithnithe iompórtáiltear fíor, dleathach, agus nach bhfuil siad míthreorach. Ní iompórtáiltear athbhreithnithe ó thríú páirtithe ach amháin nuair a bhíonn próisis i bhfeidhm chun é sin a chinntiú.​

De réir mar a bhailíonn agus a sholáthraíonn tríú páirtí athbhreithnithe iompórtáilte, ní féidir TikTok Shop a ráthú i gcónaí:​

*   go raibh an t-athbhreithniú scríofa ag fíorchustaiméir den táirge ábhartha nó go bhfuil an t-athbhreithniú oibiachtúil, macánta, agus nach bhfuil sé maslach;​

*   gur scríobh duine nár íocadh an t-athbhreithniú nó gur tugadh dreasacht dó/di athbhreithniú dearfach a scríobh; agus​

*   nach bhfuil aon athruithe idir an táirge atá á cheannach agat agus an táirge a ndearnadh athbhreithniú air.​

Má thagann muid ar an eolas nach gcomhlíonann aon athbhreithnithe iompórtáilte a thaispeántar ar TikTok Shop na caighdeáin seo, bainfimid na hathbhreithnithe allmhairithe ábhartha.​

Faigh tuilleadh eolais faoi athbhreithnithe custaiméirí ar TikTok Shop go ginearálta (faisnéis maidir le conas athbhreithniú custaiméara a thuairisciú san áireamh) i [TikTok Shop RA/AE Polasaí Athbhreithniú Custaiméara](https://sf19-draftcdn-sg.ibytedtos.com/obj/ies-hotsoon-draft-sg/magellan_ecommerce/tts_eu_pdp_review_guidelines_en-IE.html).​
