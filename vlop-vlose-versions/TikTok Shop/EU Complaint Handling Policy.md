Complaints for business users of TikTok Shop ​

TikTok Shop takes complaints from its business users seriously. This page explains how you can get in touch with TikTok Shop if you have a complaint about something that affects you as a business user operating as a seller, partner or creator on TikTok Shop to offer goods or services to consumers in the UK or EU. ​

Raising a complaint​

You can raise a complaint about TikTok Shop in the following ways:​

1.  If you are seller, you can raise a ticket (or use the instant message chat function by clicking on the headphones icon) in the Help Centre located in the TikTok Shop Seller Centre.​

2.  If you are TikTok Shop partner, you can raise a ticket in the [TikTok Shop Partner Centre](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/contact-us/create). ​

3.  If you are a TikTok Shop creator, you can raise a ticket in the Creator Centre or via the 'Live Chat' function.​

You can also raise a complaint via our dedicated Platform to Business complaints form [here](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/report/P2B).​

Raising a complaint is free of charge.​

What can you raise a complaint about​

You can raise a complaint via these routes about:​

1.  Any technical issue relating directly to the provision of TikTok Shop and that affects you.​

2.  Any measures taken by TikTok Shop, or the behaviour of TikTok Shop, that affect you (please note: you should raise a complaint about content or account violations for which an appeal route is made available); or​

3.  Any breach of the Platform to Business Regulations (EU) 2019/1150 by TikTok Shop that affects you.​

Some of the contact routes above may allow you to raise complaints about other issues. For example, an order fulfilment issue on TikTok Shop. ​

What happens when you raise a complaint​

When you raise a complaint we will:​

1.  Acknowledge your complaint;​

2.  Consider the issues raised and follow-up with you if needed to adequately address the issue raised.​

3.  Handle the complaint transparently and in a manner which is proportionate to its importance and complexity.​

4.  Treat equivalent situations equally;​

5.  Deal with your complaint swiftly and effectively, taking account of the importance and complexity of the issue raised; and​

6.  Communicate the outcome of our assessment to you in an individualised manner and within a reasonable timeframe.​

Gearáin d’úsáideoirí gnó TikTok Shop ​

Glacann TikTok Shop gearáin óna úsáideoirí gnó dáiríre. Míníonn an leathanach seo conas is féidir leat dul i dteagmháil le TikTok Shop má tá gearán agat faoi rud éigin a théann i bhfeidhm ort mar úsáideoir gnó ag feidhmiú mar dhíoltóir, comhpháirtí nó cruthaitheoir ar TikTok Shop chun earraí nó seirbhísí a thairiscint do thomhaltóirí sa RA nó san AE.​

Gearán a dhéanamh​

Is féidir leat gearán a dhéanamh faoi TikTok Shop ar na bealaí seo a leanas:​

1.  Más díoltóir tú, is féidir leat ticéad a ardú (nó feidhm chomhrá na teachtaireachta meandracha a úsáid trí chliceáil ar dheilbhín na gcluasán) san Ionad Cabhrach atá suite in [Ionad Díoltóra Siopa TikTok](http://seller.eu.tiktok.com/contact-us?lang=en-GB&region=IE).​

2.  Más comhpháirtí TikTok Shop tú, is féidir leat ticéad a ardú in [Ionad Comhpháirtithe Siopa TikTok](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/contact-us/create).​

3.  Más cruthaitheoir Siopa TikTok thú, is féidir leat ticéad a ardú san Ionad Cruthaitheoir nó tríd an bhfeidhm ‘Comhrá Beo’.​

Is féidir leat gearán a dhéanamh freisin trínár bhfoirm ghearán tiomnaithe Ardán do Ghnó [anseo](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/report/P2B).​

Tá déanamh gearán saor in aisce.​

Céard is féidir leat gearán a dhéanamh faoi?​

Is féidir leat gearán a dhéanamh trí na bealaí seo faoi:​

1.  Aon cheist theicniúil a bhaineann go díreach le soláthar TikTok Shop agus a théann i bhfeidhm ort.​

2.  Aon bhearta a ghlac TikTok Shop, nó iompar TikTok Shop, a théann i bhfeidhm ort (tabhair faoi deara le do thoil: ba cheart duit gearán a dhéanamh faoi sháruithe ar ábhar nó ar chuntas a gcuirtear bealach achomhairc ar fáil ina leith); nó​

3.  Aon sárú ar na Rialacháin Ardán go Gnó (AE) 2019/1150 ag TikTok Shop a théann i bhfeidhm ort.​

D’fhéadfadh cuid de na bealaí teagmhála thuas deis a thabhairt duit gearáin a dhéanamh faoi cheisteanna eile. Mar shampla, ceist um chomhlíonadh ordaithe ar TikTok Shop. ​

Cad a tharlaíonn nuair a dhéanann tú gearán​

Nuair a dhéanann tú gearán déanfaimid:​

1.  Do ghearán a admháil;​

2.  Smaoineamh ar na ceisteanna a rinneadh agus obair leantach a dhéanamh leat más gá chun aghaidh a thabhairt go cuí ar an gceist a rinneadh.​

3.  An gearán a láimhseáil go trédhearcach agus ar bhealach atá comhréireach lena thábhacht agus lena chastaíocht.​

4.  Cásanna coibhéiseacha a chaitheamh go cothrom;​

5.  Déileáil go tapa agus go héifeachtach le do ghearán, agus tábhacht agus castacht na ceiste a rinneadh á gcur san áireamh; agus​

6.  Toradh ár measúnachta a chur in iúl duit ar bhealach aonair agus laistigh de thréimhse réasúnta ama.​
