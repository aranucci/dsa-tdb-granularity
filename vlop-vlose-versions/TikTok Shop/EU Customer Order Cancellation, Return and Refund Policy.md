2\. Definitions​

Defined terms in this Policy have the following meaning:​

*   Carrier refers to a logistics service provider.​

*   Change of Mind Return refers to the enhanced timeframe in which Customers may change their mind. All Customers purchasing from Sellers on TikTok Shop have the right to change their mind, cancel their order and/or return the product within 30 days from the date the Customer received the product. products are eligible for this type of return, except for those as set out in Section 6.3.2 of this Policy. See Section 6.1 for more details.​

*   Customer refers to an individual who has purchased a product from a Seller on TikTok Shop.​

*   Package refers to the parcel containing the Customer's TikTok Shop order.​

*   Product Issue refers to an issue with a product caused by the product's non-compliance with the contract of sale or with requirements set under applicable EU and national laws, for example, objective conformity requirements set under the Sale of Goods Directive 2019/771 (in each case, as updated, amended and superseded from time to time). ​

*   Returnless Refund refers to a scenario where a Seller accepts a Customer's refund request without requiring them to return the relevant product. The two types of Returnless Refund are as follows, the availability of which depends on the value of the product being returned: ​

*   Automatic Returnless Refund; or​

*   Seller-managed Returnless Refund.​

*   Seller refers to a third-party trader on TikTok Shop.​

*   Shipping Type refers to the method by which a TikTok Shop order is delivered to a TikTok Shop Customer.​

*   Ship by TikTok refers to a Shipping Type where Sellers use TikTok Shop's logistics services to fulfil orders, when available. ​

*   Ship by Seller refers to a Shipping Type where Sellers choose their own logistics service to fulfil orders. This option is only available to certain Sellers meeting objective criteria, as further explained in the TikTok Shop EU Customer Order Shipping Policy.​

4\. Customer Legal Rights​

4.1. Cancellations, returns, refunds and other remedies​

*   Customers have certain rights to cancel orders, return products, receive refunds, and/or exercise remedies. These rights apply once an order has been confirmed by the Seller and continue to apply after the Customer has received their order, as described further below. ​

*   Sellers are solely responsible for understanding and complying with their legal obligations in relation to a Customer's rights to cancel orders, return products, receive refunds and other remedies.​

*   The process for dealing with cancellations, refunds and returns depends on the Shipping Type used by Sellers.​

*   Section 5 of this Policy applies to orders which have not yet had their order status in the Seller Center updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection". ​

*   Section 6 of this Policy applies to orders which have had their order status in the Seller Center updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection" or "Shipped".​

5\. Cancellation ​

5.1. General Principles ​

*   The general principles for cancellation of a TikTok Shop order that has not had the Seller Center order status updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection" are as follows:​

*   Once payment is made, Customers can cancel an order before the Seller Center order status is updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection".​

*   Sellers and Customers can communicate with each other before a cancellation, in line with the [](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8264827860829984)[TikTok Shop EU Customer Service Policy for Sellers](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8264827860829984). If the Seller believes that the Customer is abusing the cancellation process, they can raise a appeal with our customer service team in the seller center.​

*   Sellers must not refuse or fail to process cancellation requests in a way that violates a Customer's legal rights and this Policy.​

5.2. Cancellation by The Customer​

*   A Customer's request for cancellation of a TikTok Shop order will be processed automatically for:​

*   Ship by TikTok orders;​

*   Ship By Seller orders, where the cancellation request was made within 1 hour of the Customer placing the order; and​

*   Ship By Seller orders, where the cancellation request was made more than 1 Working Day after the late dispatch deadline.​

*   For Ship By Seller orders, where the cancellation request was not made within 1 hour of the Customer placing the order, requests for cancellations must be processed by Sellers. In this scenario:​

*   Sellers must respond to cancellation requests within 1 Working Day. If they fail to do so, the cancellation request will be automatically processed; and​

*   Sellers must not refuse or fail to process cancellation requests in a way that violates a Customer's legal rights.​

5.3. Cancellation by The Seller ​

*   For order cancellations requested by Sellers prior to the Seller Center order status being updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection", the following applies:​

*   Sellers may only cancel TikTok Shop orders in accordance with our [TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6488689480009504&article_type=agreement&identity=1).​

*   Before a Seller cancels an order due to: (i) failure by the Customer to make any due payment; or (ii) the Customer not providing the Seller with information that is necessary to ship the order, the Seller must first notify the Customer and provide them with a reasonable opportunity to make payment or provide the necessary information (as applicable).​

*   Subject to applicable law, the Seller may cancel an order before it has been dispatched if the Seller’s stock has suffered unforeseen stock damage that occurred after the order was placed but before the order has been confirmed which means the item is unsellable.​

*   Notice of all cancellations must be provided promptly to the Customer via in-app notifications and via e-mail. All notices must be in the Customer's local language and must clearly explain the reason why their order was cancelled. ​

*   Sellers must not request that a Customer cancels their order due to the Seller's stock deficiencies or issues. ​

*   Sellers should be aware that cancelling an order may lead to negative feedback from Customers. ​

*   TikTok Shop may investigate a Seller and take enforcement action in accordance with the TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service in the following circumstances:​

*   Detection of a high frequency of order cancellations; ​

*   TikTok Shop's suspicion of any violation by the Seller of our TikTok Shop EU Terms and Policies; or​

*   TikTok Shop's suspicion of any fraudulent behaviour by the Seller.​

*   The Customer will be automatically refunded for the amount that the Customer has paid for the product and delivery fees.​

5.4. Cancellation by TikTok Shop​

*   \- Notwithstanding that TikTok Shop is not a party to the contract for sale of products sold on TikTok Shop, Sellers agree that TikTok Shop may cancel an order in accordance with the TikTok Shop EU Seller Terms of Service.​

6\. Returns, Refunds and Remedies After Shipping​

*   This section applies to orders which have had their status in the Seller Center updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection" or "Shipped". ​

6.1. Returns, and Refunds and Other Aftersale Remedies​

*   Customers may raise a return or refund request via TikTok Shop after the status of their order is updated to "To Ship - Awaiting Collection".​

*   Customers have a statutory right to change their mind, cancel their order and or/return their product within 14 days after the day the Customer receives the product. This is the Customer's statutory cancellation right.​

*   However, TikTok Shop Customers are given an enhanced timeframe in which they may change their mind: all Customers purchasing from Sellers on TikTok Shop have the right to change their mind, cancel their order and/or return the product within 30 days from the date the Customer received the product. These are Change of Mind Returns.​

*   Customers may submit complaints to the Seller. The Customer may also request support by clicking on the relevant order in the app. ​

*   A Customer may be entitled to additional legal remedies if they have received a product with Product Issues. It is the Seller’s responsibility to honour such rights in accordance with applicable law and regulations. ​

6.2. Handling Returns and Refunds ​

*   If a Customer files a return request, Sellers must follow the requirements below.​

*   Unless stated otherwise, the requirements in this section apply to both Change of Mind Returns and refunds for Product Issues. ​

6.2.1. Refund/Return Requests​

*   When a return request is raised by a Customer, and it falls within the automatic approval rules set out in this Policy, the Customer will immediately be able to ship the product back to the Seller.​

*   TikTok Shop will automatically approve all customer return requests, except for the return requests relating to the following products:​

*   Personalised products or products that are made to the Customer's specification (such as tailor-made clothing, monogrammed products, etc); ​

*   Sealed products which are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons because they have been unsealed after delivery; ​

*   Products which, after delivery, become mixed with other products and cannot be separated; ​

*   Audio, video or computer software products where the seal has been broken; ​

*   Newspapers, journals and magazines; and​

*   Products which may deteriorate or expire rapidly and therefore are not suitable for return (such as flowers or food).​

*   The Seller must respond to the Customer's return request within 2 Working Days after the Customer has submitted the request, if the return request has not automatically been approved.​

*   The approval of a return request does not result in the automatic approval of a Customer's refund. ​

*   Refunds for return requests will not be processed until the customer provides proof of the return, or when the Seller receives the product (whichever is earlier), unless the product is eligible for a Returnless Refund from TikTok Shop or the Seller in accordance with Section 6.2.5 or a refund following a Customer drop-off return in accordance with Section 6.2.4 of this Policy. ​

*   TikTok Shop reserves the right to automatically process all Customer return/refund requests, except where the product is not eligible for a return. ​

*   If a product is not eligible for a return, Sellers must manually review all Customer return/refund requests within 2 Working Days of submission by the Customer. If a Seller does not approve or reject the request promptly, the request will be automatically processed by TikTok Shop. ​

*   Sellers must not reject Customer requests without justification or where a Customer has a legal right to a refund/return.​

6.2.2. Return Basic Principles​

The following general principles apply to all returns:​

*   Customers must return their product within 14 days from the day on which the return request is made to the Seller. If the Customer fails to send their product back or enter the return tracking information within 14 days of making the return request, the return request may be cancelled.​

*   Once a Customer's return shipment tracking information is available, the Seller does not need to confirm that they have received the return shipment information via the Seller Center. ​

*   If the Seller takes no action in the Seller Center 2 calendar days after the return has been received according to the shipment tracking information or 14 calendar days after the Customer's return shipment information becomes available, TikTok Shop will automatically refund the Customer. ​

*   If the return has been lost and is not delivered back to the Seller within 14 calendar days after the Customer's return shipment information becomes available, Sellers can raise a compensation request with TikTok Shop via the 'Help Center' page of the Seller Center.​

*   Unless agreed otherwise with a Customer, all refunds must be made to the same payment method used by the Customer to place the order.​

*   If a Customer accidentally sends back the incorrect product, the Customer may get in touch with the Seller. There is no guarantee to the Customer, from the Seller or TikTok Shop, that any incorrect products, or any additional products mistakenly returned with the correct product, will be found or returned. The Seller does not have to compensate Customers for such products. ​

6.2.3. Shipping Costs of Product Returns​

*   If a Product Issue is the cause of the Customer refund/return request, Sellers must bear the cost of the return shipping.​

*   If the Carrier is the cause of the refund/return request (e.g. If the is Package damaged in transit), Sellers should follow the process set out in Section 6.5 of this Policy.​

*   For Change of Mind Returns, the cost of return shipping is covered either by TikTok Shop if the return is arranged by TikTok Shop or by the Seller if the return is arranged by the Seller. ​

6.2.4. Customer Drop-off Returns​

*   Customers can return products at TikTok Shop's designated drop-off points, unless they are considered heavy or bulky products. TikTok Shop will assess and may approve the refund request after Customers drop off their products at a designated drop-off point. ​

*   The Seller does not then need to approve the refund request after receiving the returned product. ​

*   Sellers can contact TikTok Shop via the Seller Center if there are issues with the returned item's condition.​

6.2.5. Returnless Refunds​

*   The following general principles apply to Seller-managed Returnless Refunds:​

*   Sellers are solely responsible for all Seller-managed Returnless Refunds, including setting the rules for when a Seller-managed Returnless Refund is available, and paying refunds to Customers. Sellers are solely responsible for any errors or mistakes in relation to Seller-managed Returnless Refunds. ​

*   A Sellers' use of the Seller-managed Returnless Refund feature is entirely at their own risk and the feature must only be used in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.​

*   Any cost incurred as a result of a Seller-managed Returnless Refund will be fully borne by the Seller and not covered in any way by TikTok Shop.​

*   Sellers can allow Returnless Refunds by selecting the "Auto-handle resolutions" button under the "Manage Returns Tab" in the Seller Center.​

*   Sellers can select their preferred options for 'Product Category', 'Return Reason' or 'Refund Range' when setting their rules for Seller-managed Returnless Refunds. Sellers can set multiple rules to cover different types of return/refund requests.​

*   When a Customer's return/refund request complies with the Returnless Refund rules set by the Seller, it will trigger a Returnless Refund for the relevant product.​

*   If a product recall is issued for a product for which a Returnless Refund was approved, Sellers may be required to comply with the relevant provisions included in any Product Recall Notice​

6.3. Customers' Change of Mind Return and Refund​

6.3.1. General Principles for Change of Mind Returns​

*   The following general principles apply to Change of Mind Returns:​

*   Customers are allowed to initiate a Change of Mind Return from the moment they receive their order confirmation.​

*   TikTok Shop will not automatically issue refunds for Change of Mind Returns. The Seller must receive the returned product and/or the Customer must provide proof that the return has been dispatched to the Seller in order to receive a refund, unless the product is eligible for a Returnless Refund. ​

*   If the Package is lost or damaged after a Change of Mind Return has been initiated and before the Customer has received their order, the Customer's Change of Mind Return request will automatically be cancelled and the Seller will not need to review it. TikTok Shop will refund the Customer if Ship by TikTok was used and the Seller will refund the Customer if Ship by Seller was used, once the Carrier has marked the Package as lost or damaged.​

*   If the order status is "Delivered", the Customer has 30 calendar days from the time they received the order to initiate a Change of Mind Return. The Seller can provide a longer window for a Change of Mind return if they wish.​

6.3.2. Eligible Products for Change of Mind Refunds​

*   All products sold on TikTok Shop are eligible for a Change of Mind Return, except for the following products:​

*   Personalised products or products that are made to your specification (such as tailor-made clothing, monogrammed products, etc);​

*   Sealed products which are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons because the seal has been broken (for example cosmetics, personal health care products, feminine hygiene products and, intimate apparel such as underwear; ​

*   Products which, after delivery, become inseparably mixed (due to their nature) with other products (for example, if one beauty product is mixed with anothe;​

*   Audio, video or computer software products where the seal has been broken;​

*   Products which may deteriorate or expire rapidly and therefore are not suitable for return ; and​

*   Newspapers, journals and magazines.​

*   Where a product is not eligible for a Change of Mind Return, any decision to issue a refund is at the Seller's discretion, except when the product is eligible for a Returnless Refund.​

*   For the avoidance of doubt, the above products may still be cancelled and/or returned by the Customer, if they are considered to have Product Issues as described in Section 6.4.​

6.3.3. Refunds for Change of Mind Returns ​

*   For Change of Mind Returns, the Seller must refund the Customer the original product price that the Customer paid for the product. Customers must not be required to pay any fees for a Change of Mind Return. ​

*   If the value of the returned product is reduced as a result of any handling by the Customer beyond what is necessary to establish the nature, characteristics and functioning of the product (in other words, the sort of handling that might reasonably be allowed in a physical shop), the Seller may deduct an amount (equal to the reduction in value) from the refund, up to the full purchase price of the product. ​

*   Depending on when the Customer initiates the order cancellation, return or refund request, the Seller may also need to refund the Customer the amount the Customer paid for shipping the product, as follows:​

*   If the request was initiated within 14 days of delivery of the product, the Seller must refund the Customer the shipping costs unless the Customer opted for a delivery option that was more expensive than the standard delivery option. In such a situation, the Seller is only required to refund the Customer the cost of standard delivery. ​

*   If the request was initiated 15 days or later after delivery of the product, the Seller does not need to refund the shipping costs to the Customer.​

6.4. Product Issues​

*   A Customer can submit a return and/or refund request if a product suffers Product Issues.​

*   If the Customer has a valid complaint under applicable consumer law, the Seller must approve the Customer's request to return the defective product and at the Customer's choice either: ​

*   Refund the price paid for the product (including any delivery costs); ​

*   Repair (free of charge) the defective product; or​

*   Replace (free of charge) the defective product with a new product of equivalent or better functionality or specification.​

*   Under applicable laws and regulations, there may be additional remedies available to Customers for products with Product Issues after the cancellation period, or beyond any extended returns benefit offered by the Seller. The Seller must honour these consumer rights under applicable consumer law.​

*   As included under Section 6.2.3, Sellers must bear the cost of the return shipping and cannot charge Customers for the costs of returning products with Product Issues.​

6.4.1. Inconsistent or Incorrect Products​

*   \- Customers can raise a return and/or refund request for products they receive that are wrong or physically different to what the Customer ordered. Customers may be required to attach supporting materials when submitting such requests. ​

*   Products that will be considered physically different include but are not limited to the following: ​

*   Products that are not as described in the Seller's product listing;​

*   Products that are counterfeit; and​

*   Products of the wrong specification, such as the wrong color, shape, technical features and brand. ​

*   If the Customer has a valid complaint under applicable consumer law, the Seller must approve the Customer's request to return the inconsistent or incorrect product and comply with the Customer's choice.​

6.4.2. Defective Products​

*   Customers can raise a return and/or refund request for products that are damaged, faulty or defective upon delivery. Customers may be required to attach supporting materials when submitting such requests.​

*   Products that will be considered defective include but are not limited to the following: ​

*   Products that are damaged or broken;​

*   Products that are not of satisfactory quality or performance (including due to faulty design or product safety concerns); ​

*   Products that are not fit for the purpose for which they were sold to the Customer; ​

*   Products that are not compliant with EU laws, and in particular (but without limitation) the conformity requirements set under the EU Sale of Goods Directive; and​

*   Any other Product Issues that negatively affect the Customer's product use, safety, or security. ​

*   In the case of further escalation by the Customer, the Customer may request that TikTok Shop adjudicates an aftersale dispute, as outlined in the [TikTok Shop EU Aftersale Dispute Escalation Policy.](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8264827860551456) ​

*   Where a dispute arises regarding a return request caused by a defective product, it is the Seller's responsibility to:​

*   Prepare and submit any relevant supporting documents to demonstrate the product's compliance with the sales contract; and​

*   Prepare and submit all required proof of the product meeting safety/compliance standards, including but not limited to product authenticity documents and conditions of shipping. ​

*   If the Customer has a valid complaint under applicable consumer law, the Seller must approve the Customer's request to return the defective product and comply with the Customer's choice.​

6.5. Logistics Issues​

*   For Ship by TikTok Shipping Types, TikTok Shop Customer Service will endeavour to resolve aftersales requests received from Customers relating to order fulfilment on behalf of Sellers. Customer requests relating to logistics issues may include: lost, tampered, misrouted, delayed or damaged Packages. ​

6.5.1. Handling of Logistics Issues for Ship by Seller​

*   In case of Ship by Seller, the Seller must follow our [TikTok Shop EU Customer Order Shipping Policy](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6671917692765985) [](https://seller-ie.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6671917692765985) and arrange the timely shipment of an order. ​

*   At a minimum, where the Customer has a valid complaint under applicable law in relation to the delivery of their order, the Seller shall at the Customer's choice either: ​

*   Refund the Customer (including any delivery costs); or​

*   Ship a replacement product to the Customer at the Seller's cost.​

*   If a Seller believes the Customer is abusing the return and/or refund request processes on TikTok Shop, they can raise a appeal in the Seller Center. ​

6.5.2. Carrier-caused Logistics Issues for Ship by TikTok​

*   There may be situations where logistics issues are caused by the Carrier. If the Seller believes that this is the case, they can begin a claim against the Carrier by raising an appeal in the Seller Center. TikTok Shop will investigate and review whether the claim should be approved. It is the Seller's responsibility to provide any required supporting materials.​

*   If the Seller's claim is approved, they will receive any compensation they are entitled to within 5 Working Days (notwithstanding any exceptional circumstances). ​

6.6. Delayed Delivery​

*   An order is considered to be delayed 48 hours after the estimated delivery date as shown on the order confirmation message. ​

*   If delivery of the Package is delayed, the Seller will endeavour to deliver the order within an additional reasonable period of time. If the Seller fails to deliver the product within this additional period of time, the Customer may be entitled to cancel the order and obtain a refund.​

*   Investigations may be required to determine if and when an order will be received, and to determine who is responsible for a refund where applicable.​

*   If an investigation is required regarding delayed delivery, Sellers may be required to submit the following to TikTok Shop: ​

*   Evidence that the Package had been handed over to the correct Carrier. ​

*   Details of the Carrier and shipment, such as the dispatch date, time, and location.​

*   Details about the product, such as the quantity, condition when packaged, and any attached Package seals.​

*   TikTok Shop reserves the right to request any additional documentation from the Seller that TikTok Shop deems relevant to support its investigations. However, for Ship by TikTok, Sellers may not be required to provide evidence and TikTok Shop may contact Carriers on behalf of Sellers.​
