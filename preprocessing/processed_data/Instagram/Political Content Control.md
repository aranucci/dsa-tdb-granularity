About Political Content Control on Instagram

We may recommend political content from accounts people don’t already follow based on personalized signals. If you want to see more or less political content, you can change your Political Content Control setting at any time. This control identifies whether content is political in nature but does not make determinations about political opinion.

Political content includes content that mentions:

Governments

Elections

Social topics
Change your Political Content Control setting

Open the Instagram app.

Tap ![]( **Profile** or your profile picture in the bottom right to go to your profile.

Tap ![more options]( **Menu** in the top right.

Tap **Content preferences**, then tap **Political content**.

Select your preferred political content control setting.

To see less political or social topics in your suggested content, tap **See less**.

To keep political or social topics in your suggested content the same, tap **Standard**.

To see more political or social topics in your suggested content, tap **See more**.

You can also learn how to [see fewer ads about social issues, elections or politics]( while on Instagram. Learn more about [how Meta addresses civic content]( on the Transparency Center.
