**About TikTok LIVE**

TikTok LIVE allows viewers and creators to interact in real time. As a creator, you may have access to LIVE features such as effects, [multi-guest hosting]( [moderation]( [LIVE Gifts]( and other settings. As a viewer, you can engage with your favorite creators' LIVE content by watching their LIVE videos, subscribing to their LIVE communities, participating in the chat, reacting to their LIVE with virtual Gifts, and more.

Note: LIVE and Gift features aren't currently available everywhere.
**Who can go LIVE on TikTok?**

To go LIVE on TikTok, you must:
•  Be at least 18 years old to go LIVE.
•  Be at least 18 years old (or 19 in South Korea) to access Gifts or collect Diamonds during a LIVE.
•  Meet a local threshold of minimum followers.

Remember to follow our [Community Guidelines]( [LIVE Monetization Guidelines]( [Terms of Service]( and [Creator Code of Conduct]( to promote a fun, positive, and safe environment on TikTok.
**How to watch a LIVE on TikTok**

To watch a LIVE, open the TikTok app, then tap **LIVE** at the top to view videos from creators who are currently LIVE. You can also watch LIVE videos that appear on your For You and Following feeds, inbox, search results, and other creator's profiles.
**How to go LIVE on TikTok**

1\. In the TikTok app, tap the **Add post +** button at the bottom.
2\. Tap **LIVE** at the bottom. You may need to scroll across.
3\. Add a title, use LIVE tools, or adjust your LIVE settings.
4\. Tap **Go LIVE** to start your LIVE.

Keep in mind that you can also adjust LIVE tools and settings, including safety settings, during your LIVE by tapping **More** ... at the bottom, then **Settings**.

When you're ready to end your LIVE, tap the **End** **LIVE** button at the top, then **End now**.

**About comments on TikTok LIVE**

As a creator or moderator on TikTok LIVE, you can manage and interact in the chat in many ways, including:
•  Turning comments on or off.
•  Muting comments from specific viewers for a specified amount of time.
•  Reporting and filtering unkind, spammy, or [community-flagged comments](
•  Reviewing filtered or community-flagged comments to approve or permanently hide them from the chat.\*
•  Sending an automated message in the LIVE chat to let viewers know that you've chosen to filter comments. This setting is only available to creators.\*
•  Blocking comments that contain your chosen keywords or phrases.\*
•  Pinning comments to the top of the LIVE chat.\*
•  Translating comments to your app's default language.\*
_\*Only available in the TikTok app._

A few things to know:
•  Only creators who meet the [eligibility requirements]( can go LIVE on TikTok.
•  Moderators can only use comment settings if they're granted comment permissions by the creator.
•  To go LIVE on a web browser, you must download [LIVE Studio]( (Windows) or [OBS Studio]( (Linux, macOS, or Windows). LIVE moderators don't require access to LIVE Studio or OBS Studio when moderating on a web browser.
**Community-flagged comments on TikTok LIVE**

Community-flagged comments are comments that the TikTok community previously blocked, muted, or reported for being unkind, spammy, or offensive. As a creator, if you choose to filter community-flagged comments during your LIVE, these comments won't be visible to viewers and they'll appear faded to you and your moderators.
**How to manage comments on TikTok LIVE**

As a creator or moderator on TikTok LIVE, you can choose to allow, block, or filter comments directly from your LIVE settings or chat.

**TikTok app**

**From LIVE settings**
1\. Tap the **Add post +** button at the bottom.
2\. Tap **LIVE** at the bottom. You may need to scroll across to find it.
3\. Tap **Settings**.
4\. Tap **Comment settings**, then choose your preference for the following settings:
   ༚  **Allow comments:** Turn comments on or off for viewers during a LIVE. This setting is turned on by default.
   ༚  **Filter comments:** Select which comments you'd like to filter, including spam, potentially unkind, and community-flagged comments. Potentially unkind and spam comments are filtered by default, however you can turn off filtering for both. This setting is only available to creators.
   ༚ **Mute duration**: Select how long you'd like to mute comments from muted viewers. You can choose to mute their comments for the entire time you're LIVE or for a select amount of time.
༚ **Mute rules**: Automatically mute viewers who comment unwanted words, phrases, or emojis by adding the exact keyword you want to mute. Only viewers who comment the exact keyword without any variations will be muted. For example, adding "Boring" will only mute viewers who comment "Boring" and not those who comment "You're not boring" or "Boringggg." Choose to mute viewers for a select amount of time or the entire LIVE. Muted viewers won't be able to comment and their previous comments will automatically be removed from the chat. Keep in mind mute rules only apply to one LIVE and will need to be set each time.
   ༚  **Blocked keywords:** Add up to 500 keywords or phrases that you want to block and hide from your LIVE chat. Each keyword can include up to 30 characters. Comments that contain blocked keywords won't be displayed to you or your viewers during your LIVE. By default, similar versions of keywords you add will also be blocked. For example, if you block "bad" then "badness" and "baddd" will also be blocked. You can turn this setting off to block only the specific keywords you add.
   ༚  **Show the most sent comment:** When the same comment is sent within a short period of time, it will be shown outside of the comment area by default. You can turn this setting off to control how comments are displayed.

Keep in mind that **Comment settings** are available before and during a LIVE.

**From LIVE chat**
1\. During a LIVE, tap the comment you want to manage in the chat. You can also tap the viewer's profile, then tap the **More** ... button.
2\. Choose how you'd like to manage the comment:
   ༚  **Reply to TikToker**: Reply to the viewer's comment in the chat.
   ༚  **Pin this comment**: Pin a comment to the top of the chat for a brief period of time.
   ༚  **Translate**: Translate the comment to your app's default language. To change the comment back to its original language, tap the translated comment again. Translated comments are only displayed to you in the chat.
   ༚  **Mute account**: Mute the viewer's comments for a select amount of time. They'll be notified that they've been muted and can't submit comments until the mute duration is over or they're unmuted.
༚ **Block**: Block the viewer's comment. This also blocks the viewer from interacting with the creator through their posts, profile, or direct messages. They'll be notified that they've been blocked and can't submit comments or watch any of the current creator's LIVE videos unless they're unblocked.
   ༚  **Report**: Report the viewer's comment.

Moderators with [granted permissions]( can tap **Share** at the bottom of the LIVE, then **Settings** to manage comments.

**How to approve or hide comments on TikTok LIVE**

If you choose to [filter and review community-flagged comments]( during a LIVE, you and your moderators will have the ability to approve comments or keep them hidden from viewers. This feature is only available in the TikTok app.
To manage filtered comments:
1\. During a LIVE, go to the filtered comment.
2\. From here, you can:
   ༚  Tap the **Review comment** button next to the filtered comment, then tap **Yes** to approve the comment or **No** to keep the comment hidden.
   ༚  Press and hold on the filtered comment, then tap **Approve comment** to display the comment.

A few things to know:

• Moderators need to be granted permission by the creator.

**What is multi-guest on TikTok LIVE?**

The multi-guest feature allows creators to host guests during their LIVE video. Viewers can send guest requests or creators can invite a limited number of guests to connect to their LIVE in audio or video mode. During the LIVE, [other viewers can send Gifts]( to the creators and connected guests.
**How to manage your multi-guest settings on LIVE**

To manage your multi-guest permission settings:

1\. During your LIVE, tap **Guests** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Settings** button, then adjust the following permission settings for your LIVE:

༚  **Allow requests from viewers**: Allow multi-guest requests from all viewers.

༚  **Allow requests from followers only**: Only allow multi-guest requests from people who follow you.

༚  **Moderators**: Add or remove moderators. Once you add moderators, you can give them permission to use certain LIVE settings.

To manage your multi-guest layout settings:
1\. During your LIVE, tap **Guests** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Settings** button, then adjust the following layout settings for your LIVE:
   ༚  **Panel**: This layout keeps you on the main screen. We recommend using this layout for one-on-one LIVE videos or guest audio chatting.
   ༚  **Grid**: This layout gives guests more exposure on the screen. We recommend using this layout for talent performances or game play.
   ༚  **Fixed layout**: This layout will remain the same regardless of the number of participants. Otherwise, the layout will automatically adapt based on the number of viewers.
**How to manage guests during your LIVE**

To invite guests to join your LIVE:
1\. During your LIVE, tap **Guests** at the bottom.
2\. Tap **Invite**, then select other creators or friends to join as guests.

To remove a guest from your LIVE:
1\. During your LIVE, tap **Guests** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Disconnect** button next to the guest you want to remove.
**How to join a LIVE as a guest**

To request to join someone's LIVE:
1\. During someone's LIVE, tap **Multi-guest** at the bottom, then tap **Request**. Keep in mind that the multi-guest option may not be available for everyone's LIVE.
2\. Once they accept your request, adjust your LIVE video settings.
3\. Once you're ready, tap **Go LIVE**.

To accept an invite to join someone's TikTok LIVE:
1\. Tap **Accept** on the LIVE invitation.
2\. Adjust your LIVE video settings.
3\. Once you're ready, tap **Go LIVE**.

Note: Before you join their LIVE, we'll check your device's camera and microphone permissions to make sure you can connect. Your camera or microphone won't be enabled until you go to your LIVE video settings.

**What are LIVE Events on TikTok?**

LIVE Events allow creators to let their audience know when they'll be going LIVE in advance so viewers can register and receive notifications about the event.
**How to create a LIVE Event**

1\. In the TikTok app, tap the **Add post** + button at the bottom.
2\. Tap **LIVE** at the bottom. You may need to scroll across.
3\. Tap **LIVE Center**.
4\. Tap **LIVE Events**.
5\. Tap **Create an event**, then follow the steps to create an event. Keep in mind that the time you choose for an event will be shown in the viewer's local time.

You can also create a LIVE event from [TikTok Studio]( by tapping **LIVE**, then **LIVE Events**.
**How to register for a LIVE Event**

1\. In the TikTok app, go to the creator's profile.
2\. Tap **LIVE Event**. If there's more than one event, select the event you want to register for.
3\. Tap **Register**.
4\. Tap **Add to device calendar** or **Done**.

You can also add a LIVE Event to your calendar from the **Event details** screen by tapping the **Share** button at the top, then **Add to calendar** at the bottom. Keep in mind that we may ask you to grant access to your device's calendar.
**How to cancel your LIVE Event registration**

1\. In the TikTok app, go to the creator's profile.
2\. Tap **LIVE Event**. If there's more than one event, select the event you want to cancel your registration for.
3\. Tap **Registered**. You can also tap the **Share** button at the top, then tap **Unregister** at the bottom.
**How to share a LIVE Event**

1\. In the TikTok app, go to the creator's profile.
2\. Tap **LIVE Event**. If there's more than one event, select the event you want to share.
3\. Tap the **Share** button at the top, then follow the steps to share the event.
**What are TikTok LIVE replays?**

TikTok LIVE replays are saved recordings of your past LIVE videos. Replays are available for you to view and download for 30 days.
**Why does TikTok save LIVE videos?**

We save LIVE videos to:
•  Make LIVE replays available for you to download or clip.
•  Improve and develop content moderation on TikTok.
•  Prevent and respond to potentially harmful content in accordance with our [Community Guidelines.]( For more information, see our [Privacy Policy](
**How to access your LIVE replays**

1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **LIVE** at the top.
4\. Tap **LIVE recordings**.

Keep in mind that you can also access your LIVE replays from the [LIVE Center](
**How to manage your LIVE replays**

After you end your LIVE, you can download, clip, or delete your LIVE replays.

To download a LIVE replay:
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **LIVE** at the top.
4\. Tap **LIVE recordings**.
5\. Locate the LIVE replay you'd like to download, then tap **Download**.

To clip a LIVE replay:

2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **LIVE** at the top.
4\. Tap **LIVE recordings**.
5\. Locate the LIVE replay you'd like to clip, then tap **Clip**.

To delete a LIVE replay:
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **LIVE** at the top.

**What are LIVE Gifts on TikTok?**

LIVE Gifts is a feature that allows viewers to interact with creators' LIVE streams by sending virtual Gifts. After you [turn on LIVE Gifts]( and [go LIVE]( your viewers can send virtual [Gifts]( to your LIVE to engage and express themselves. LIVE Gifts is one of the ways your viewers can make your content more popular.

TikTok uses [Diamonds]( to recognize a creator's popularity and contributions to our platform. We'll give you Diamonds based on how popular your LIVE content is or if you win campaigns or missions. The number of Diamonds a creator receives is one of several factors that we take into account to determine their total LIVE rewards under our [Reward Policy]( At the end of your LIVE, you'll see the total number of Diamonds collected in your LIVE summary.

Note: Collecting Diamonds is subject to our [Virtual Items Policy](
**What are the eligibility requirements for LIVE Gifts?**

To turn on LIVE Gifts, you must meet the following requirements:
•  Live in a location where LIVE Gifts is available.
•  Be at least 18 years old (or 19 in South Korea).
•  Be eligible to go LIVE.
•  Have an account that is in good standing and adheres to our [Community Guidelines]( and [Terms of Service](

Note: Government, Politician, and Political Party Accounts, and other public interest accounts are not eligible to collect Diamonds through LIVE Gifts.
**How to manage LIVE Gifts**

To turn LIVE Gifts on or off:
1\. On the **LIVE** screen, tap **Settings**.
2\. Turn the **LIVE Gifts** setting on or off.
3\. Make any additional changes on the **LIVE** screen and tap **Go LIVE** to complete.
