**About Video Gifts on TikTok**

Video Gifts are a way your viewers can react and show their appreciation for your content. Turning on Video Gifts is one way you can collect [Diamonds]( for the popularity of your videos on TikTok.

To be eligible for Video Gifts, you must meet the following requirements:
•  You must live in a location where Video Gifts is available. This feature is not available everywhere.
•  You must be 18 years of age or older (or 19 in South Korea).
•  You need at least 10,000 followers and your account must be at least 30 days old.
•  You must have published a public video in the last 30 days.
•  Your account must be in good standing and adhere to our [Community Guidelines]( and [Terms of Service](
•  You must have a Personal Account. Business Accounts, Government, Politician, and Political Party Accounts and any other public interest account are ineligible to receive Gifts on their content.

Videos are ineligible if they include:
•  Duets and Stitches.
•  Ads, sponsored content, or promoted posts.
•  Content that violates our [Community Guidelines](

Note: Receiving Gifts and collecting Diamonds is subject to our [virtual items policies](
**How to turn on Video Gifts**

To turn on Video Gifts:
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **Video Gifts** within **Monetization**.
4\. Turn the **Gifts** setting on, review the eligibility requirements, and tap **Turn on Gifts**.
**How to manage Video Gifts**

To turn Video Gifts on or off in TikTok:
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **TikTok Studio**.
3\. Tap **Video Gifts** within **Monetization**.
4\. Turn the **Gifts** setting on or off.

**What are Diamonds on TikTok?**

Diamonds are a type of virtual item that TikTok uses to recognize creator popularity, their ranking, and their contributions to the platform. Creators can collect Diamonds from Gifts sent in their LIVE streams, or by contributing to the platform through missions and campaigns. Once you [collect Diamonds]( you may be eligible to receive rewards, such as digital goods or services.

Note: Collecting Diamonds is subject to our [Virtual Items Policy](
**How to collect Diamonds**

To be eligible to collect Diamonds, you must meet the following requirements:
•  Live in a country where Diamonds are available.
•  Be at least 18 years old (or adhere to the local age requirement).
•  Be eligible to use certain features that allow you to collect Diamonds.
•  Have an account that is in good standing and adheres to our [Community Guidelines]( and [Terms of Service](
**How to view your Diamonds balance**

To view your Diamonds balance:
1\. In the TikTok app, tap **Profile** at the bottom.
2\. Tap the **Menu** ☰ button at the top, then select **Balance**.
3\. Tap **LIVE rewards**.

For more information about your balance, tap **Help & feedback**.
