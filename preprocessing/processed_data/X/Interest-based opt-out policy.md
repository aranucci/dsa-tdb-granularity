**Who served this ad?**

This ad was served by X. X allows advertisers to run advertising campaigns in publishers’ mobile applications and websites to reach audiences that may be interested in their products or services.
**Why am I seeing this ad?**

To create a more customized online experience, X may collect information about your device and the ads you view or click. When you download, visit, or interact with X, or the app or website of a X advertiser, X may collect information in connection with that activity. X may also receive information from a publisher’s app or website where you receive a X ad.

Please read [X’s Privacy Policy]( to know more about the data X collects and how it is used.
**What choices do I have?**

X respects your privacy as a customer, and you can opt-out from being included in X's interest-based advertising services on supported devices: Your iOS mobile device may provide a “Limit Ad Tracking” setting. On Android devices, your settings may allow you to “Opt out of Interest-Based Ads”. When you have opted out using this setting on a device, X will not use in-app information collected from that device to infer your interests or serve ads to that device that are targeted based on your inferred interests.

Your mobile web browser may also provide a “Do Not Track” browser setting. When you have enabled this setting in your browser, X will not use mobile web browsing information from that browser to infer your interests or serve ads to that device that are targeting based on your inferred interests.

Please note that while opting out limits X's collection of information to serve you ads based on your inferred interests, it does not prevent you from receiving ads from X, it affiliates, or third parties.
