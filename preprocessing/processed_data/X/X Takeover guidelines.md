How does this policy vary from country to country?
**Trend Takeover +**

**Argentina**

Gambling advertisers are permitted to use this product, so long as they comply with the [Argentina Gambling Policy](

**Australia**

Alcohol advertisers targeting Australia are permitted to use Trend Takeover+, subject to restrictions, and are only allowed with prior authorization from X.

**Brazil**

Brazilian alcohol advertisers are permitted to use this product, so long as alcoholic beverages have a strength less than 13%.

**Japan**

Gambling advertisers targeting Japan are permitted to use this product, subject to restrictions.

Prior authorization from X is required.

**Mexico**

Gambling advertisers are permitted to use this product, so long as they comply with the [Mexico Gambling Policy](

Alcohol advertisers targeting Mexico are permitted to use this product so long as they agree to our terms and conditions.

**UK**

Alcohol advertisers targeting the UK are permitted to use this product, subject to restrictions.

Prior authorization from X is required.

**U.S.**

Daily Fantasy Sports ads are permitted with the following restrictions;

Disclaimers about age and state eligibility requirements are required

Information on gambling addiction hotline is required

No minors should be depicted

No unfair or misleading representations of winning

Sports Betting ads are permitted with the following restrictions;

No ability to place a bet in ad or on the landing page

No minors should be depicted

No unfair or misleading representations of winning

Disclaimers stating that sports betting is not legal in states where prohibited must be present
