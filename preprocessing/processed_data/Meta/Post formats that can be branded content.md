Post formats that can be branded content

You can use the branded content tag with the following post types and formats:

Photos

Videos

Links

Text

Live broadcasts

Carousels

Stories

Branded content can also be tagged with messages sent in your Facebook and Messenger channels.

Depending on your content type, you may need to follow policies other than the branded content policies.
