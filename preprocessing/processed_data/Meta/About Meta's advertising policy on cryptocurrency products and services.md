About Meta's advertising policy on Cryptocurrency Products and Services

If you plan to run ads that promote the trading of cryptocurrency or related products and services, you’ll need to meet our eligibility requirements. These requirements help us identify advertisers who are legitimate and use safe cryptocurrency advertising practices.
Confirm your eligibility to run cryptocurrency ads

To confirm your eligibility to advertise cryptocurrency products and services:

Check to see if you meet the eligibility requirements listed [here]( These include licenses from countries with developed cryptocurrency regulatory frameworks.

If you believe you meet the requirements, submit an application through the [Authorizations and Verifications]( tab in Meta Business Suite. We’ll then review your application and assess your eligibility.

Not all applications are eligible to advertise cryptocurrency products and services. To determine eligibility, we assess information from the application and a variety of sources. Eligibility signals may change over time. As they do, we'll update our policy.

Below are commonly asked questions related to eligibility:

![](
Do I need prior permission to run ads for exchange traded funds (ETFs) consisting of shares in companies that are involved in cryptocurrency-related activities?

Advertisers who run ads to promote ETFs that consist of shares of cryptocurrency companies don’t need to apply and confirm eligibility at this time.

However, advertisers who promote ETFs that consist of cryptocurrency or track cryptocurrency prices need to apply and confirm eligibility.

![](
Do I need prior permission to run ads for NFTs (non-fungible tokens)?

Advertisers who run ads to promote non-fungible tokens (NFT) don’t need to apply and confirm eligibility at this time.
Application process

Proceed to complete the authorization process within the [Authorizations and Verifications]( tab in Meta Business Suite.

Next to **Cryptocurrency ads**, click **View details**.

Click **Request authorization** to start.

Review the steps in the pop up and click **Get started**.

Choose the eligible country where you will run your ads from the dropdown list, and click **Next**.

Provide your business details and and select the ad account(s) that you want to be authorized to advertise. You can select multiple ad accounts. Once complete, click **Next**.

Upload the relevant cryptocurrency license or regulatory certification, and click **Next**.

**Note:** If the license or certification is not in English, you will need to upload an official notarized English translation of your cryptocurrency license(s) or regulatory certification(s) along with the original document(s).

Acknowledge you have read the Meta Cryptocurrency Ads Addendum and click **Next**.

Review your information for accuracy and click **Submit**.

After you submit, your application will be reviewed by Meta. You will be notified of a decision usually within 48 business hours, although in some cases review times may be longer. You can track your application status in the [Authorizations and Verifications]( tab in Meta Business Suite.

To maintain your authorization to run cryptocurrency ads, you must maintain an active license or certification with the relevant regulatory body. If Meta determines that you no longer have an active license, your authorization status may be revoked.
Sharing authorization across ad accounts

Authorized advertisers can request to extend their authorization to run cryptocurrency ads to other ad accounts within their same [business portfolio]( You must have at least one approved submission to be able to share your authorization.

Requests must be submitted via the [Authorizations and Verifications]( tab in Meta Business Suite.

To share your authorization to run cryptocurrency ads:

Next to **Cryptocurrency ads**, click **View details**.

Click **Request authorization**.

Review the steps in the pop up and click **Get started**.

For **Type of request**, select **I want to apply existing authorizations to an additional ad account**, and click **Next**.

Select the existing authorization and the ad account(s) you want to apply the authorization to, and click **Next**.

Review your information for accuracy and click **Next**.

You should see a **Verification successful** status right away for the new ad account(s) authorized.
How we review cryptocurrency ads

The review process for ads related to cryptocurrency involves 2 parts:

**Ad review:** Our ad review system identifies whether the ads are within the scope of the cryptocurrency ads policy.

**Ad account review:** When we identify that an ad is within scope, we check to see if the associated ad account has been approved to advertise cryptocurrency products and services.

Your ad violates our policy if it’s within the scope of the policy and your associated ad account hasn’t been approved for cryptocurrency. Note that your ads must also not promote products prohibited in our [Fraud, Scams and Deceptive Practices]( or [Financial and Insurance Products and Services]( policies.
